Apex is an evidence-based stock-grading engine — a research tool, not financial advice and no guarantee of returns. It ranks the S&P 500 + mid-caps on documented return factors (momentum, trend, relative strength, low-volatility, structure), sector-neutralised, to surface the strongest long setups and flag the weakest. Factor edges are real but modest and can fade; it ranks probabilities, it doesn't predict the future. Built & run in Singapore · part of Adam Phang's work.
