A systematic engine that trades the strong, sizes by risk, and steps aside in storms.

Helm blends three evidence-based sleeves — trend and mean-reversion on a liquid basket of US equities/ETFs, plus a multi-asset trend sleeve that goes long or short bonds, gold, commodities and the dollar (managed-futures style, ~uncorrelated to stocks). Sized inverse to volatility and scaled to a vol target. Every component is validated out-of-sample and only kept if it adds value. Tracked as a $100k paper book until it earns a live deployment.
