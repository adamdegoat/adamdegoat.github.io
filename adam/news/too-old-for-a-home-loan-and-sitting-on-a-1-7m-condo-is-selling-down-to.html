Too Old for a Home Loan and Sitting on a $1.7M Condo: Is Selling Down to a Resale HDB Worth It?年过60、手握$1.7M condo却借不到贷款：卖掉换转售HDB值不值？

$1.7M condo value before right-sizing换小房前的condo估值

If you are in your 60s with a fully paid condo worth around $1.7 million, you are sitting on substantial wealth, but getting a bank loan to move elsewhere is no longer straightforward at this age. Banks calculate the maximum loan tenure against your age, and the closer you are to 65 or 70, the shorter that window, which sharply limits how much you can borrow.

Selling down to a resale HDB flat is one option that can unlock a meaningful amount of cash. After accounting for CPF refunds with accrued interest, agent fees and stamp duties, the net proceeds can still leave a retiree with several hundred thousand dollars in liquid savings, depending on which flat they buy. The lifestyle adjustment is real though: smaller living space, no private condo facilities, and a different community profile.

For homeowners who are asset-rich but cash-light, this kind of move can genuinely improve day-to-day financial comfort in retirement. The numbers need to be worked out carefully for your specific situation, but for many people in this position, selling the condo and right-sizing into an HDB flat is a financially sound path worth taking seriously.

卖掉私宅换购转售HDB组屋，是一个能够套现相当金额的选择。扣除须归还的CPF连同应计利息、经纪费和印花税后，净所得仍可能让退休人士手握几十万现金，具体数字取决于买入哪一套组屋。当然，生活方式的调整是真实存在的：居住空间缩小，没有私人condo的设施，社区环境也不一样。

对于资产丰厚但手头现金不宽裕的屋主来说，这种搬迁方式确实能切实改善退休后的日常财务状况。当然，需要根据你个人的具体情况仔细计算，但对很多处于这个处境的人来说，卖掉condo、换入HDB组屋，是一条在财务上相当稳健、值得认真考虑的路。
