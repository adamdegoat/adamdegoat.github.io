Lentor Gardens Residences Moves 270 Units at Average $2,350 Per Square FootLentor Gardens Residences开盘周末售出270个单位，平均每平方英尺$2,350

$2,350 psf average price at Lentor GardensLentor Gardens的平均价格

Lentor Gardens Residences sold 270 units over its launch weekend at an average price of $2,350 per square foot. That level of take-up in a single weekend shows that buyer appetite in the Lentor Hills corridor has not faded, even as sentiment in other parts of the market has softened.

The Lentor area has now seen several launches, each priced progressively higher than the last, and buyers have kept showing up. The Lentor MRT station, the greenery-focused planning of the area and the appeal of a freshly built township environment have made this stretch attractive to families and upgraders willing to pay for a newer product in a well-planned setting.

If you were holding out for Lentor prices to ease as more supply comes in, the sales figures suggest patience alone is unlikely to get you a cheaper entry point. At $2,350 psf average, this is premium pricing for a location outside the central region. Anyone seriously considering Lentor should look honestly at whether the lifestyle and locational factors justify the cost, rather than expecting the market to move down to meet them.

Lentor一带已陆续推出多个项目，每次推出的价格都比上一次更高，但买家依然接连入场。Lentor MRT站、区域以绿化为主的规划理念，以及全新市镇环境的吸引力，让这片区域对愿意为优质新产品付出溢价的家庭和提升型买家颇具吸引力。
