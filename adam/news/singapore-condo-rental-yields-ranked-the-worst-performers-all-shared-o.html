Singapore Condo Rental Yields Ranked: The Worst Performers All Shared One Trait新加坡condo租金回报率排名：表现最差的项目都有一个共同点

When rental yields across Singapore condo projects are ranked, the pattern at the bottom is consistent. The weakest performers are typically projects dominated by large units, three-bedders and four-bedders, located outside the core city areas. The pool of tenants willing to pay high rents for spacious apartments far from town is simply too thin to keep occupancy and income stable.

The problem is a mismatch between what was built and what the rental market actually wants. Developers sometimes size units upward to push higher absolute selling prices, but tenants, especially corporate ones, tend to prioritise MRT access and commute time over floor area. A well-located two-bedder will almost always rent faster and at a better yield than a spacious four-bedder in a suburban project with poor connectivity.

If rental yield is part of your investment case, the practical screen is straightforward: look at the unit mix and the location type before anything else. Projects with a high share of large units in primarily owner-occupied suburbs are the ones most likely to disappoint on income.

将新加坡各condo项目的租金回报率排名后，垫底的规律相当一致。表现最弱的项目，通常是以大单位为主的发展，三房式和四房式，位于核心城区以外的地段。愿意为远离市区的宽敞公寓支付高租金的租户群体，实在太少，难以维持稳定的入住率和租金收入。

问题的根源是供需错配。发展商有时会扩大单位面积来推高绝对售价，但租户，尤其是企业租户，往往更重视靠近MRT和通勤便利，而非楼面面积。一个位置优越的两房式单位，几乎总是比郊区交通不便项目里的宽敞四房式租得更快、回报率更高。

如果租金回报是你投资考量的一部分，筛选标准其实很直接：先看单位组合和地点类型。大单位比例高、位于以自住为主的郊区的项目，最有可能在租金收益方面让你失望。
