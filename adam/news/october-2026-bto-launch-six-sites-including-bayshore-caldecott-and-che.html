October 2026 BTO Launch: Six Sites Including Bayshore, Caldecott and Chencharu Reviewed2026年10月BTO推出：Bayshore、Caldecott和Chencharu等六个地点全解析

The October 2026 BTO exercise covers six sites spread across very different parts of Singapore. You are looking at Bayshore and Caldecott on the city fringe, Mattar near Bidadari, Tengah in the west, Chencharu up north, and Sembawang North near the coast. Each comes with its own set of trade-offs on transport, surrounding amenities and how built-up the neighbourhood already is.

Bayshore sits close to East Coast Park and the upcoming Bayshore MRT station on the Thomson-East Coast Line, which makes it one of the more sought-after sites but prices will reflect that. Caldecott offers a quieter, greener setting with existing MRT access. Mattar benefits from a mature estate around it. Tengah and Chencharu are still being developed from scratch, while Sembawang North suits buyers who are comfortable being further from the city.

With six sites on the table, the key is to be honest about your priorities before you ballot. Location and transport access matter most for long-term resale value, while existing amenities affect your day-to-day from the moment you collect your keys. Running through each site against your own checklist will serve you better than simply going for the lowest price tier.

2026年10月的BTO申购活动共涵盖六个地点，分布在新加坡各不相同的区域。包括靠近市区边缘的Bayshore和Caldecott、位于Bidadari附近的Mattar、西部的Tengah、北部的Chencharu，以及靠近海岸的Sembawang North。每个地点在交通、周边配套和周围成熟程度方面各有利弊。

Bayshore靠近东海岸公园，以及即将开通的汤申-东海岸线（Thomson-East Coast Line）Bayshore MRT站，因此备受追捧，价格自然也会反映这一点。Caldecott环境较为清静，绿意盎然，现有MRT接驳也方便。Mattar周围是成熟组屋区，配套齐全。Tengah和Chencharu目前仍在从零开发阶段，而Sembawang North则适合不介意离市区较远的买家。

面对六个地点，最重要的是在抽签前先想清楚自己真正在乎什么。地点和交通配套对长期转售价值影响最大，而现有配套设施则从你拿到钥匙那天起就直接影响日常生活。逐一对照自己的需求清单，比单纯追求最低价格层级来得踏实。
