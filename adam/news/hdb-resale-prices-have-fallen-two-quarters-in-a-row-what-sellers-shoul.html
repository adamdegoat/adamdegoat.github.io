HDB Resale Prices Have Fallen Two Quarters in a Row. What Sellers Should KnowHDB转售价连续两季下跌，卖家该知道什么

HDB resale prices have now slipped for two consecutive quarters, marking a real shift from the sustained climb the market saw over the past few years. The drops are not dramatic, but the direction is clear enough that sellers can no longer set asking prices based on peak 2024 or early 2025 expectations.

Several things are behind the softening. A steady flow of new BTO completions has given buyers more alternatives, and higher borrowing costs have made some buyers more cautious about stretching their budgets. The cooling measures introduced over the past two years have also filtered out a portion of demand that was driven by urgency or speculation.

For anyone planning to sell their HDB flat, the practical takeaway is to anchor your asking price to recent transacted prices for your block and town rather than current listings. Buyers have more options now and are more price-sensitive, and overpricing from the start will cost you time and likely result in a lower final price anyway.

HDB转售价格已连续两个季度下滑，标志着市场走势从过去几年的持续攀升出现了真实的转变。跌幅并不剧烈，但方向已足够清晰，卖家不能再以2024年高峰期或2025年初的预期来定价了。

市场走软背后有几个原因。BTO新竣工单位持续流入，给买家带来更多选择；借贷成本走高也让部分买家对拉高预算更加谨慎。过去两年出台的降温措施，也将一部分由急迫感或投机心态驱动的需求过滤掉了。

对于任何有意出售HDB组屋的人来说，实际的做法是以你那座楼或所在市镇的近期成交价作为参考，而不是参考目前的挂牌价。现在的买家有更多选择，对价格也更敏感，从一开始就定价过高，只会浪费你的时间，最终的成交价反而可能更低。
