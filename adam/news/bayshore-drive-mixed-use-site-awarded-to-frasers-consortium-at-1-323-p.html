Bayshore Drive Mixed-Use Site Awarded to Frasers Consortium at $1,323 Per Square FootBayshore Drive综合用途地段由Frasers财团以每平方英尺$1,323夺标

$1,323 psf land price per plot ratio每容积率的土地价格

A Frasers-led consortium has won the government land sale site at Bayshore Drive with a bid of $1,323 per square foot per plot ratio. This figure sets the cost benchmark for the land alone, before a single brick is laid, any unit is marketed, or a developer margin is added. The site will eventually become a mixed residential and commercial development near the upcoming Bayshore MRT station on the Thomson-East Coast Line.

Bayshore is a new precinct still taking shape. Its appeal is the combination of proximity to East Coast Park, a fresh MRT connection and a neighbourhood that has not yet been fully built out. The mixed-use element means the final development will likely include retail and amenities at ground level alongside residential units, which tends to make a precinct more liveable over time.

For buyers tracking this area, the land price is a useful signal. With the developer paying $1,323 psf just for the plot, launch prices for residential units will almost certainly start well above $2,000 psf and could run considerably higher depending on unit mix and market conditions at the time of launch. Anyone considering Bayshore should factor in that premium now rather than being surprised when the project comes to market.

由Frasers领导的财团以每容积率平方英尺$1,323的出价，赢得了Bayshore Drive的政府售地地段。这个数字是纯粹的土地成本基准，在任何一块砖头砌上去、任何单位开始销售或加入发展商利润之前。这块地段最终将发展成为汤申-东海岸线（Thomson-East Coast Line）即将开通的Bayshore MRT站附近的住宅与商业综合发展项目。

Bayshore是一个仍在成形中的新区域，吸引力在于毗邻东海岸公园、即将拥有全新MRT连接，以及整个区域尚未完全发展成熟。综合用途的定位意味着最终建成的项目很可能在住宅单位之外，底层也会有零售商店和配套设施，这往往会让一个区域随着时间推移变得更宜居。

对于关注这一带的买家来说，地价是一个有用的参考信号。发展商单是土地就已支付$1,323 psf，住宅单位的开盘价几乎可以确定会远超$2,000 psf，而且根据单位组合和上市时的市场状况，价格可能还会高出不少。任何考虑Bayshore的人，现在就应该把这个溢价纳入考量，而不是等到项目推出时才感到意外。
