October 2026 BTO Launch: A First Look at All Six Sites Including Bayshore, Caldecott and Tengah2026年10月BTO推出：六个地点抢先看，包括Bayshore、Caldecott和Tengah

HDB will launch six BTO sites in October 2026, spread across Bayshore, Caldecott, Mattar, Tengah, Chencharu and Sembawang North. The spread covers a wide range of locations, from the established east to the newer northwest towns, giving applicants with different priorities a real choice this round.

Each site comes with its own mix of pros and cons. Bayshore sits near the upcoming Bayshore MRT station and offers waterfront access but is priced at a premium. Caldecott and Mattar are more central but likely come in smaller numbers. Tengah, Chencharu and Sembawang North offer larger projects at more affordable price points, though buyers will need to be comfortable with longer commutes and less mature surroundings.

If you are planning to apply in October, the smart move is to narrow down which site actually fits your lifestyle before applications open. Ballot odds, budget and how much you mind waiting for amenities to fill in should all shape your pick, not just the sticker price.
