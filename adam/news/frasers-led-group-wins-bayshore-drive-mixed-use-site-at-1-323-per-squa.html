Frasers-Led Group Wins Bayshore Drive Mixed-Use Site at $1,323 per Square Foot of Buildable SpaceFrasers领头财团以每容积率$1,323拿下Bayshore Drive综合用途地段

$1,323 psf land price per plot ratio每容积率地价

A consortium led by Frasers Property has won the right to develop a mixed-use site at Bayshore Drive, paying $1,323 per square foot per plot ratio. The site will carry both residential and commercial components, and its position close to the future Bayshore MRT station on the Thomson-East Coast Line makes it one of the more attractive parcels to have come to market in the east in recent years.

The land price is a useful early signal for what buyers can expect when this project eventually launches. Developers stack construction costs, financing, professional fees and a profit margin on top of land cost, which means future launch prices well above $2,000 psf are a reasonable expectation, and possibly higher depending on how the project is positioned in the market.

For buyers watching the east coast corridor, this deal confirms that developer interest in Bayshore is serious and well-funded. It is worth tracking as the project takes shape, but go in with clear eyes about the entry price this land cost will likely produce.

由Frasers Property领头的财团成功投得Bayshore Drive的综合用途地段发展权，出价为每平方英尺容积率$1,323。该地段将同时包含住宅和商业部分，位置靠近Thomson-East Coast Line上即将开通的Bayshore MRT站，是近年来东部少数极具吸引力的地块之一。

这个地价，是买家预判该项目日后推出时售价的早期参考指标。发展商在地价基础上还需叠加建筑成本、融资费用、专业费用和利润空间，因此未来推出价超过$2,000 psf是合理的预期，实际定价还可能更高，具体取决于项目的市场定位。

对于关注东海岸走廊的买家来说，这笔交易证实了发展商对Bayshore的兴趣是认真且财力雄厚的。值得持续追踪项目的后续进展，但对于这个地价将会带来的入场价，要有清醒的预期。
