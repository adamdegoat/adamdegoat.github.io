Lentor Gardens Residences Sells 270 Units at Average $2,350 per Square FootLentor Gardens Residences以平均每平方英尺$2,350售出270个单位

$2,350 psf average Lentor launch priceLentor新盘平均价格

Lentor Gardens Residences sold 270 units at launch, with buyers paying an average of $2,350 per square foot. This follows strong take-up at earlier projects in the Lentor Hills cluster and confirms that new launches in this part of the north are holding buyer interest at prices well above $2,000 psf.

The Lentor area has a clear anchor in the Lentor MRT station on the Thomson-East Coast Line, giving residents a direct rail link into the city centre. As successive projects have launched and sold, supporting amenities including schools, food and beverage options and neighbourhood retail have started to fill in, which has given buyers more confidence in the area as a long-term bet.

For anyone comparing a new launch at Lentor against resale options nearby, $2,350 psf is now your working reference. Resale units priced significantly above this will face stiff competition, while future new launches in the same corridor will need a clear reason to justify any premium over what Lentor Gardens has just established.
