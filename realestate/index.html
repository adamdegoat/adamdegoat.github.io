01CaveatLive now · installable app
adamdegoat.github.io/caveat
Valuation & market intelligence

Everything an agent needs, in one app

A five-tool co-pilot built on 58,000 official sale transactions, 106,000 rental contracts and OneMap — every number is real and the working is shown, so you can defend it to a client.

  • 📊Valuation — comparable price band, an interactive map, nearby amenities, rental yield & a written summary (HDB & condo; landed + new-launch reference too).
  • 📈Market terminal — live median prices, PSF momentum, the rental market & gross yields, by town and region.
  • 🧮Affordability & stamp duty — ABSD, BSD, loan headroom, with every rate verified and the working shown.
  • 📍Prospecting — what just sold in your farm area, with a warm outreach message drafted for each.

02ThesisLive now · research engine
adamdegoat.github.io/thesis
Investment-grade research

Which projects are a smart buy — and why

Every Singapore condo project scored A–F on official caveats — and its value signal is backtested against what prices actually did. Caveat tells you what a unit's worth; Thesis tells you whether it's a smart buy.

  • 🎯Value vs nearby — whether a project's price per sqft sits below the comparable condos right around it, with the reference price shown so you can defend it.
  • ◆Launches — selling now & coming next — every current launch with the resale comps behind its premium, plus an Upcoming watchlist of not-yet-launched projects, each benchmarked against real resale.
  • 🔬Field research — a weekly agent adds plain-English notes (MRT, supply, developer, en-bloc) with sources on the top projects — clearly marked to verify.
  • 🏆Backtested & honest — the value signal behind the grade is tested against what prices actually did (a short, one-period window, stated plainly), and re-validated every week.

03StewardLive now · deal pipeline
adamdegoat.github.io/steward
Transactions & deadlines

Win the deal — then never miss a step

Caveat and Thesis help you win the client; Steward gets the deal to completion. It tracks every live transaction — HDB, private & rental — and computes each statutory deadline from your dates, so an OTP expiry or stamp-duty cut-off never slips through the cracks.

  • ⏱️SG deadline engine — enter one date (OTP granted, exercised, TA signed) and every downstream deadline — option expiry, exercise, stamp duty, completion — appears with a live countdown.
  • 📋This-week dashboard — every action across all your concurrent deals, sorted by urgency, the most pressing first — so juggling two or three clients never means a dropped follow-up.
  • ✅Stage-gated checklists — the right steps for each transaction type, from OTP to keys, so nothing gets skipped.
  • 🛡️Compliance & commission — CDD/AML reminders, 5-year record-keeping prompts, and expected-vs-received commission tracked per deal.

04AbacusLive now · calculator suite
adamdegoat.github.io/abacus
Every number, done right

The calculators clients actually ask for

Fourteen free Singapore property calculators, grouped for buyers, sellers and investors — every figure on the real MAS & IRAS rules, with the working shown. Hand a client a number they can trust, on the spot.

  • 🏠Buyers — HDB/EC grants, stamp duty (BSD + ABSD), affordability (with joint-borrower IWAA), progressive payment, monthly repayment, buy-vs-rent & rental stamp duty.
  • 💰Sellers — the cash you actually pocket after the CPF refund + 2.5% accrued interest, Seller's Stamp Duty, and a 5-year MOP checker.
  • 📈Investors — decoupling vs ABSD, rental yield, property tax, and refinancing break-even.
  • ✓Verified — every formula tested against official IRAS worked examples; rates current to the 2025 SSD & property-tax changes.

05CompassLive now · installable app
adamdegoat.github.io/compass
The whole buyer journey

A buyer's entire purchase, on one page

Caveat, Thesis & Steward win and run the deal; Compass turns a nervous first-timer into a confident client. A few plain-English questions become a personalised roadmap they can print and share — and your name rides along on every copy.

  • ✅Eligibility, up front — whether they can even buy that HDB / EC / condo / landed, with the blocker spelled out before anyone wastes time.
  • 💰Budget & full upfront cash — what they can afford under TDSR/MSR & LTV, then every dollar of downpayment, BSD, ABSD and legal needed.
  • 🗓️Timeline & checklist — OTP to keys for their exact path, plus an action checklist (IPA/HFE, lawyer, insurance) so nothing slips.
  • ✓Teaching, not a black box — plain-English "what this means" notes on every step; rates shared with Abacus and watched for changes.
