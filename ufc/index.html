Install UFC · Fight Week

Add it to your home screen — opens full-screen like a real app, with the live countdown.

📱 iPhone / iPad (Safari)
  1. Tap the Share button ⎙ at the bottom of Safari.
  2. Scroll down and tap “Add to Home Screen”.
  3. Tap Add (top-right). Done — find the UFC icon on your home screen.

Must be open in Safari (not Chrome) on iPhone.

🤖 Android (Chrome)
  1. Tap the ⋮ menu (top-right).
  2. Tap “Install app” or “Add to Home screen”.
  3. Confirm Install.
💻 Desktop (Chrome / Edge)
  1. Click the install icon ⊕ in the address bar (right side), or the ⋮ menu.
  2. Choose “Install UFC · Fight Week”.
