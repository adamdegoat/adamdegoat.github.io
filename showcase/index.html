Project 01 / 03

Latest Project Launch

A complete agent website — rebuilt mobile-first, and built to qualify leads on its own.

The problem
Most agent sites are dated and desktop-only. A buyer lands, sees a wall of listings, and leaves — and the agent never even knows they were there.
What it does
367 fast, mobile-first pages covering every launch and guide. Its centrepiece is a first-of-its-kind "What Can I Afford?" engine: a buyer enters their finances and instantly sees their real budget, every stamp duty, and the homes that actually fit.
The payoff
The site has the buyer's first conversation for them — and hands the agent a warm, already-qualified lead instead of a cold click.

Project 02 / 03

Caveat

An instant valuation & prospecting co-pilot — the answers an agent needs, in seconds.

The problem
Pricing a flat or condo, checking a client's stamp duty and ABSD, and finding prospects means juggling URA, HDB and spreadsheets — usually after the client has already asked.
What it does
One search returns a data-driven valuation for HDB and condos, live ABSD & stamp-duty figures, market context, and prospecting leads — all from free official data, no costly subscriptions.
The payoff
An agent answers "what's it worth?" and "what will I pay?" on the spot, in front of the client — credible and fast.

Project 03 / 03

Thesis

A research engine that grades every Singapore condo project on real transaction data.

The problem
"Is this project actually good value?" is almost always a gut call — or a developer's sales pitch.
What it does
Thesis scores every project A–F on actual caveats: relative value, price timing (mean-reversion), yield, liquidity, tenure and upcoming supply — and flags whether a new launch is overpriced versus nearby resale. The method is backtested.
The payoff
The agent gives clients an evidence-backed view no portal offers — the confidence to buy the quiet laggard, not the hyped launch.
