# Reception IG - Operating System

The system that runs @reception.sg on Instagram. Built from three deep research passes (SG Meta-ads reach, best-in-class B2B profile/content, and the honest funnel/metrics/timeline). This is the strategy layer; craft rules live in `brand/tokens.json`, content formats in `PLAYBOOK.md`, ready-to-post assets in `POST-KIT.md`.

## 0. The one truth to internalise

**Instagram is not the closer. It is a credibility-and-reach machine whose only job is to manufacture qualified WhatsApp/DM conversations for the founder.** Adam closes, in person or in chat. Every post, ad, and metric is judged on whether it feeds a real conversation - not on likes, reach, or followers.

**Who IG actually works for (the ICP):** owner-operator small businesses where one person is both the boss and the receptionist, feels the missed-message pain daily, and personally scrolls Instagram - salons, nails, aesthetics, dental/clinics, F&B, aircon, home services, tuition, reno, pet care. Bigger or committee-run businesses are a founder-outreach + referral play, NOT an IG-ads play. Do not spend ad budget chasing them.

## 1. The flywheel (the five pieces must feed each other, never run in isolation)

1. **Organic Reels + carousels** build credibility AND (critically) build free retargeting audiences - everyone who watches becomes re-reachable.
2. **Paid click-to-WhatsApp ads** buy the reach you can't get organically yet and drop owners straight into a chat.
3. **Retargeting** re-reaches the warm majority from 1 and 2 who watched but didn't message (3-5x cheaper than cold). Most-skipped, highest-value piece.
4. **Founder DM outreach** (10 personalised DMs/day) works the same warm pool with a human touch.
5. **Every closed client** mints a testimonial/result clip -> feeds organic -> feeds retargeting proof -> and a referral -> which Adam closes -> which mints another proof. One clip serves all five channels.

## 2. Profile kit (fix this first - a prospect judges you in ~50ms on the pfp + first bio line + top grid row)

- **Name field (the searchable 30-char slot, not the @handle):** `Reception - AI Front Desk` or `Reception - 24/7 Answering`. Puts the search keyword (front desk / answering / receptionist) where IG search indexes it.
- **Bio (outcome-first, one CTA). Recommended:**
  > Your business never misses another customer.
  > A 24/7 front desk that answers every message in seconds and books the job, even after hours.
  > For SG salons, clinics, F&B, trades and more.
  > Try it free ↓
- **Link:** one on-brand mini-page on reception.sg (NOT a bare Linktree - a generic link page signals "still figuring it out"). Max 3 routes: **Try it free (primary) · See it work (demo) · Message us (WhatsApp).**
- **Profile picture:** the bell glyph only (not the full lockup), must be legible at 40px. Same mark on IG, site, and WhatsApp.
- **Story Highlights (left to right = the buyer journey):** Start Here (what it is) -> See It Work (a real demo) -> Results (proof) -> Pricing -> FAQ (kills objections so they're never a DM) -> Try Free. One cover system: cream/terracotta block + single SVG icon + one word. No emoji.
- **Pinned posts (top 3 grid slots):** (1) the product-demo reel, (2) a proof post (real SG result, once we have one), (3) a how-it-works -> "try free" carousel.

## 3. Content system

- **Mix (rolling 10 posts):** 4 problem-aware/POV education · 3 how-it-works/product demo · 2 proof · 1 direct offer.
- **Cadence:** 3-5 posts/week, consistent. Quality + a coherent grid beats volume. Only proof and offer posts carry an explicit CTA; education stays premium and restrained.
- **Anchor formats:** the product-demo reel (the chat pieces we built) and the bold editorial carousel. Both are faceless - fits the brand.
- **Hook rule:** first 3 seconds name the pain or a counterintuitive line, never a topic intro. On-screen text readable sound-off.
- **Grid stays premium, not templated:** alternating rhythm (demo -> statement -> proof), real product UI over AI-gradient cards, palette + type hold cohesion. (Full pillars + hook bank: `PLAYBOOK.md`.)
- **Open question for Adam:** research says a founder's *face* converts hardest for trust. Our brand is deliberately faceless/designed. Option to add occasional founder-on-camera reels (higher trust) or stay fully designed. His call.

## 4. Paid ads (this is the actual reach lever - organic alone will not find your clients)

- **Build in Ads Manager, never "Boost."** Boost gives no objective control, no custom audiences, no real tracking.
- **Objective:** Engagement -> "Get more messages" (WhatsApp) to start; upgrade to the Leads/WhatsApp objective once conversion tracking is live.
- **Targeting (2026 reality - granular interest-stacking is dead, Meta went broad+AI):** Location = Singapore, age 25-55. Add 2-4 interest *hints* only (Small business owners, Entrepreneurship, Meta Business Suite / WhatsApp Business, Digital marketing). **The real targeting is the creative naming the trade** on screen ("Salon owners in Singapore..." / "Aircon bosses..."). This is why the per-trade demo reels are gold as ads.
- **Creative:** the trade reels (Bloom salon, Frostpoint aircon, + dental/tuition/F&B to come). Native/founder-style beats polished. 9:16, 15-25s, captioned, pain-hook in 3s.
- **Destination = click-to-WhatsApp** (WhatsApp is SG's default; converts ~2-3x better than landing pages, and the 72-hour free-reply window gives Adam 3 days to qualify and close for free). **Honest note:** wiring Reception to auto-answer WhatsApp needs the WhatsApp API (product currently avoids it, web-first). Interim funnel: ad -> WhatsApp -> Adam greets fast + drops the live web front-desk link (reception.sg demo) so they talk to Reception -> qualify -> free build. North-star later: Reception itself answers the WhatsApp, so the ad's reply IS the demo.
- **Budget:** S$15-20/day (NOT S$5 - under ~S$30/day starves Meta's learning). Leave untouched 10-14 days. Scale winners +20% every 3-4 days; add creative angles rather than only raising spend.
- **Build custom audiences from day one:** video viewers (75%), page/IG engagers -> then a 1-3% SG lookalike off demo-bookers once ~100 exist.

## 5. Retargeting (the highest-ROI piece almost every founder skips)

- Only ~2-4% convert on first contact; warm retargeting converts 3-5x better. For a trust-heavy "let AI answer my customers" purchase, this is where deals close.
- **Audiences:** 75% video viewers (14-30d, hot), engagers (180d), profile visitors, DM openers.
- **Sequence by intent (don't show everyone the same ad):** hottest -> direct offer (free build); next -> social proof; next -> objection-handling ("does it sound human / is it accurate / what's the cost").
- **Frequency cap 5-7 per person / 7 days. Attribution window 45-90 days** (B2B cycles are long).

## 6. Metrics dashboard (track weekly - Monday, prior 7 days). North stars in bold.

| Metric | Tells you |
|---|---|
| Reach (organic + paid) | Are you in front of anyone |
| Reel views / avg watch % | Is the hook landing |
| Profile visits | Did reach convert to interest |
| Profile -> action rate | Bio/CTA quality (10-20% good) |
| **New WhatsApp/DM conversations** | **Real top-of-pipeline** |
| Qualified conversations | Right-fit owners (target 40-60%) |
| **Demos / free builds booked** | **The real "lead"** |
| Cost per qualified lead (paid) | Ad efficiency (SG B2B realistically S$40-120) |
| Trial -> paid close rate | Offer + product truth (beat the 6-14% B2B benchmark) |

**Diagnose top-down:** no reach = hook problem; reach but no profile visits = wrong audience/weak hook; visits but no chats = bio/CTA/offer; chats but no demos = qualification/offer; demos but no closes = pricing/pitch. Fix the earliest broken link first. Kill any angle that gets reach but zero conversations after 1-2 weeks; scale any that produces conversations at/under target cost.

## 7. The 30/60/90 plan

- **Days 0-30 (foundation + first conversations - do NOT wait for organic):** ship the profile kit; 3-5 reels/week; ONE small CTWA ad live; **10 personalised founder DMs/day** to hand-picked owner-operators; build custom audiences from day one. *Goal: 5-10 qualified conversations, 2-4 free builds started.* Early leads come from outreach + paid + Adam's network, NOT organic yet.
- **Days 31-60 (retargeting + proof loop):** launch the retargeting sequence (offer -> proof -> education); turn every free build into a "look what it caught" clip + testimonial (also a 1:1 send-to-prospect asset); double down on the 1-2 reel angles + 1 ad that produced chats, kill the rest; start referral asks. *Goal: first paid closes; CPQL trending to S$40-120; 10+ conversations/mo.*
- **Days 61-90 (scale + flywheel):** scale budget only on proven creative/audiences; formalise referrals (specific ask at the wow-moment + a reward, tracked separately); Adam now spends most time closing. *Goal: predictable weekly conversations from 3 sources (organic, paid+retargeting, referral).*

## 8. Honest expectations (no false confidence)

- **IG organic will not produce leads in the first few weeks.** It's "posting into a void" early (8 views, likes from friends). Organic compounds from month 2-3. Early leads come from paid + outreach + network. **Quitting during the void is the #1 killer.**
- **Budget for S$40-120 per qualified SG B2B lead**, settling after a 2-4 week learning period. S$5/day buys ~3-7 leads/mo - enough to test, not scale.
- **First closes realistically 30-60 days; a durable pipeline is a 90-day build.** The free-build offer is the unfair advantage - it removes buyer risk and should beat the 6-14% B2B close benchmark. Lean on it hard.
- **Manage to conversations and demos, not followers.** 10-15 qualified conversations/month within 60-90 days is the realistic, achievable target with the full system running.

## 9. Decisions needed from Adam before build

1. **Ad budget:** in for ~S$15-20/day? (This is the reach switch. Below ~S$15 it barely works.)
2. **WhatsApp:** what's the WhatsApp Business number, and interim - Adam answers fast + sends the web demo link, or do we invest in wiring Reception to the WhatsApp API so it auto-answers?
3. **Founder on camera:** occasional founder-face reels for trust, or stay fully faceless/designed?
4. **Bio:** approve the recommended bio (or pick another).
