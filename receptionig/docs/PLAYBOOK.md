# Reception IG - Content Playbook

The professional operating system for @reception.sg. Built from current (2025-2026) practice: Instagram's own ranking guidance plus Later/Buffer/Metricool/Hootsuite/Sprout. This is the "plan" layer that sits above single posts. Craft rules for individual assets live in `brand/tokens.json`; production notes per campaign live in `POST-KIT.md`.

## The one thing to design around

Instagram 2026 is a **search-and-send engine**, not hashtag-and-like. The ranking signals, in order: **watch time, sends-per-reach (DM shares), likes-per-reach.** Two rules follow:

1. **Design every post to be SENT.** A DM share is worth ~15 likes and is the #1 driver of reach to non-followers. Give each post a role-based reason to forward: "send this to the partner still answering messages at midnight."
2. **Discovery is SEO, not hashtags.** Instagram reads on-screen text, transcribes audio, and indexes the caption. Put the search keyword in all three, early. Hashtags are just a filing label now (keep to 5, we already do).

Good news: Reception's buyers literally search their pain and trade ("speed to lead", "after-hours enquiries", "property agent tools", "front desk"), and the content is inherently sendable (an agent forwards "the other agent just replied first" to their whole team chat). This format fits a Singapore B2B service unusually well.

## Cadence + workflow (batch, don't improvise)

- **Volume:** 3-4 posts/week to start (2 reels + 1-2 carousels). Reels for reach, carousels for saves.
- **Plan fortnightly, theme quarterly.** Never start from a blank page - pull from the hook bank below.
- **Batch:** one ~3-4 hour block = ~2 weeks of content (script all hooks, build all assets, write all captions together).
- **Pipeline per post:** hook (written first) -> 3-beat script (Hook / Value / CTA) -> storyboard frame-0 -> build -> QC checklist -> schedule -> publish with keyword caption + alt text + location tag + custom cover -> read ratios -> feed winners back into the hook bank.
- **Tools:** Metricool or Later (calendar + scheduling + analytics), ManyChat (DM funnel, below), a Notion/Doc hook bank.

## Hook bank (starter - add to it every week)

Frame-0 text hooks, sound-off readable, keyword-aware (property / insurance / financial):
1. It's 11:47pm. A buyer just messaged about your listing. Who replies? (LIVE)
2. The most expensive lead in your pipeline: the one you finally saw at 7am.
3. Your buyer messaged three agents last night. One replied. Guess who's showing the unit.
4. The other agent isn't better than you. They just replied first.
5. You lost a client last night and you'll never know it.
6. "Sorry, just seeing this now" is how agents lose deals.
7. Most enquiries now come in as a DM at 10pm. Where do yours go?
8. What your front desk should do while you sleep.
9. Reply in 5 minutes = up to 9x more likely to win the client. Most agents take hours.
10. An AI that answers your clients 24/7 without getting you in trouble with CEA. (compliance)
11. Yes, it answers buyers all night. No, it will never quote a valuation. (compliance)
12. Three leads you're quietly losing after office hours.

## Reels standard (grade every reel against this)

- First **frame** has the text hook (sound-off readable). No logo intro, no slow build.
- Hook lands in <=1.5-3s: pattern interrupt / curiosity gap / contrarian / stat.
- Length = shortest that lands. Kinetic explainers: **10-25s**.
- Cut every **1.5-3s, on the beat**, zero dead air.
- Primary **keyword** in on-screen text early (and spoken, if there's VO).
- **Audio baked** (our rule). Upgrade path: make it *original* audio (a signature bed + the bell sound) so we own a sonic identity; ride a trend-adjacent sound in-app on lighter posts for reach.
- **Last frame loops** into the first.
- **Custom cover** with keyword text (never Instagram's auto-pick).
- No external watermark (CapCut/TikTok), never recycled clips (2026 originality score).
- A specific **CTA** = keyword DM trigger.

## Carousel standard  [DECISION NEEDED - see note]

Research is emphatic: **carousels are Instagram's highest-engagement + top-save format, and the pro standard is 6-10 slides.** 2 slides forfeits the completion-rate, dwell, and save signals the format exists for. Our current house rule is MAX 2 (punchy). Recommended change: **6-8 slide narratives** as the default educational carousel, keeping 2-slide only for one-line punch posts.
- Slide 1 cover: 8-10 word hook, one idea, curiosity gap, feels incomplete.
- Slide 2: credibility + what's coming.
- Slides 3-n: one point per slide, consistent template, open loops.
- Final slide: CTA (save / DM keyword / book).
- 4:5 (1080x1350), one headline + one subhead per slide, manual alt text.

## Caption standard

- **Hook -> Value -> CTA** (or PAS). Hook in first ~80-125 chars (before "...more").
- **Keyword in the first sentence** + semantic variants (SEO).
- ~150-220 words for educational; shorter for promo.
- A **send prompt** ("send this to...") near the end.
- **Specific CTA = keyword DM trigger** ("Comment AUDIT"). Beats "link in bio" ~5x.
- **5 niche hashtags** max. Sharp SG operator voice, no AI cliche, no emoji, no em/en dashes.

## Keyword DM funnel (the biggest growth unlock)

Set up **ManyChat** (Meta-approved, official Graph API - never unofficial tools) on @reception.sg:
1. Post CTA: "Comment **AUDIT**" (or DEMO / PRICE).
2. **Instant** auto-DM (speed decides: ~21x more conversion replying in 1 min vs 30) = friendly line + the thing they asked for (a 60-sec demo clip / the after-hours-leak one-pager / a book-a-setup link).
3. Qualify in-DM with one question ("how many enquiries a week do you reckon you miss after hours?").
4. Hand to Adam / a Calendly link. Founding rate S$99/mo if naming price.
This replaces "link in bio" and is close to a cheat code for a Singapore B2B service. Needs the user to connect ManyChat to the IG account.

## Metrics - optimise ratios, not totals

Weekly, per format: **retention / 3-sec hold** (aim >60%), **sends-per-reach** (the growth signal), **saves-per-reach**, **follows per 1,000 reach**, and the business metric: **keyword-comments -> DMs -> setup calls booked.** Iterate by changing ONE variable at a time; kill hook styles that don't get sent; use **Trial Reels** to test a hook on non-followers first.
