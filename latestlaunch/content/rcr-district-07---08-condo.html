Rest of Central Region – Districts 07-08

Districts 07 and 08 fall within Singapore's Rest of Central Region (RCR), sitting outside the prime Core Central Region but remaining close to the central business zone.

These districts include the Middle Road, Golden Mile, and Little India corridors, offering buyers central-adjacent residential options with RCR pricing.
