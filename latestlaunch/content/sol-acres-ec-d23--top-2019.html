Executive Condos / Sol Acres EC (D23) - Choa Chu Kang Grove

Sol Acres EC (D23) - Choa Chu Kang Grove

Sol Acres is an anticipated Executive Condominium development by MCL Land, featuring a 99-year leasehold with 1,327 residential units ranging from 1 to 5 bedrooms. First-time applicants are eligible for up to $30,000 in CPF Housing Grants.

Location & Accessibility

The development sits at Choa Chu Kang Grove in District 23, positioned between Keat Hong and Teck Whye LRT Stations, both within a 2-minute walk. 2 LRT stations from Keat Hong LRT Station, you will reach Choa Chu Kang MRT Station & Bus Interchange and Lot 1 Shopping Mall.

Major expressways including the Kranji Expressway (KJE) and Bukit-Timah Expressway (BKE) are accessible within minutes. The future Bukit Panjang integrated MRT Station on the Downtown Line is approximately 2 LRT stops away.

Nearby Amenities

Lot One Shoppers' Mall offers six levels of retail, supermarket, F&B options, and a Shaw Cinema. Junction 10 Shopping Mall and Bukit Panjang Plaza are within 2-3 LRT stops. Keat Hong Shopping Centre is within walking distance for groceries and meals.

Educational Facilities

The area contains multiple schools: Teck Whye Primary, Concord Primary, South View Primary, Choa Chu Kang Primary, Bukit Panjang High School, and Pioneer Junior College.

Unit Types

Floor plans include: One Bedroom + Study, Two Bedroom + Study, Three Bedroom + Utility Room, Four Bedroom + Flexi Room.

Developer

MCL Land, a Jardine Matheson Group member under Hongkong Land Holdings, has built quality residential properties across Singapore and Malaysia for over 40 years.
