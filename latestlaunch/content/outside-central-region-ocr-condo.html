Outside Central Region (OCR) Condo

The Outside Central Region (OCR) covers Districts 15-19 and 22-28, encompassing the East, North East, North, and West Planning Regions of Singapore.

Overview of OCR Properties

The OCR includes densely populated neighbourhoods such as Punggol, Woodlands, and Jurong, featuring mass-market condominiums and an increasing number of executive condominiums. Due to cooling measures and TDSR restrictions, buyers have become increasingly price-sensitive, benefiting smaller units and outlying projects with more affordable quantum compared to central regions.

Government decentralisation strategy is driving development of regional centres, including the West region's Jurong Gateway as Singapore's second commercial hub and the 360-hectare Jurong Lake District featuring both commercial and residential precincts designed for young families.

District 15-16: East Coast

The East Coast area is popular with private home owners. This beautiful rustic area has some of Singapore's biggest bungalows and houses. The area is popular due to its proximity to the East Coast Park, which is Singapore's longest park with a coastline view.
