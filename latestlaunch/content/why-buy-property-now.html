Why Buy Now?

With interest rates expected to fall, construction costs rising, and three strong demand drivers underpinning Singapore's property market, the question is: would you still want to wait?

Interest Rate Projections

UOB expects home mortgage rates to be around 2% by 2026, compared to 3.5% in June 2024.

Construction and Manpower Costs Expected to Continue Rising

Construction and manpower costs are projected to continue rising, suggesting future development will become increasingly expensive.

2025 Condo Launch Pricing

Upcoming condos next year at Clementi Ave 1 and at Lorong 1 Toa Payoh are expected to launch from $2,500 psf or higher.

Three Strong Demand Drivers for Singapore's Property Market

UOB has identified three strong demand drivers for Singapore's property market: rising income levels, population growth, and economic vitality.

Singapore is experiencing record population growth, creating sustained housing demand.

Ongoing economic strength supports continued property market momentum.

It's Your Choice

With 3 strong demand drivers for Singapore's property market, higher PSF pricings for future harmonised condos, higher construction and higher manpower costs, how will the real estate market shift if interest rates fall? Would you still want to wait?
