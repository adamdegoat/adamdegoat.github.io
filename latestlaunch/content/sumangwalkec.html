Sumang Walk EC (D19) 99 YRS / TOP: 2023

Sumang Walk EC is an upcoming waterfront living executive condo in Punggol at District 19. It was the one and only Executive Condo to be launched in 2019, developed by City Developments Limited (CDL) and TID Residential.

Location & Connectivity

The development sits between Punggol Way and Sumang Walk near My Waterway @ Punggol, positioned between Sumang LRT and Nibong LRT stations. These stations interlink with the Punggol LRT network to Punggol MRT station.

Residents gain easy access to the Tampines Expressway (TPE) and Kallang Expressway (KPE). Shopping and dining options nearby include Waterway Point, Punggol Plaza, and Seletar Mall.

Project Specifications

District: D19 Hougang, Punggol, Sengkang. Developer: CDL Constellation Pte Ltd & TID Residential Pte Ltd. Expected TOP: 2022–2023. Tenure: 99 years.

Approximately 820 units comprising 3- to 5-bedroom configurations. Site Area: 27,056.4 sqm / 291,232.7 sqft. Approximately 13 blocks of 10–17 storeys with basement carpark.

Payment options: Normal Progressive Scheme (NPS) and Deferred Payment Scheme (DPS).

About the Developer — CDL

City Developments Limited operates globally across 97 locations in 26 countries and has developed over 40,000 homes. The company is one of Singapore's largest commercial landlords and manages over $3.5 billion in funds under management.
