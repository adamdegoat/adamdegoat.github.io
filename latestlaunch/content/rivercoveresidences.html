Rivercove Residences Executive Condo, District 19, Seng Kang

Rivercove Residences is an executive condo development in Seng Kang, located at Anchorvale Lane adjacent to the Punggol Reservoir. The project offers convenient access to Layar and Tongkang LRT stations within a 3-minute walk, connecting to Sengkang MRT Station.

Project Details

Developer: Hoi Hup Realty & Sunway Development. Total units: 628 (3-, 4-, and 5-bedroom configurations). Site Area: 226,117 sq ft. Gross Floor Area: 678,342 sq ft. 10 blocks, 16 storeys each. 99-year tenure. District 28.

Unit sizes: 3-bedroom, 484 units (904-1,163 sq ft); 4-bedroom, 112 units (1,184-1,281 sq ft); 5-bedroom, 32 units (1,485 sq ft).

Location & Amenities

The development overlooks Sengkang Riverside Park with scenic waterfront views. Residents access park connectors linking the Sengkang Sports & Recreation Centre and Anchorvale Community Centre. Facilities include swimming pools, badminton courts, soccer fields, hockey rinks, and water sports activities.

Easy access to dining establishments along Jalan Kayu. Seletar Mall and Fernvale shopping options serve residents.

Nearby Schools

Anchor Green Primary, Nan Chiau Primary School, Springdale Primary School, and Nan Chiau High School are within walking distance.

Connectivity

Strategic expressway links include the Central Expressway, Tampines Expressway, and Seletar Expressway for efficient regional connectivity.
