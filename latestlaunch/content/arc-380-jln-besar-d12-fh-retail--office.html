ARC 380 (Jln Besar) D12, FH (Retail / Office)

ARC 380, the former Eminent Plaza, is a freehold commercial development strategically positioned at the intersection of Jalan Besar and Lavender Street. Located minutes from Singapore's Central Business District, Marina Bay, and Orchard Road, the project offers exceptional business convenience and connectivity.

Project Information

Developer: Prominent Site Pte Ltd / Prominent Plaza Investments Pte Ltd. Address: 380 Jalan Besar, Singapore. District: 12. Tenure: Freehold. Expected TOP: December 31, 2018. Site Area: 4,558.10 sqm (49,063 sqft).

Building Specifications

The 16-storey commercial development comprises a 12-storey office tower and a 4-storey retail podium. Total units include 144 office spaces and 23 retail units (15 food & beverage and 8 shops).

Office units range from 65–72 sqm and 93 sqm. Retail spaces vary from 21–96 sqm depending on category.

Ground Level & Retail

A spacious high-volume plaza fronts the main retail entrance at Jalan Besar and Lavender Street junction, with a secondary entrance connecting to the nearby Bendemeer MRT station.

Integrated Facilities

The development incorporates dining and retail outlets designed for busy professionals, eliminating the need to leave the premises for daily needs.

Sky Terrace (Level 4)

Features BBQ facilities set within landscaped gardens, suitable for corporate bonding and celebrations. The 12-metre-high covered terrace adjoins an open roof garden.

Roof Terrace (Level 16)

Offers a lap pool, gymnasium, and function room for corporate events. Provides panoramic views of Kallang Basin and the city skyline.

Unique Selling Points

Rare freehold commercial asset with affordability. Excellent investment potential and central location. Prominent frontage with high foot and vehicular traffic. Bendemeer MRT station within walking distance. Major expressway connectivity. Eligible for foreign ownership. No ABSD or SSD applicable to commercial units. Distinctive curved tower design with heat-strengthened laminated low-E glass. Every office unit maximises panoramic views.

Connectivity

Nearby are the existing Farrer Park, Boon Keng and Lavender MRT stations, as well as the upcoming Bendemeer MRT station which is just 5 mins walk away.
