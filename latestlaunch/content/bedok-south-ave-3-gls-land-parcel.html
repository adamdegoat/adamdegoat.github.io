Bedok South Ave 3 GLS Land Parcel

This page details a Government Land Sale (GLS) residential site located at New Upper Changi Road and Bedok South Avenue 3 (Parcel B), awarded to CEL Residential Development Pte Ltd on February 23, 2016.

Award and Pricing

The land parcel was awarded for $419 million, translating to approximately $760 per square foot per plot ratio. Analysts estimate the finished project may command $1,200 to $1,300 psf average pricing.

Site Specifications

The 2.4-hectare 99-year leasehold site encompasses a maximum permissible gross floor area of 551,414 square feet, yielding approximately 720 residential units. The development includes a childcare centre facility.

Established Residential Estate

The land parcel is located within an established private residential estate and near Tanah Merah MRT Station. Nearby condominium developments include Stratford Court, East Meadows, Casa Merah, and Optima@Tanah Merah.

Transportation and Connectivity

The site benefits from proximity to major arterial roads including the East Coast Parkway (ECP) and Pan Island Expressway (PIE), with convenient access to Tanah Merah MRT Station connecting residents throughout Singapore.

Nearby Amenities

Residents enjoy shopping and dining at Changi City Point, Bedok, and Simei Town Centres. Recreation facilities include Bedok Reservoir Park and East Coast Park. Reputable schools nearby include Anglican High School and Temasek Junior College. The location offers proximity to Changi Business Park and Singapore University of Technology and Design.

Land Parcel Particulars

Location: New Upper Changi Rd/Bedok South Ave 3 (Parcel B). Site Area: 24,394.0 m². Lease: 99 years. GFA: 51,228 m². Residential Units: 720.
