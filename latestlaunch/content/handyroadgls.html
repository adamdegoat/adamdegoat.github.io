Handy Road GLS Land Parcel

Information about the Handy Road Government Land Sales (GLS) residential development in Singapore's Orchard Road district.

Details on Handy Road GLS Site

The land parcel sits near the premier shopping precinct at Orchard Road, the Arts and Cultural Hub at Bras Basah, Bugis, School of the Arts (SOTA) and Singapore Management University (SMU).

Prime Residential District with Excellent Accessibility

The future residential development along Handy Road and Mount Sophia Road will be situated in an established residential area. It is well-served by the adjacent Dhoby Ghaut MRT Station which is a major rail transit interchange between the North-South (NSL), North-East (NEL) and Circle (CCL) lines. The Bencoolen MRT Station on the Downtown Line is also nearby.

Particulars of Land Parcel

Location: Handy Road / Mount Sophia. Site Area: 4,796.2 m². Lease Period: 99 years. GFA: 11,446 m².
