Ideas for Balcony & Roof Terrace

Balconies and roof terraces are one of the dreams of Modernist architecture, serving as beautiful outdoor extensions to homes. As living spaces become more compact, these areas have transformed from purely functional spaces into valuable zones for relaxation and entertainment.

Key Uses

A balcony or roof terrace can serve as a relaxation sanctuary—a private retreat within your own residence. It also works as an entertainment venue, an excellent location for hosting guests, and a garden extension where you can incorporate outdoor landscaping and greenery.

Design Evolution

Rather than serving solely as storage or laundry areas, modern balconies and terraces now function as extensions of interior living spaces. They provide personal outdoor areas that integrate nature while maintaining privacy.
