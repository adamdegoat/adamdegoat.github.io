Executive Condos / Household Status

Household Status

First-timer Applicants

First-time buyers and their essential family members must meet specific criteria. They cannot be owners of HDB flats purchased directly from HDB, DBSS flats, or Executive Condominiums bought from developers. Additionally, they must not have sold such properties previously or received CPF Housing Grants for HDB resale flat purchases. Other housing subsidies, including benefits from the Selective En bloc Redevelopment Scheme or HUDC estate privatization, also disqualify applicants from first-timer status.

Second-timer Applicants

Second-timers are individuals or family members who have previously owned or currently own HDB flats purchased from HDB, resale flats bought under CPF Housing Grant Schemes, DBSS apartments, or Executive Condominiums obtained from developers. They may also include those who received other housing subsidies in the past.

Couples with Mixed Status

When one spouse is a first-timer and the other is a second-timer, the couple receives first-timer priority status for flat allocation. This priority includes access to a higher proportion of available flats, additional ballot chances for unsuccessful Build-To-Order attempts in non-mature estates, eligibility for the Parenthood Priority Scheme, Parenthood Provisional Housing Scheme, Tenant Priority Scheme, and the Staggered Downpayment Scheme.
