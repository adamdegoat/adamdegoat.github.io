Time Savings at Parktown Residence

How much is your time worth? Parktown Residence is a condo development designed to offer ultimate convenience as an integrated development that will stand the test of time.

Do Buyers Desire a Home That Offers Ultimate Convenience?

Research demonstrates that home buyers and investors strongly desire convenience features in residential properties. Integrated projects have become increasingly popular due to their ability to provide streamlined, all-in-one lifestyle solutions.

This shows that home buyers and investors desire convenience — highlighting the one-stop lifestyle approach of the integrated condo project.

One-Stop Lifestyle

Parktown Residence offers integrated amenities within a single development, eliminating the need for residents to travel elsewhere for daily necessities. The development includes on-site shopping facilities and comprehensive services.

Reputable Developer

The project is developed by CapitaLand, a reputable developer known for managing quality shopping malls and residential communities throughout Singapore.

Location and Nearby Amenities

The development features a shopping mall at the ground level with vehicle access integration. Nearby amenities include IKEA, Courts, Giant supermarket, and Tampines North Polyclinic, which opened on September 29, 2023, providing comprehensive and holistic primary care for the growing population in Tampines.

Educational facilities nearby include Poi Ching School, a SAP school in the area.

First Mover Advantage — Cross Island Line

The development provides a first-mover advantage with proximity to the Cross Island Line MRT station. The Cross Island Line is a fully underground MRT line that will significantly reduce travel times to Ang Mo Kio, Hougang, Punggol, King Albert Park, and Jurong Regional Centre.

Currently, travel from Tampines North to these destinations takes considerably longer without the Cross Island Line connectivity.
