Royal Square at Novena - Showflat

Royal Square at Novena is a new integrated mixed-use development strategically located next to Novena MRT station in District 11. The 33-storey building features medical suites, retail shops, F&B outlets, and a Courtyard by Marriott Hotel with direct MRT access via covered linkway.

Project Overview

Location: District 11, adjacent to Novena MRT. Lease: 99 years. Completion: March 2017. Direct connection via covered linkway to Novena MRT station.

Unit Types & Sizes

The development comprises medical suites of 420–786 square feet across 14 levels, and retail and F&B units of 226–670 square feet on 2 retail levels. Restaurant spaces of 2,583–4,790 square feet are located on the communal landscape deck at Level 6. Day surgery, imaging centre, and laboratory facilities are not for sale. 250 hotel rooms across Levels 21–33 are also not for sale.

Unique Features

The development is the 1st and only retail shops open for sale within Health City Novena. Notable advantages include proximity to prominent healthcare institutions like Tan Tock Seng Hospital and IRAS, expected pedestrian traffic of 30,000 daily by 2030, and its role as an anchor commercial property in the emerging Novena Health City hub.

Investment Highlights

No Additional Buyer Stamp Duty (ABSD). No Seller Stamp Duty (SSD). No loan restrictions for commercial properties. Limited supply of strata medical suites available for individual purchase. Growing demand from medical tourism and expanding medical services.
