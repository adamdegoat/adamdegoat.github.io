Parc Riviera (D05) - 99 yrs / TOP: 2020

Parc Riviera is a residential condo by EL Development along West Coast Vale. A luxury home next to the scenic Sungei Pandan park connector by the tranquil Sungei Ulu Pandan river, allowing residents to enjoy riverside adventures every day.

Project Details

Parc Riviera is the latest condo project on a 99 years leasehold plot of land occupying an area of 18,906.7 sqm consisting of 752 residential units with 2 Blocks of 36 Storeys with 4 levels of multi storey carpark.

Developer: EL Development. Tenure: 99 years leasehold. TOP: August 2020. Location: West Coast Vale, District 05.

The Ulu Pandan Park Connector links the Western Adventure Loop to the park connectors in the south, and Jurong.

Parc Riviera is only minutes to Clementi Mall and Jurong East.

Gather for good moments and better views

The Sky Park offers panoramic views spanning from Pandan Reservoir to Bukit Timah Natural Reserve and includes a sky jacuzzi, panoramic pavilion, and observation deck.

The 5th storey Garden Deck features green spaces, community gardens, pavilions for entertaining, a planting area, pavilions for barbequing, and a kids' fun zone.

Immerse in the delight of watery wonders

The development includes a 50-metre lap pool, aqua gym, leisure pool, splash pad, and children's pool, creating a seamless flow of water bodies.

Be spoilt by a luxurious spread of facilities

Facilities encompass a clubhouse, gymnasium, multi-game court, basketball half-court, and adventure trail.

Time for some relaxation and rejuvenation

Unit types available: 1-bedroom, 2-bedroom, and 3-bedroom.
