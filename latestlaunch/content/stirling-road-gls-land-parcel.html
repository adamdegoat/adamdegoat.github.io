Stirling Road GLS Land Parcel

The Stirling Road Government Land Sale (GLS) parcel represents a residential development opportunity in a centrally located Singapore site with excellent connectivity to major business districts and transportation networks.

Ideal Location with Excellent Connectivity

The site benefits from proximity to key areas, being only 10 minutes away from the Central Business District, Orchard Road as well as the two Integrated Resorts. The parcel connects to major arterial roads including Alexandra Road, Queensway, West Coast Highway, and Ayer Rajah Expressway. Residents can walk to Queenstown MRT Station in under 5 minutes.

Shopping and Dining

The location offers access to nearby shopping centers including Anchor Point, Queensway Shopping Centre, and Tiong Bahru Plaza (two MRT stations away). Additional dining options exist at lifestyle clusters like Rochester Park and Dempsey Hill.

Schools

The site suits families, with nearby schools including Queenstown Primary and Secondary School, Gan Eng Seng Primary School, and Crescent Girls' School. Tertiary institutions such as the National University of Singapore and Singapore Polytechnic are also accessible.

Recreational Facilities

The Southern Ridges nearby offers flora, fauna, and panoramic views of the city, harbor, and Southern Islands.

Stirling Road GLS Site Details

Location: Stirling Road. Site Area: 21,109.5 m². Lease Period: 99 years. Allowable Development: Residential. Permissible GFA: 88,660 m². Maximum Building Height: Part 4-storey and part 163 m AMSL.
