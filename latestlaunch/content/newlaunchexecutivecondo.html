Executive Condos / Executive Condo (EC)

Executive Condo (EC)

Executive Condos represent a hybrid housing model combining public and private sector features. Introduced in 1996 during peak private property prices, they address the needs of buyers who cannot afford private properties but exceed public housing income thresholds.

About Executive Condos

The income ceiling has been raised from $8,000 to $14,000, broadening eligibility. With private properties exceeding $1,200 per square foot, Executive Condos appeal strongly to upgraders and young couples seeking more affordable alternatives.

Key Eligibility Requirements

Executive Condo buyers must form a family nucleus with at least two Singapore citizens or one citizen plus one Permanent Resident.

A five-year minimum occupation period applies before resale in the open market. Foreigners may only purchase after ten years.

Policy Measures (December 9, 2013)

HDB introduced three regulatory measures. 30% Mortgage Servicing Ratio Cap: buyers can allocate maximum 30% of monthly gross income toward EC loan servicing.

Resale Levy for Second-Timer Applicants: applies to projects where land was purchased on or after December 9, 2013, varying by previous flat type.

Reduced Cancellation Fees: lowered from 20% to 5% of purchase price.

Advantages of Executive Condos

Executive Condos offer no Additional Buyer's Stamp Duty, no Seller's Stamp Duty (given the five-year minimum occupation requirement), and deferred payment options.

Owners enjoy unrestricted commercial/industrial property investment, condo-quality design and amenities, CPF housing grants up to $30,000 for first-timers, and pricing approximately 20–30% below comparable private condominiums, supporting stronger capital appreciation potential.
