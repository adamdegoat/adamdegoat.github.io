Commercial / Centrium Square - Freehold Office Showflat

Centrium Square - Freehold Office Showflat

Centrium Square is a large freehold mixed-use commercial development in Singapore's District 08, featuring premium office units, medical suites, and retail shops in a highly visible location near Farrer Park MRT station.

Overview

The project comprises 143 premium office units (with 4.5m high ceiling, column free and ensuite), 39 medical suites and 49 retail shops located within one minute's walk to Farrer Park MRT and City Square Mall. The development is centrally positioned with easy access via major roads and expressways, making it suitable for business expansion, new setups, relocation, or investment purposes.

Convenient and Central Location

Outside the ERP zone on Serangoon Road. 3 minutes' walk to Farrer Park MRT, City Square Mall, and Connexion. 5 minutes' drive to Novena MRT, Bugis Junction, and Raffles City. 10 minutes' drive to CBD and Marina Bay areas. Connected to CTE and PIE expressways.

New Distinctive Integrated Commercial Development

The property features a 115m wide and highly visible frontage facing Serangoon Road with a distinctive 5-storey podium facade showcasing geometric tessellation patterns reflecting Little India's heritage. The building incorporates high performance, low-emissivity double glazed panels for energy efficiency and noise reduction, achieving Gold Green Mark Certification.

Office Units and Medical Suites

All units feature a 4.9-metre floor-to-floor height with column-free, flexible layouts allowing unit combinations. Full-height glass windows provide unobstructed views of Little India, Orchard Road, and Marina Bay. Each unit includes individual toilet facilities and A/C ledges. The office tower has 6 passenger and 2 service lifts.

Retail Units

Retail spaces face Serangoon Road with wide unobstructed frontage. Two pairs of escalators at atrium ends maximise shop exposure on Levels 1 and 2. A diverse mix of restaurants, shops, supermarkets, and food courts support office users' daily needs.

Fifth Storey Facilities

The podium roof features an outdoor roof garden with a 12-metre high covered sky terrace below the office tower and two outdoor pavilions for communal use.

Investment Benefits

Large freehold site with limited comparable supply. No Additional Buyers Stamp Duty or Sellers Stamp Duty. Loan-to-value up to 80% of purchase price. No foreign ownership restrictions.

Project Details

Developer: Feature Development Pte Ltd. Location: 320 Serangoon Road. Tenure: Freehold. Expected TOP: 31 January 2021. Expected Legal Completion: 31 January 2024. Total Units: 143 office units, 39 medical suites, 49 retail shops. Site Area: 6,365.8 sqm. Car Park: 124 + 4 handicapped spaces. Architect: DP Architects Pte Ltd. Solicitor: Rodyk & Davidson.
