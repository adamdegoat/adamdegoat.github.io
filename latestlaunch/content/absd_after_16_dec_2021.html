Additional Buyer Stamp Duty (ABSD) after 16 Dec 2021

This guide addresses frequently asked questions about ABSD regulations implemented after December 16, 2021, in Singapore.

Residency Status

The applicable ABSD rate depends on the buyer's residency status on the purchase date. Buyers must have received official residency status from the Immigration and Checkpoint Authority before the purchase to qualify for lower rates. Future changes to residency don't affect the original ABSD calculation.

Property Count

Once a sale contract is executed (Acceptance to Option to Purchase), the existing property is considered sold and no longer counts toward the individual's property total, even if legal completion hasn't occurred.

ABSD Rates

Backdating an Option to Purchase to avoid higher ABSD rates constitutes a criminal offense under Section 62 of the Stamp Duties Act. Penalties include fines up to $10,000 or imprisonment up to 3 years. The Inland Revenue Authority conducts regular audits.

ABSD Spouses Remission

Married couples with at least one Singapore citizen spouse can receive ABSD refunds on second property purchases if both names appear on the deed and the first property sells within 6 months of purchase or TOP/CSC issuance—whichever is earlier. The couple must remain married and cannot acquire a third property before the first sale.

6-Month Sale Timeline Extension

No extensions exist for the 6-month timeline, regardless of market conditions or personal circumstances. The policy strictly applies to all applicants.

Single Purchasers

ABSD remission applies only to married couples. Singles, parent-child, and sibling partnerships cannot access this concession.

Third Property Purchase

ABSD refund does not extend to third or subsequent properties, even if the couple ultimately owns only one property.

Seller's Stamp Duty (SSD) Holding Period

The SSD holding period begins from the instrument's execution date (Acceptance to OTP or Sale and Purchase Agreement), not the Option to Purchase issue date.

SSD vs. HDB MOP

SSD holding periods differ from HDB minimum occupation periods. SSD applies from the purchase instrument date, while MOP typically starts at key collection.

Lease Duty Renewal

Lease renewals requiring executed documents incur lease duty as new leases.

Lease Duty Refunds

No refunds apply to leases already in use. Refunds only apply to aborted leases never commenced.
