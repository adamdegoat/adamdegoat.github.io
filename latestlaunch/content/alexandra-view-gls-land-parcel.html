Alexandra View Government Land Sale (GLS) parcel by URA

A residential development with commercial space at Alexandra View was awarded to Tang Skyline Pte Ltd following a competitive bidding process in November 2015.

Latest News Update (13 Nov 2015)

Tang Skyline Pte Ltd, a subsidiary of Far East Consortium International, won the land parcel with the highest bid of S$376.9 million after competing against 10 other bidders. The bid equated to approximately S$9,158 per square metre on gross floor area or $851 per square foot. Estimated breakeven pricing for the new development was projected between $1,350 to $1,400 per square foot.

Site Specifications

The development allows for a maximum of 21 commercial units, including a supermarket of at least 1,000 sqm. The 99-year leasehold site could yield approximately 400 residential units across 8,398.5 square metres, positioned adjacent to Redhill MRT Station.

Site Area: 8,398.5 m² (Plot 1); 30.8 m² (Plot 2). Lease Period: 99 years. Allowable Development: Residential with commercial at ground floor.

Amenities and Features

The location offers proximity to recreational parks at Telok Blangah Hill and Mount Faber. Nearby commercial amenities include Tiong Bahru Plaza, Queensway Shopping Centre, and IKEA Alexandra. Educational institutions in the vicinity comprise Crescent Girls' School and Gan Eng Seng Primary and Secondary Schools.
