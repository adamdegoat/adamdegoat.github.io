Why Buy Now?

How will the real estate market shift in 2025? This page examines whether current conditions in Singapore's property market favour buying now.

IF INTEREST RATES FALL, WILL HOME LOAN MORTGAGE RATES ALSO FALL?

If Federal Reserve interest rates decline, home loan mortgage rates typically follow downward. Historical data shows that even with elevated rates, property demand has remained resilient in Singapore's market.

IF MORTGAGE INTEREST RATES FALL, WILL DEMAND FOR PROPERTY RISE?

Transaction numbers dropped after the ABSD was raised in 2021's cooling measures and again in 2023's raising of ABSD for foreigners from 30% to 60%. Even though transaction volumes were steadily lower, the prices remained resilient with new launches seeing a drop in price due to the foreigner ABSD increase. This 2023 cooling measure has led to a huge increase in foreigners applying for Singapore PR status in 2024 to save on ABSD. Expect to see a lot more Singapore PRs being approved soon and entering the real estate market in 2025 and beyond.

IF PROPERTY DEMAND RISES, WILL SELLERS / DEVELOPERS PRICE UP OR DOWN?

Even with higher interest rates and demand down due to cooling measures, prices continue to rise as sellers are unwilling to lower prices (especially resale). New launch prices saw a slight decrease in 2024.

IF PRICES ARE EXPECTED TO RISE, DO YOU WANT TO ENTER NOW OR LATER?

"Property market bears expect slower population growth, an ageing population, and a structural growth slowdown to weigh on the long-term property market outlook. We disagree and believe home prices will double by 2030. That implies a 5 to 6 percent increase per annum and would mark a reversal from a long downtrend in home prices.", Morgan Stanley (2017)

New condo launches in OCR were selling at an average of $1,300 psf in 2017 and are expected to hit around $2,700 psf by 2030.

EVEN HDB FLAT PRICES ARE ALSO RISING, 1,000 UNITS OF MILLION DOLLAR FLATS EXPECTED TO BE TRANSACTED IN 2024

Million-dollar HDB flats represent a significant market segment, with approximately 1,000 units expected to transact in 2024 across locations including Marine Parade, Yishun, Sembawang, and Clementi.

IS PROPERTY A HEDGE AGAINST INFLATION / INVESTMENT / SAVINGS PLAN?

"In the long run, inflation is just a tax on holding money.", Adam Smith (The Father of Economics)

Property serves as protection against inflation. Historical data demonstrates property appreciation consistently outpaces inflation rates.

NEW LAUNCHES IS A SAFE ASSET CLASS

Multiple case studies show owners achieved substantial profits from new launch purchases at Seaside Residences, Grandeur Park Residences, and Treasure at Tampines through disciplined entry and exit strategies.

EMPOWERING YOU TOWARDS A RESPECTFUL RETIREMENT

Property is both a savings mechanism and an investment vehicle. Progressive payment structures allow buyers to 'save as you earn' while building equity through property ownership.
