About Zyon Grand

Soaring 62 storeys high, the iconic twin tower residence of ZYON GRAND is set to transform into a new icon near Singapore's coveted River Valley precinct, with direct access to Havelock MRT station.

Inspired by the Art of Ikebana

Taking prime position in a prestigious enclave undergoing revitalisation, ZYON GRAND stands at the heart of what is fast becoming the city's new focal point. Located near the River Valley precinct, ZYON GRAND offers a life that is more vibrant, more liveable, and more lovable – ideal for families and the discerning alike.

Sweeping views from selected residences extend from the downtown skyline and Marina Bay Sands, all the way to Sentosa.

Architecture & Design

Designed by world-renowned Japanese architecture firm Nikken Sekkei in collaboration with ADDP Architects LLP, ZYON GRAND is an unrivalled integrated development crowned by two stunning residential towers infused with Japanese sensibility and refinement.

Integrated Development

The only integrated development directly linked to Havelock MRT station, ZYON GRAND is a truly unrivalled residence that brings together luxury homes, restaurants (F&B) and supermarket, an early childhood development centre, and Singapore's first long-stay serviced apartments.

Neighbourhood

Step into a neighbourhood filled with sophisticated charm and gentle rhythm. From artisanal cafés and glitzy shops to leafy trails and hilltop strolls, ZYON GRAND places you at the heart of it all.

Landscape & Nature

Inspired by the flowing forms of canyons and rivers, the lush landscape at ZYON GRAND weaves nature seamlessly into your home. Like ikebana, every element is choreographed to evoke poetry and elegance.

Arrival & Entry

Arrive at an exclusive drop-off reserved solely for residents. As an integrated development directly linked to Havelock MRT station, ZYON GRAND offers unmatched connectivity.

The Grand Pavilion makes an unforgettable first impression both poetic and sublime, with an elegant sculptural garden cradled by reflective waters.

Relaxation Lagoon

Flowing gently from the Wellness Oasis, the Relaxation Lagoon is surrounded by water for both play and pause. Reminiscent of meandering streams and shaded river islands, this tropical retreat welcomes everyone.

Social Habitat

From the Grand Pavilion, step into the Social Habitat, where spaces like Club Gourmet and Club Social are created to inspire connection and celebration.
