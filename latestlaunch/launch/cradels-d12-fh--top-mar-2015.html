About Cradels

Cradels is a freehold condominium in District 12, completed in March 2015. The city-fringe Balestier and Toa Payoh belt it sits in offers central convenience at a more accessible entry point than the prime core.

City-fringe District 12

District 12 spans the Balestier, Toa Payoh and Serangoon fringe — close enough to the city for a short commute, while keeping the everyday affordability and the food, heritage and amenity mix that make the area popular with both owner-occupiers and tenants.

Freehold tenure in a city-fringe location like this is the long-hold attraction for buyers who don't want a lease running down.

For buyers today

Completed in 2015, Cradels is a resale proposition today — suited to buyers who want a freehold central-fringe unit that's already established.
