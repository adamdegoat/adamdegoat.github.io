About Springleaf Residence

Mega condo only 2-mins walk to Springleaf MRT Station by Guocoland. The development offers seamlessly integrated urban living within a 100-meter sheltered walkway to the Thomson-East Coast Line station, positioned near the private Springleaf landed enclave.

Location and Connectivity: Seamlessly Integrated Urban Living

Its strategic location within 100m sheltered to Springleaf MRT station on the Thomson-East Coast Line ensures effortless access to the city centre, Woodlands Regional Centre, and other key destinations. With easy reach via major expressways like CTE and SLE, as well as upcoming transport enhancements such as the North-South Corridor, commuting is a breeze.

Amenities and Lifestyle: Where Nature Meets Urban Comfort

From premier shopping destinations like Thomson Plaza, AMK Hub, and Junction 9 to diverse dining options spanning hawker centres to upscale restaurants, every need is catered to. Outdoor enthusiasts can explore the nearby Upper Seletar Reservoir Park and Springleaf Nature Park.

Design and Sustainability: Luxury Rooted in Eco-Consciousness

From indigenous landscaping to energy-efficient features, every detail embodies environmental responsibility. The range of unit types, from 1 to 5 bedrooms, showcases thoughtful GFA harmonisation, with floor plans optimised for spaciousness and natural light. Backed by reputable developers and the escalating demand for housing in the vicinity, this development holds significant appeal for discerning investors.
