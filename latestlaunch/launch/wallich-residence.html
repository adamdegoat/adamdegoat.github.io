About Wallich Residence

Singapore's Tallest Luxury Apartment

Wallich Residence represents an exclusive collection of 181 luxury homes with one to four-bedroom units, junior penthouses, and a signature penthouse. Each home is impeccably finished with top-of-the-line materials and fittings, a reflection of the distinctive aesthetics that Wallich Residence offers.

Four Levels of Amenities

The residence features hotel-inspired concierge service, an infinity pool at level 39, a private library at level 52, and observation platforms at level 62. Residents enjoy sky gardens, cabanas, private chef dining options, and theatrical screening spaces for entertaining.

Tanjong Pagar Centre Integration

Wallich Residence is located on the 39th to 64th floors of Tanjong Pagar Centre, an integrated vertical city housing Grade-A offices, hospitality, retail, and dining. The development includes a reimagined Tanjong Pagar Park and a 32,000-square-foot City Room designed for community gatherings and cultural events.

Urban Park and City Room

The centrepiece features unique public spaces that provide activity and open space to visitors, including public art installations and outdoor performance areas. This design extends recreational activities well beyond business hours.

Location and Neighborhood

The property sits directly above Tanjong Pagar MRT station, positioning residents near the CBD while offering proximity to Chinatown's heritage sites and the lively dining scene along Ann Siang Hill, Club Street, Tras Street, and Duxton Hill.
