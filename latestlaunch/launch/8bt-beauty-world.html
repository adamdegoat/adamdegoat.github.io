About 8@BT

8@BT draws inspiration from Bukit Timah's rich, natural and cultural heritage and offers a sustainable lifestyle that centers on a cosmopolitan vibe and tropical industrial heritage. The development is only a 2-minute stroll to Beauty World MRT Station, providing seamless connectivity to major locations.

Location & Connectivity

Descriptions of proximity to Beauty World MRT, transport hub access, nearby schools (primary and international), historical landmarks (Old Bukit Timah Railway Station, Old Ford Factory), and nature reserves including Bukit Timah Nature Reserve and Singapore Botanic Gardens.

Lifestyle & Dining

Information on culinary offerings along Lorong Kilat, Chun Tin Road, and Cheong Chin Nam Road, plus access to green spaces and dining establishments within the neighborhood.

Facilities & Design

Details on resort-style facilities, lobby amenities (Fitness Room, Meeting Room, Living Room), thoughtful unit layouts, natural lighting, and ventilation.
