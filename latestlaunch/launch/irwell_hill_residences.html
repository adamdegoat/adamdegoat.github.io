About Irwell Hill Residences

Irwell Hill Residences is an upcoming new condo along Irwell Bank Road near Great World City, featuring two iconic 36-storey towers comprising 540 units on an elevated terrain integrating with the existing four heritage raintrees. The development showcases a stunning champagne gold pixel-patterned facade redefining Singapore's skyline, designed by world-renowned Dutch architecture firm MVRDV.

Location & Accessibility

Irwell Hill Residences is a short stroll to the upcoming Great World MRT station (Thomson-East Coast Line), and near to Orchard MRT station (North-South Line), and Orchard Road shopping belt. The site sits at the junction of Irwell Bank Road and River Valley Road, offering quick access to a plethora of amenities and facilities near Great World City shopping mall and the Orchard Road shopping belt.

Schools & Amenities

Popular primary schools within the area include River Valley Primary School (within 1km) and Anglo-Chinese School (Junior). Notable nearby landmarks include Orchard Road and Tanglin shopping belts, Clarke Quay, Dempsey Hill, Tiong Bahru Conservation Area and the Singapore Botanic Gardens.

Architecture & Design

Irwell Hill Residences create a lush haven around the conserved heritage raintrees offering respite from the bustle of the city, with wellness and comfort at its heart. The project was envisioned by world-renowned architecture practice MVRDV from the Netherlands, known for their progressive and stunning designs, marking the architectural firm's first residential project in Singapore.

The architecture integrates sustainable construction method and connects the botanical landscape with the outdoor spaces of the balconies to create a green and enjoyable dwelling experience.

Unit Features & Finishes

Apartments come with thoughtful touches that allow one to transform spaces to accommodate working from home and designs that incorporate sustainable materials. Each unit features marble flooring in the living and dining room, finished with branded fittings from Gessi, sanitary ware from Geberit & equipped with Miele & Bosch appliances. Units include a Smart Hub gateway allowing homeowners to remotely control certain appliances, such as the air conditioning & lighting, via a mobile app.

Facilities & Amenities

Residents benefit from a wide array of facilities catered to delight and entertain. Health-focused amenities include three pools, the Spa Pool, Club Pool and the 50m Infinity-Edge Raintree Pool, or work out at the state-of-the-art Gym at Irwell elevated above road level or enjoy a game at the Tennis Court.

Irwell Vista, located on Level 24 of both towers, features a purpose-designed workspace exclusively for residents, Vista Co-Work. The bespoke workstations are equipped with WiFi and charging points, all in a setting that offers a spectacular view of the sky framed by greenery.

Premier Residential Services

The on-site Premier Residential Services provide residents with seamless assistance from our team of hospitality-trained Residential Hosts, from day-to-day needs such as arranging for laundry and delivery acceptance and transport bookings; to special occasions like catering for parties and restaurant reservations.

Land Acquisition & Valuation

Irwell Hill Residences was a government land sales (GLS) site that attracted a total of seven bids. The highest bid of $583.9 million was submitted by CDL Perseus, translating to $1,515 psf per plot ratio (ppr).

About the Developer, CDL

City Developments Limited (CDL) is a leading global real estate company with a network spanning 104 locations in 29 countries and regions. The company has a proven track record of 60 years in real estate development, investment and management and has developed over 48,000 homes and owns around 23 million square feet of gross floor area in residential for lease, commercial and hospitality assets globally.
