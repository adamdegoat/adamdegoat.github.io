About Tre Residences

TRE Residences is urban resort living at its finest — a unique botanical retreat where the elements of nature, water, earth and sky, are landscaped in a layered vista of lush greenery, water gardens and sky terraces.

Location & Connectivity

The development provides access to Orchard Road within minutes by car. Nearby expressways include KPE, PIE, and CTE. Only a 1-minute walk to the Aljunied MRT station connects you seamlessly to a day of sun, sand and sea at East Coast Park.

Schools in proximity: Geylang Methodist Primary/Secondary, Kong Hwa Primary, Dunman High, Chung Cheng High.

Amenities & Features

The property features integrated indoor and outdoor spaces with a shimmering azure pool, wave pool, Infinity Fall water feature, Serengeti landscape, Alfresco dining and BBQ Bay, gymnasium, Floating Lounge, and Sun Lounge.

The Amazon Terrace on the 4th level offers a tropical bliss nestled on the 4th level where the sights, sounds, and scents of nature come together in playful wonder.

Accessibility

CBD access is convenient via MRT connectivity. The Orchard Road shopping belt offers big-name boutiques, niche dining, and entertainment options. Nearby attractions include East Coast Park, Marina Barrage, Marina Bay Sands, Gardens by the Bay, Singapore Flyer, Katong, Old Airport Road Food Centre, and Singapore Sports Hub.
