About Hillhaven

Hillhaven is a residential development with 341 units located at 5 Hillview Rise in Hillview, offering 2 to 4-bedroom residences across two towers of 27 and 28 floors, providing convenient access to a growing network of businesses as well as several reputable education institutions, near nature reserves and shopping malls.

Location

Located at Hillview Rise, Hillhaven is a residential development with 341 units offering 2- to 4- bedroom residences in 2 towers of 27 and 28 floors respectively.

Hillhaven's strategic proximity to Hillview MRT Station and two Integrated Transport Hubs – Beauty World and Bukit Panjang – provides quick and convenient access for residents.

Hillhaven is surrounded by amenities, including a variety of malls like HillV2 and The Rail Mall, as well as recreational spaces such as Hillview Community Club and the upcoming National Service Hub.

Facilities & Amenities

At Hillhaven, you will find opportunities to unwind through activities like working out at the gym or urban farming. Create new memories with family and friends at the pickleball court and function pavilions, most ideal for hosting various events.

Features include a 50m pool, Bikers' Corner, and access to the 20km Western Adventure Loop.

Unit Design

Hillhaven provides thoughtfully designed unit configurations. 2-bedroom units are well-suited for individuals starting their housing journey. 3-bedroom units have layouts designed to maximise space efficiency.

Selected 3-bedroom units include attached study rooms convertible to hobby or work spaces; 4-bedroom units suit larger or multi-generational families.

Architecture & Design

Inspired by the Bukit Timah Nature Reserve and the surrounding nature parks, the design captures an abstraction of the beauty of the tropical rainforest.

Designed by multiple President Design Award winning architect Arc Studio, and award-winning landscape architect Tinderbox.
