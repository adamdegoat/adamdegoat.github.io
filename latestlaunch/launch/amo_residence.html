About AMO Residence

AMO Residence is a new mixed-unit condominium development in mature Ang Mo Kio by three established developers. The project capitalizes on proximity to the Thomson-East Coast Line and views of Bishan-Ang Mo Kio Park and Lower Pierce Reservoir.

Location & Connectivity

Located on Ang Mo Kio Avenue 1, the development sits within walking distance of Mayflower MRT station on the Thomson-East Coast Line (TEL), expected operational by end of 2024. The site benefits from access via SLE and CTE expressways, with Orchard Road reachable in under 20 minutes via direct train.

Brighthill MRT Station, one stop away, will serve as an interchange for the upcoming Cross Island Line.

Nearby Amenities

The area features proximity to primary schools including Ai Tong School and CHIJ St Nicholas within 1km. Secondary schools, tertiary institutions, and international schools are within minutes. Bishan-Ang Mo Kio Park provides recreational green space adjacent to the development.

Design & Facilities

The landscape follows De Stijl minimalist principles with organic overlays representing nature. A 50-meter lap pool anchors the site between the two towers. Amenities include swimming pool, function rooms, gymnasium, tennis court, and communal spaces. Units feature smart home technology and modern kitchen finishes.
