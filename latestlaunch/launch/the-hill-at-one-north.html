About The Hill @ One North

The Hill @ One North is a mixed-use development in District 05 within the One-North innovation hub, positioned less than five minutes' walk from Buona Vista MRT and Star Vista shopping mall. The project combines residential living with commercial spaces in a pedestrian-centric environment designed around research, business, and lifestyle amenities.

Location Of The Hill @ One North

The project occupies a space bounded by Slim Barracks Rise and One-North Park within Fusionpolis. Proximity to key precincts like Biopolis and Nepal Hill, plus pedestrian corridors along Portsdown Road and One-North Gateway, enhance connectivity. One-North Park adjacent to the development serves recreational purposes for walking, running, and cycling.

One-North Context

One-North was conceptualized back in 1991 as part of Singapore's National Technology Plan. The hub evolved from a research destination into a vibrant ecosystem hosting global company headquarters, startups, and innovation centers. The area emphasizes an integrated work-live-play-learn environment combining industry facilities with residential communities and educational institutions.

Living Experience

The development features smart home technology at your fingertips, creating a connected and modern living environment. Marketing emphasizes contemporary design with open layouts, premium finishes, and smart home technology positioned as more than housing—a statement of modern urban living.

About The Developer

Kingsford Development Pte Ltd, incorporated in 2011, operates as a subsidiary of the broader Kingsford Group involved in real estate development, property management, and manufacturing across China and Australia. The company won the Singapore Prestige Brand Award - Global Brands in 2019.
