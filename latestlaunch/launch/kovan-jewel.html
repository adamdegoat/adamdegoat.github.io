About Kovan Jewel

Kovan Jewel is a new freehold residential development in Singapore's northeastern region, designed to blend contemporary architecture with colonial aesthetic influences. Located in the established Kovan neighbourhood, the project offers boutique living with proximity to transit, shopping, and community amenities within a vibrant precinct alongside Hougang and Serangoon.

Location & Neighbourhood

Kovan is a vibrant, well-established residential area in Singapore's northeastern region. The neighbourhood offers a unique blend of modern convenience, cultural diversity, and a strong sense of community. Residents enjoy access to Kovan MRT Station (496m away), Heartland Mall, Kovan Market & Food Centre, and multiple supermarkets including FairPrice and Cold Storage.

The area benefits from proximity to NEX Shopping Mall in nearby Serangoon. The Serangoon Integrated Transport Hub seamlessly links bus interchange and MRT stations with commercial developments. Kovan is well-connected via major expressways including CTE, SLE, TPE, and KPE.

Nearby Schools

Within 1km: Paya Lebar Methodist Girls School (Primary), Xinghua Primary School.

Within 1-2km: CHIJ Our Lady of Good Counsel, Holy Innocents Primary School, Maris Stella High School, Montford Junior School, Rosyth School, Zhonghua Primary School.

Future Growth Drivers

Three key growth areas near Kovan include the Serangoon sub-regional centre, Serangoon Integrated Transport Hub, and the future Paya Lebar Airbase relocation. These developments, along with Defu, are expected to create lively commerce and lifestyle hubs for residents and enhance property values.

Facilities & Design

Kovan Jewel features green spaces and recreational amenities including a swimming pool, poolside pavilions, gym facilities, and vintage swing sets. The project is designed by Team Design, architects behind The Sail @ Marina Bay and Stars of Kovan.

The development offers unit types ranging from 1-bedroom to 4-bedroom layouts, including options with studies and dual-key configurations.
