About Terra Hill

Terra Hill is a freehold condo located at Yew Siang Road in D05 - Buona Vista / West Coast of Singapore brought to you by Hoi Hup Realty & Sunway Developments. The project sits adjacent to the Greater Southern Waterfront development and offers hillside positioning with panoramic views toward Kent Ridge Park.

Location & Connectivity

Terra Hill occupies a hillside plot near Pasir Panjang MRT station. The site is less than a short 4 minute stroll from Pasir Panjang MRT on the Circle Line and approximately 10 minutes' drive to VivoCity and Sentosa Island, with 15 minutes to the CBD and 20 minutes to Orchard Road. Nearby schools include the National University of Singapore, Blangah Rise Primary School, Anglo-Chinese School (Independent), ISS International School, Tanglin Trust School, and The Japanese School.

Greater Southern Waterfront

Spanning from Pasir Panjang to Marina East, the coastal transformation will set forth new standards of urban life, work and play. Development occurs in phases beginning with the former Pasir Panjang Power District, Keppel Club, and Mount Faber within 5-10 years.

Landscape & Amenities

The site features tiered terrain with distinct zones including The Valley, The Sanctuary, The Alcove, The Enclave, and The Hillside. Amenities include Chill-Out Deck, hot tub, Jacuzzi seating, Family BBQ Pavilion, Club Room, Screening Room, and Fitness Studio with TechnoGym equipment. Terra House is a space dedicated for good times with social spaces across two storeys.

Unit Design

Thoughtful integrations amplify a space with conscientious planning and efficient design. Selected units include spacious store rooms. All master bedrooms feature full height wardrobes and side panel dressers ensuring maximum storage. Interior finishes use brands including De Dietrich, Gessi, Laufen, and Samsung.

Nearby Amenities

Proximity to Seah Imm Food Centre, VivoCity (Singapore's largest shopping mall), Pasir Panjang Food Centre, Alexandra Retail Center, Queensway Shopping Centre, and nearby NTUC Fairprice locations.

Developer Background

Hoi Hup Sunway is described as an award winning collaboration. Their previous project Sophia Hills received Gold at the FIABCI World Prix d'Excellence Awards 2020. Ki Residences at Brookvale received multiple design and sustainability awards including ProperGuru Asia Proper Awards and EdgeProp Singapore Excellence Award Design Excellence.

Unique Selling Points

Joint venture between reputable developers. Proximity to amenities and Pasir Panjang MRT. Adjacent to Greater Southern Waterfront. Direct access to Kent Ridge Park. City-fringe location near Mapletree Business City, Sentosa, VivoCity. Freehold status with rare hillside positioning.
