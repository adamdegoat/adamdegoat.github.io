About Royal Green

Royal Green is a freehold condo in Bukit Timah (former Royalville) developed by Allgreen. The development comprises 285 units on an exclusive low-density residential enclave with all units oriented north-south, situated within three minutes' walk to Sixth Avenue MRT.

Location & Accessibility

The District 10 area is especially sought after for young couples and families, featuring multiple F&B establishments and top schools including Nanyang Girls' High School, Methodist Girls' School, Raffles Girls' Primary, Nanyang Primary, Hwa Chong Institution, and National Junior College.

Sixth Avenue MRT provides convenient access within walking distance, with approximately 15–20 minute train rides to Singapore's city centre.

Development Characteristics

RoyalGreen features eight residential blocks with a maximum of five storeys, creating pockets of sunlight through lush greenery. The enchanting green landscape provides an integrated nature experience while maintaining an exclusive low-density development atmosphere in a private residential enclave.

Amenities & Nearby Attractions

The vicinity offers food and drinks, sports, and art establishments. Singapore Botanic Gardens (UNESCO World Heritage Site) is 2.6km away, providing plenty of spaces for nature-related activities.
