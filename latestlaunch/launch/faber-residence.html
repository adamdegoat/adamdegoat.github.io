About Faber Residence

A new condominium development in Singapore's Clementi district positioned as the first major residential launch in the Faber Hills estate in over a decade. The project combines riverside living with proximity to vibrant urban amenities, featuring extensive landscaping and courtyard-centered design.

Welcome to Faber Residence

Welcome to Faber Residence where serenity and nature surround you, and your home is luxuriously situated by a river. Yet moments away, Clementi buzzes with vibrancy, and Jurong Lake District nearby offers new ways to work and play.

The development previewed October 18, selling more than 85% of its total units during its preview weekend.

Location & Connectivity

Fronting the Ulu Pandan River nestled within the Faber Hills landed housing estate, the project features direct access to the park connector. The future Jurong Town Hall MRT station is nearby, and the Cross Island Line also serves this region.

A short sheltered walk takes you to a bus stop, so you can easily get to Clementi and Jurong East MRT stations. You're minutes to AYE and PIE.

Neighbourhood Character

Set in one of Clementi's most exclusive neighbourhoods, Faber Residence occupies a very special enclave of private houses and wide streets. Entry price for semi-detached homes at Faber Hills is around $6.5 million.

Educational Proximity

You're close to good schools of all levels at Faber Residence, in particular the prestigious Nan Hua Primary School within 1km. From the nearby Pei Tong Primary School, Clementi Town Secondary School, and National University of Singapore up the road.

Growth Areas & Strategic Position

In addition to JLD, you're also surrounded by key growth areas such as an education belt, one-north's hub for research, science and infocomm technology, and the future Greater Southern Waterfront. One-north is Singapore's Silicon Valley, home to A*STAR, Apple, P&G and Grab.

Nature & Recreation

At your doorstep is Sungei Ulu Pandan, a beautiful green river which the Ulu Pandan Park Connector runs alongside. From here, you're connected to other nature trails nearby and the stunning Jurong Lake Gardens moments away.

Design & Architecture

The design draws inspiration from both its riverfront setting and its position within a landed estate. The façade features warm, earthy tones that blend seamlessly with lush landscaping, which includes over 140 plant species, many of which are native to the riverbank. About 25% of the units enjoy direct views of the river and park connector.

Amenities & Courtyards

GuocoLand worked with project architect P&T Consultants and landscape architect Ortus Design to introduce two inner layers of open spaces and views. These include a series of three swimming pools - a wading pool, a leisure pool and a 50m lap pool — which together create the impression of a seamless 100m-long water feature.

The landscape is centred around a network of 18 courtyard gardens, each blending seamlessly with the outdoor environment. Featuring lily ponds, quiet seating nooks, communal pavilions, shaded tree groves and lush flowering beds.

Unit Features

A feature of GuocoLand residences, the provision of flexible spaces means you can move a wall to create a larger living area for your growing family. Or convert a flex room to a baby's nursery, study or games room.
