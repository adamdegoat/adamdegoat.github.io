About Park Colonial

Park Colonial is a brand new upcoming condominium development located right beside Woodleigh MRT Station, developed by CEL Unique Development. The project sits on a prime city fringe plot in Bidadari Estate that is under the Government Master Plan to expand the city.

Location & Connectivity

Park Colonial is strategically positioned next to Woodleigh MRT with connectivity via the North East Line. The site offers proximity to major expressways (CTE, PIE, KPE) and is approximately 10–15 minutes by car to Orchard, CBD, and MBFC areas. Serangoon MRT Interchange is one stop away, linking Circle Line connections.

Nearby Amenities

Schools nearby include Cedar Primary, Maris Stella Primary, St. Andrew's Junior School, Cedar Girls' Secondary, Maris Stella High School, St. Andrew's Secondary, Stamford American International School, and Australian International School.

Shopping options include NEX Shopping Mall, The Venue Shopping Mall at Potong Pasir, upcoming Woodleigh Mall, and Poiz Center. Dining options include Yeo Keng Nam Chicken Rice, Ser Seng Herbs Restaurant, and Nam Hoe Noodles House.

Why Buy Park Colonial

Key selling points include minutes to CBD and City area, proximity to CTE and PIE, along NEL and 1 stop to NEL Serangoon Circle Line, good capital appreciation from Bidadari Rejuvenation, upcoming shopping developments, potentially high rental demand, and choice of 1–5 bedroom unit types.

Development Features

The project includes colonial-inspired landscaping across two layers of luxury amenities. Features highlighted include the Island Pool, Island Deck, Sensory Spa Pavilion, Colonial Boulevard, and Tranquil Waterway.

Bidadari Greenway

The Bidadari Greenway will run alongside the Bidadari Park Drive Road, forming a green spine that traverses through the estate, enabling residents to walk and cycle seamlessly.
