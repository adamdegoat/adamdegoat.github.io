About TwentyOne Angullia Park

TwentyOne Angullia Park sits in a secluded cul-de-sac off Orchard Boulevard, positioned opposite Ion Orchard shopping centre. Designed by award-winning SCDA Architects, the development won recognition at the Asia Pacific Property Awards 2015 for Residential High-rise Architecture.

Architectural Design

The development is designed to exude pure, pristine and pragmatic design, featuring high-efficiency layouts with travertine materials and glass and steel elements. Spaces showcase full height windows, high ceilings and panoramic views.

Location & Positioning

The property enjoys proximity to luxury hotels including St Regis, Marriott, and Four Seasons, along with upscale dining and retail options on Orchard Road. Marketed as Singapore's definitive luxury address, comparable to renowned global landmarks like The Peak (Hong Kong) and Central Park (New York).
