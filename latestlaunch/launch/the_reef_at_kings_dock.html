About The Reef at King's Dock

The Reef at King's Dock is located within 2 mins walk to Harbourfront MRT interchange & VIVO CITY mega mall. Designed by Rotterdam-based architect Kees Christiaanse, this waterfront development features Singapore's first floating deck in a residential development.

Location & Connectivity

The Reef At King's Dock stands in a waterfront location that is well connected to the CBD by MRT and car. Residents enjoy access to Harbourfront MRT station interchange serving both the North-East Line and the Circle Line within a two-minute walk, plus proximity to Singapore's mega shopping mall at Vivo City just three minutes away.

The project is within a short walking distance to Mount Faber Park, Telok Blangah and Labrador Nature Park. Major highways including West Coast Highway, Ayer Rajah Expressway, and Central Expressway are nearby.

Greater Southern Waterfront Context

The Greater Southern Waterfront, which extends from Pasir Panjang to Marina East, will be transformed into a new major gateway and location for urban living along Singapore's southern coast. Development will occur in phases over the next 5-10 years, with a continuous waterfront promenade will seamlessly connect various places of interest.

The development includes the new Pasir Panjang Linear Park will connect West Coast Park to Labrador Nature Reserve. Port facilities relocating to Tuas by 2027 will free up prime land for residential and commercial redevelopment.

Signature Features

The Reef at King's Dock raises the bar for waterfront living. Bask on an expansive, 180-metre-long sun deck, the first ever in a residential development in Singapore. The deck is designed as a granite monolith that provides a spectacular seaside experience that lets residents be one with the sea.

Residents enjoy an ensemble of swimming pools, an Olympic-length pool, a splash pool for the kids, and a rejuvenation pool designed for ultimate relaxation, that move with the ebb and flow of the tide.

Natural Environment & Marine Life

Surrounding the glittering pools of The Reef at King's Dock lies an underwater sanctuary brimming with life. The coastal seawall features native shrubs, grasses and wildflowers in a glorious world where a variety of marine life thrive. A Marine Viewing deck allows residents to observe the vibrant underwater colony of corals and sea creatures.

Landscaping & Community Spaces

The landscaping at The Reef at King's Dock brings together the best of both worlds, a sublime marriage of openness and intimacy, a meeting of garden and sea. Features include pocket alcoves, carved out from the abundance of greenery and a reflecting pool.

Amenities include diverse play areas designed for endless hours of fun, tennis courts, Adventure Splash for children, and Petscape for pets.

Building Design & Architecture

The interplay of building sizes gives the entire development a luxurious and spacious atmosphere. Three distinct building types offer variety: The Courtyard Blocks house the most exclusive apartments with commanding views of the sea towards Sentosa Island and Mount Faber. Enjoy resort-living in a coastal oasis at the Garden Villas or be at the heart of it all, at the medium-rise Urban Villas.

Featuring predominantly metallic hues, the buildings shimmer as the rays fall upon your home.

Residential Design & Amenities

Inspired by resort spas and contemporary luxury hotels, the 429 residences at The Reef at King's Dock have been conceived as the perfect escape for the well-heeled and well-travelled.

Kitchens feature quality appliances including Miele and Whirlpool washer-dryer units. Bathrooms showcase polished marble floors, spacious mirror cabinets and fixtures from Dornbracht and bathwares from Geberit.

Every apartment features a bespoke sun screen made from the latest technology that protects your home while providing unobstructed views. Smart home features are seamlessly integrated to provide a truly elevated experience.

Sustainability

Designed with the utmost respect for our environment, most homes are oriented for natural coolness. The staggered blocks allow for natural ventilation and maximise the scenic views. The project achieved Building and Construction Authority of Singapore Green Mark GoldPLUS standard.
