About Royalgreen

285 exquisite freehold homes along Anamalai Avenue, just off Bukit Timah Road in prime District 10. The exclusive residential enclave is surrounded by low-rise private houses.

Location & Accessibility

Royalgreen is easily accessible by both car and public transport. Sixth Avenue MRT Station is within 5 minutes' walking distance. The development sits in an established neighbourhood known for its chic yet family friendly vibe, with proximity to dining and shopping on Sixth Avenue.

Education Belt

At the heart of the prominent Bukit Timah education belt, Royalgreen is within 1 km of Nanyang Girls' High School, and within 2 km of Methodist Girls' School (Primary & Secondary), National Junior College and Hwa Chong Institution, plus nearby international schools.

Design & Amenities

The development features a full-length 50m lap pool sparkling with turquoise water alongside children's and spa pools. Additional amenities include a gym with pool views, rooftop tennis court, gourmet pavilion, and hammock garden. Units showcase marble flooring in living areas and timber floors in bedrooms.
