About Marina One Residences

Spearheaded by Temasek and Khazanah, Marina One Residences is a world class integrated development at Marina Bay, comprising luxury residences, unique retail offerings and Grade-A offices nestled amidst lush greenery and green parks.

An Integrated Space Shaped Around You

Marina One integrates residences, retail, and Grade-A offices within the Marina Bay CBD, seamlessly connected to 21st century infrastructure with stunning city and sea views.

Design & Architecture

Award-winning design by Christoph Ingenhoven emphasises sustainable Supergreen architecture. Kathryn Gustafson designed a lush central garden creating a luxurious oasis at the heart of the city, offering tranquillity and serenity.

Location & Connectivity

Positioned near Marina Bay MRT (future largest interchange by 2021) and Downtown MRT with access to four MRT lines: North-South, Circle, Thomson, and Downtown lines. Connected to Raffles Place via future underground pedestrian link.

Green Features & Amenities

Features 65,000 sq ft of lush greenery and waterfall features. Amenities include 50-meter lap pool, 200 sq.m. fully equipped gym, private dining rooms, and teppanyaki terraces.

Unit Specifications

Premium finishes include Villeroy & Boch bath wares, Miele appliances, and Poggenpohl kitchen designs. Architectural identity features bespoke signature weave adorning the balcony facade.

Why Invest

Integrated live-work-play community at Marina Bay. Only development with 65,000 sq ft internal garden. Flanked by Marina Station Square and Central Linear Park. Supergreen development with multiple sustainability awards.
