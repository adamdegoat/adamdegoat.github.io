About Riverfront Residences

Riverfront Residences is a new upcoming launch nestled in Hougang Avenue 7, occupying a 200-metre riverfront with a gross plot ratio of 2.8 allowing approximately 1,472 units across 9 blocks of 16-storey condominiums plus 2 basement levels for parking.

Connectivity

Riverfront Residences will be optimally connected by various transportation modes. There will be Hougang Bus Interchange nearby as well as several MRT stations like Hougang, Buangkok and Kovan MRT. The residents will be able to travel to any other part of the city and its suburbs including Dhoby Ghaut and China Town as all these MRT stations are connected by the North East Line.

Nearby Amenities

Closely located to Riverfront Residences is Hougang Mall, a popular shopping mall close to the Hougang Central Bus Interchange. The Hougang Mall is also host to some important anchor tenants like the National Library Board, KFC, and McDonald's. Riverfront Residences also has a panoramic view of the river, that offers a majestic and beautiful environment and a pleasing scenery.

Riverfront Residences is close to amenities like Giant (Hougang Plaza), NTUC Fairprice (Hougang Lifestyle Mall), and Giant (Compassvale Bow). The closest shopping malls are The Midtown, Eastpoint Mall, and Hougang Mall. Nearby primary schools include CHIJ Our Lady Of The Nativity, Holy Innocents' Primary School, and Punggol Primary School.

Facilities

Set to be completed by 2024, this 1,472-unit residential project in Hougang has a large land area enabling developers to integrate over 100 facilities. These include three clubhouses with different amenities like a gym and KTV/movie room, co-working and study space, and a fully-equipped kitchen. There will also be a dockless bike sharing system for residents.

Key Advantages

Beautiful view of Serangoon River. Short walking distance to Hougang MRT and amenities. Tranquil and quiet part of Hougang, away from the hustle and bustle.
