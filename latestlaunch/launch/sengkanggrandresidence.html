About Sengkang Grand Residences

A rare fully integrated condo at Buangkok MRT by two extremely reputable local developers CapitaLand and City Developments Ltd (CDL), featuring 680 residential apartments integrated with amenities such as a hawker centre, community club, childcare centre and retail shops in a transformative 3.7-hectare development.

Overview & Location

CapitaLand and CDL won the commercial-residential site with a bid of $777.78 million, equating to $923.59 per sqft per plot ratio. The site, the largest awarded since 2015, sits adjacent to Buangkok MRT Station on the North-East Line, approximately 20 minutes from the Central Business District and nine stops from Dhoby Ghaut.

Development Concept

The integrated hub comprises a 3-storey retail mall with basement, nine blocks of 8-10-storey residential towers, a bus interchange (ground floor), hawker centre (second storey), and community club spanning three storeys. A childcare centre and retail shops complete the amenities mix.

Community & Greenery

A 'Linear Park' designed with play areas and street furniture with an Outdoor Activity Plaza along the entire frontage connects the development to adjacent Compassvale Ancilla Park via the 'Green Highline.' The Community Plaza located in the heart of the development is an attractive, naturally ventilated sheltered public space.

Connectivity & Transport

Direct access to Buangkok MRT Station and future bus interchange; easily accessible via KPE, SLE, and CTE expressways. Proximity to future Punggol Digital District and Seletar Aerospace Park.

Residential Amenities

5 Exclusive Lifestyle Clubs and 2 Club Houses featuring over 40 comprehensive facilities including a 50m Lap Pool, Parkour Gym, Treetop Walk, Poolside BBQ Pavilion, Trampoline Thrill, Aqua Hammock and Pets' Play.

Units feature efficient spacious layouts with branded appliances from De Dietrich and fittings by Hansgrohe and Duravit.
