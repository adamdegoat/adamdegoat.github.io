About 35 Gilstead

A freehold residential development comprising 70 units across three 5-storey blocks, positioned near Newton MRT with easy access to Orchard shopping belt and vibrant dining options.

Location & Connectivity

The project benefits from proximity to Newton MRT Station, within walking distance. The surrounding area offers retail, entertainment, and food options via the Novesta Shopping District, Newton Food Center, Dempsey Village, and Holland Village. Proximity to the Orchard Shopping Belt provides abundant malls, shops, restaurants, and bars. Anglo-Chinese School (Primary) and Anglo-Chinese School (Barker Road) are nearby for education.

Facilities & Amenities

Residents enjoy an Infinity Lap Pool, Wading Pool, Gymnasium, Sun Deck, Various Dining Pavilion and Lush Landscaping.

Construction Quality

The development features top quality imported fittings including Bosch & Gaggenau kitchen appliances, plus Hansgrohe & Duravit sanitary fixtures.
