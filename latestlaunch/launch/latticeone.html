About Lattice One

Lattice One is a quality condo developed by reputable developer TEE Land in District 20 — Upper Thomson. With only 48 units, this freehold home has a way of inspiring feelings of relaxed exuberance.

Overview

Located in Thomson, surrounded by private properties and landed homes, adjacent to Singapore's largest nature reserve.

Nestled within a private residential enclave and on the fringe of Lower Pierce Reservoir, Lattice One has accessibility to public transport network (including the upcoming Mayflower and Bright Hill MRT stations).

Nearby Amenities

Amenities, eateries such as Sembawang Hill Food Centre, Jln Leban Shopping area, 24 hours supermarket, Thomson Plaza, Junction 8 are all within close vicinity.

Within 1-2km of CHIJ St Nicholas' Girls School, Ai Tong School, Mayflower Primary School.

Key Features

Rooftop garden with sky views, VIDA Gateway smart home automation system, high-grade interior finishes, and approved balcony screen design.
