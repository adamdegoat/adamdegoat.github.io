About OUE Twin Peaks

Two towers, 462 stylish one, two and three-bedroom apartments oozing urban sophistication, each beautifully furnished and ready to move in. OUE Twin Peaks is Singapore's first fully furnished condominium with limited edition designer furniture fittings, located in prime district 9 — a highly sought after glamour and prestigious Singapore address.
