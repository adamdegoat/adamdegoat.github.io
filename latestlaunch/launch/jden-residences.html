About J'den Residences

J'den Residences is a 40-storey luxury mixed-use development replacing the former JCube shopping mall in Jurong East. The project combines 368 residential units with a two-storey shopping mall, positioned strategically near Jurong East MRT with connections to multiple transit lines and upcoming regional developments. This development represents part of Singapore's larger Jurong Lake District transformation initiative.

Strategic Location & Jurong Lake District Vision

The property benefits from Jurong Lake District's billion-dollar rejuvenation aimed at creating the largest commercial hub outside of the Central Business District (CBD) in Singapore. The masterplan emphasizes mixed-use development, business innovation hubs, recreational amenities, sustainability, and community spaces. Government projections expect the district to provide 100,000 new jobs and 20,000 new homes by 2040-2050.

Transportation & Connectivity

J'den connects directly to Jurong East MRT interchange (North-South Line, East-West Line) and will eventually access the Jurong Region Line (phased opening from 2027). The property integrates via J-Walk, a covered elevated pedestrian network linking to Westgate and IMM Building, plus the upcoming Jurong East Integrated Transport Hub featuring bus interchange, library, community club, and sports centre.

Proximity to Schools

The development sits near multiple primary and secondary institutions: Yuhua Primary School, Fuhua Primary School, Shuqun Primary School, Crest Secondary School, and Jurong Primary School, all within Jurong East, facilitating convenient commutes for students and families.

Nearby Shopping & Amenities

Westgate Mall (2-minute walk) offers retail, dining, and entertainment including a movie theatre. IMM Mall provides outlet-style shopping with discounted brands and parking. JEM Shopping Mall features extensive retail, dining, and entertainment including a rooftop water playground. Jurong Regional Library serves as a community hub with collections, programs, and events. Ng Teng Fong General Hospital provides comprehensive healthcare including specialized clinics and emergency care.

Investment & Development Advantages

Ten key reasons highlighted include: strategic positioning as secondary CBD; multiple MRT connections; vibrant business ecosystem including Jurong Innovation District; proximity to NTU and NIE; major healthcare facilities; diverse retail/dining options; green spaces like Jurong Lake Gardens; ongoing district transformation increasing property values; employment opportunities; and investment potential through area development prospects.

CapitaLand Portfolio Integration

J'den leverages CapitaLand's master developer status within Jurong Lake District, integrating with CapitaLand Integrated Commercial Trust properties (Westgate, IMM), CapitaLand Ascendas REIT holdings (International Business Parks, various tech buildings), and CapitaLand Investment assets.

Showflat & Unit Types

Display units include: 2-bedroom+study (Type Bs1, north-facing, Stack 1); 3-bedroom (Type C1, north-facing, Stack 3); 4-bedroom premium (Type D(p), north-facing, Stack 8). Draft floorplans showcase various configurations across multiple stacks.
