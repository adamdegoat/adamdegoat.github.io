About One Holland Village

The Village Is Growing

The refreshed, and re-energised Holland Village will be a living, vibrant community where people gather to live, play, work, eat, and shop – the Village brings everyone and everything together as one.

Site Overview

The consortium won the 99-year leasehold Holland Road site through a dual-envelope tender. URA noted the compelling design concept and planning of public realm. The development sits comfortably with the existing Holland Village using the present street block grid.

Public Spaces

Three ground-floor zones: Commons Square (events/markets), Communal Green (courtyard with shops/water feature), and Pocket Park (opens to Lorong Mambong). Open walkways will connect these public spaces to the surrounding areas. Lorong Liput and Lorong Mambong extend into development, creating consistent and distinctive streetscape.

Living Options

Sereen: 1-, 2- and 3-BR units, with many offering unblocked views of the GCB areas to the north.

Leven: 21-unit, low-rise residence that embraces Holland Village's laid back character.

Qunicy Private Residences: spacious 3- and 4- BR units with private lift lobbies and privileged access to well equipped gym, club lounge and concierge service.

Vibrant Mixed-Use Development

Residents of One Holland Village Residences enjoy 13,500 sqm of new retail space for dining, shopping and lifestyle options, connected to the residences via a covered walkway.
