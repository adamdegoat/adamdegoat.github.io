About The Arcady at Boon Keng

The Arcady at Boon Keng is a freehold condo development that emphasises nature-inspired living in an urban setting — an urban oasis garden where every single day is filled with the joys of nature, near the Kallang Park Connector with botanical landscaping and family-oriented facilities.

Welcome & Concept

Discover the beautiful connection with nature at The Arcady at Boon Keng, an urban oasis garden where every single day is filled with the joys of nature. The development positions itself as a nature paradise integrated with architectural design and comprehensive family amenities.

Education & Shopping

The location provides access to multiple reputable primary schools and overseas institutions nearby. Shopping options include malls within a 10-minute drive, offering dining, entertainment, and retail experiences.

Parks & Connectivity

Residents can access the Kallang Park Connector for strolling, jogging, and biking, with connectivity to Whampoa Park Connector toward Novena.

Rooftop & Amenities

Premium facilities include Sky Gym, Arcady Club, Gourmet Vista overlooking the Kallang River, Sky Veranda for private dining, Farming Pots, and Star Gazing Lawn.

Family Facilities

Second-storey amenities feature Kids Club, Kids Party Deck, Karaoke Room, Alfresco Dining, Herb Stroll, playground, and meditation patios.

Unit Features

Each apartment combines form and functionality with branded appliances, quality finishes and fittings.
