About The Continuum

The Continuum represents a rare freehold residential development in District 15, comprising 816 units on a sprawling 263,794 sq ft land parcel acquired for S$815 million. Located at Thiam Siew Avenue between two MRT stations, the project combines Hoi Hup's residential expertise with Sunway's development capabilities to deliver what analysts consider an attractively priced freehold launch.

Location & History

The combined land parcel consists of 22 plots previously occupied by 5 bungalows and 10 pairs of semi-detached houses, originally belonging to hotelier Wee Thiam Siew. The site sits between Tanjong Katong Road and Haig Road in a mature neighborhood. Acquired on 18 November 2021 via public tender by the joint venture, it represents the largest land sale recorded in Singapore for that year.

Accessibility & Amenities

The Continuum enjoys proximity to two MRT stations: Paya Lebar Interchange (EW8/CC9) and Dakota (CC8), both within walking distance. The development is positioned within 1km of three prestigious schools: Kong Hwa, Haig Girls', Tanjong Katong Primary Schools, and Chung Cheng High School (Main). The project benefits from its location near the Paya Lebar Central precinct, selected as one of nine pilot Business Improvement District precincts by the Urban Redevelopment Authority.

Development Specifications

The land carries a residential zoning with a plot ratio of 2.8 per the URA Masterplan 2019. The pricing translates to approximately S$1,488 per square foot per plot ratio after an estimated development charge of about S$284 million. The site's substantial size provides residents with expansive common facilities and green spaces across the freehold property.

Developers Overview

Hoi Hup Realty, established in 1983, has delivered over 7,300 quality homes across private condominiums, landed housing, and mixed-use developments. The company has won over 25 awards, including BCI Asia Top 10 Developers Award (2012, 2013, 2017, 2018). Recent projects include Royal Square at Novena, a 33-storey mixed-use landmark featuring retail, restaurants, medical suites, and Singapore's first Courtyard by Marriott hotel, achieving Green Mark Platinum certification.

Market Positioning

Industry analysts view The Continuum as the biggest freehold condo launch in 2023 offering substantial variety and choices. The project is positioned to appeal to buyers seeking the rare combination of freehold tenure with modern amenities in a well-established, connectivity-rich district.
