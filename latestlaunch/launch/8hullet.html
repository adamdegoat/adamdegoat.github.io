About 8 Hullet

8 Hullet offers a freehold sanctuary within District 9's prestigious Orchard Road area, positioned near Somerset MRT. This 12-storey residential development combines luxury urban living with access to Singapore's renowned shopping, dining, and entertainment district while maintaining private neighbourhood tranquility.

Location & Accessibility

The property sits moments from Somerset MRT station (0.3km away) within Singapore's exclusive shopping belt. Nearby malls include The Centrepoint, Orchard Gateway, 313 @ Somerset, Takashimaya, and Paragon. Emerald Hill's charming conservation streets offer colonial heritage and Peranakan architecture just beyond Orchard Road's modern retail landscape.

The location balances culinary adventure with cultural exploration, from Michelin-starred dining to wine bars and live performance venues along Emerald Hill.

Development & Architecture

Designed with an exquisite arrangement of panelled spacings, the development features 12 floors rising above verdant gardens. Private lift access leads to individually curated apartments with city views spanning from dusk till dawn.

Facilities

Facilities include a swimming pool and children's pool, gymnasium, playground, BBQ area, yoga lawn and meditation lawn, seating areas, guard house and drop-off point.
