About Parc Clematis

The Parc Clematis is located in the former Park West enbloc site, leaving it in the heart of the bustling Clementi center. This mega development condo comprises over 1,400 units designed into four resort-themed quadrants. The development features more than 400,000 sqft of facilities including three 50-meter lap pools and a 100-meter beach pool.

Location & Connectivity

A sheltered walkway leads to Clementi MRT Station with access to major roads including AYE and PIE.

Nan Hua Primary School and Pei Tong Primary School are within 1 km. The development is located near One North R&D Park and the Jurong Lake District, Singapore's second CBD.

Nearby Amenities

Dining options within walking distance include West Coast Drive 502 Market (350m), Jack's Place at West Coast Recreational Complex (260m), McDonald's Restaurant (470m), and Pizza Hut (470m).

Shopping options include NTUC Fairprice (470m), New Ginza Plaza (900m), Hong Leong Shopping Centre (770m), and New Clementi Shopping Centre (600m).

Facilities & Design

The development features resort-inspired landscape with a 100-meter beach-inspired pool, three lap pools, jacuzzis, and therapeutic pools. Clubhouse facilities include gym and yoga studio with pool views.

More than 80% of the land is dedicated to landscaping and facilities, with ground floors positioned at least 20m from the ground for an enhanced sense of space.

Developer Profile

SingHaiyi Group is a fast-growing, diversified real estate company listed on the Mainboard of the Singapore Exchange. Previous projects City Suites and The Vales received CONQUAS STAR ratings from Singapore's Building and Construction Authority.
