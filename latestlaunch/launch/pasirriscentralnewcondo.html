About Pasir Ris 8

The Allgreen Properties and Kerry Properties tie-up that clinched the 99-year leasehold white site next to Pasir Ris MRT station is planning to develop about 480 apartments above three levels of retail space.

Integrated Development Features

The commercial and residential development will be integrated with a bus interchange, a polyclinic and a town plaza, offering seamless connection to public transport services and amenities for residents. The completed development will serve as a community focal point.

Pasir Ris Town Rejuvenation

Pasir Ris Town was selected for HDB's Remaking Our Heartland program. The vision emphasizes creating an Urban Sanctuary – where a Vibrant Community meets Nature, combining beaches, parks, and recreational facilities with vibrant neighborhood transformation and improved connectivity through the future Cross-Island Line.

Transport Connectivity

Residents enjoy seamless connectivity with direct access to a fully air-conditioned integrated transport hub on the ground floor, connecting to retail amenities, a new polyclinic, and community facilities. The future Cross-Island Line will provide direct access to Punggol Digital District in under 15 minutes.

Retail and Community Amenities

The shopping mall features three levels—two above ground and one basement—with basement car parking. Residents benefit from proximity to dining, retail, and the newly upgraded Pasir Ris Hawker Centre offering modern hawker culture.
