About Leedon Green

Leedon Green is a bold architectural interpretation of natural splendour featuring alternating terraces of verdant greenery, water cascades and undulating terrain. The development occupies a prime freehold location in District 10, positioned near Orchard Road, the CBD, and approximately 10 minutes' walk to Farrer Road MRT station.

Location & Accessibility

The project sits at Leedon Heights, providing ultimate accessibility where Orchard Road and the Central Business District are just minutes' drive past the Botanic Gardens (a UNESCO World Heritage Site) and Dempsey Hill. Residents benefit from proximity to top schools along Bukit Timah/Dunearn Road and nearby biotech facilities. The site borders the Pan Island Expressway and Ayer Rajah Expressway, both within three minutes' drive.

Neighborhood Features

Leedon Green is centrally located in the Holland Bukit Timah area, close to Orchard Road and at the doorstep to Holland Village, Dempsey Hill and the Singapore Botanic Gardens. The URA is expanding Holland Village as a community hub, promising future development.

Interior Finishes

Units feature imported marble finishes, Antonio Lupi sanitary wares imported from Italy and fittings from Axor and Hansgrohe from Germany. Kitchens include Ernestomeda cabinetry, VZUG appliances, and Liebherr refrigerators.
