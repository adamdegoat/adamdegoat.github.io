About Sky Eden @ Bedok

Sky Eden @ Bedok is a 99-year leasehold residential development in District 16, featuring ground floor commercial units and positioned mere minutes from Bedok MRT Station. Located within the broader East Coast revitalization master plan, the project offers convenient access to transportation hubs, educational institutions, and recreational amenities.

Strategic Location Of Sky Eden

Sky Eden takes centre-stage in the mature estate of Bedok Central, 3-min walk away from the MRT station and bus interchange. The development benefits from proximity to established networks of markets and hawker centers. Residents enjoy cycling access to East Coast Park within 8 minutes and Bedok Reservoir in 7 minutes via designated cycling corridors. Schools including Red Swastika School and Yu Neng Primary School are within 1km. The location provides connectivity to Paya Lebar Central — the most centrally located business hub outside the core city centre (3 MRT stops away), with Marina Bay Financial Centre approximately 20 minutes' drive distance.

About The Developer - Frasers Property

Frasers Property Singapore is a strategic unit of SGX-listed Frasers Property Limited, managing diverse property portfolios globally. The company owns, develops, and manages residential, retail, office, and business space properties throughout Singapore. Over the years, it has developed over 21,000 quality homes. The developer oversees a portfolio including 13 shopping malls and various office and business park properties, with significant assets held under Frasers Centrepoint Trust and Frasers Logistics & Commercial Trust.

Bedok Overview

Bedok has undergone comprehensive transformation through HDB's Remaking Our Heartland initiative (beginning 2011), featuring dedicated cycling and pedestrian paths connecting activity hubs including Bedok Reservoir Park and Bedok Town Centre. The area includes an Integrated Transport Hub, Bedok Integrated Complex merging community, eldercare, library, medical and sporting facilities. The Heritage Trail highlights significant locations commemorating social and cultural memories. The neighborhood combines mature HDB estates with modern amenities—new cafés, restaurants, and bars complement traditional food and shopping destinations.

Infrastructure & Transportation

Integrated Transport Hubs serve as fully air-conditioned bus interchanges seamlessly linking MRT stations to adjoining commercial developments. The soon-to-be-ready Thomson-East Coast Line running through Bedok South provides residents additional public transport options.
