About The Arden

Location & Connectivity

Bukit Panjang is a mature neighbourhood in District 23. The Arden is only a mere 300m from Phoenix LRT station and a short 8-mins walk to Bukit Panjang MRT Station and bus interchange.

Proximity to amenities includes Junction 10 Mall (7-minute walk), Hillion Mall (11-minute walk), Bukit Panjang Plaza, and schools like Choa Chu Kang Secondary and Pioneer Junior College.

Nearby Shopping Malls

Junction 10: Situated within a mere 7 mins walk from The Arden, features Sheng Shiong Supermarket, enrichment centre, hair salons, a gym, a clinic, and many eateries.

Hillion Mall: Close by at 11 minutes walk away is the new addition to the neighborhood, mainly focused on restaurants and eateries, NTUC Fairprice are located here.

Teck Whye Shopping Centre: Features various products and services under one roof, retail shops and supermarkets, eating outlets.

Bukit Panjang Plaza: One of the biggest malls in the area, wide variety of goods and services, NTUC Fairprice and Fairprice Finest.

Future Developments

With Jurong Lake District shaping up to be the 2nd CBD in Singapore, and Jurong Innovation District spearheading cutting-edge manufacturing, you can now stay close to myriad business opportunities.

Additional transformations include Tengah Forest Town, Jurong Region Line, Rail Corridor, Sungei Kadut Eco-District.

Amenities

From the moment you enter The Arden, you can bask in serenity with the sounds of rippling water and rustling leaves all around you.

Facilities include swimming pool, futsal court, sky running track, function room, kid's bubble pool, and kid's playground.

Design Features

Rare 3.2m Height from Floor To Ceiling at The Arden.

Unique CoSpace Concept for 3+Study & 4+Study. All blocks oriented North South facing. All blocks storey height of 3.5m, atop 7.4M Ground Elevation.

Features include premium Bosch appliances, branded bathroom fittings from hansgrohe and sanitary ware from Roca, and one-touch convenience with an interconnected network of home automation, security features.

CoSpace Concept

Open the door to new possibilities with CoSpace™, a proprietary Qingjian Realty feature. Transform space according to your needs, spaces designed to complement every stage of your life.

Three components: CoSpace™ Efficiency maximises your space to the fullest; CoSpace™ Flexibility lets you create spaces; CoSpace™ Interactivity allows you to enjoy spaces.

Key Selling Points

Well-designed new home in an inclusive landed enclave neighborhood. Rare boutique development on tranquil & elevated land beside low density exclusive 3-storey semi-detached landed estate. Comes with SMART Home Features. All blocks storey height of 3.5m, atop 7.4M Ground Elevation from Choa Chu Kang Road. First mover advantage - very low new residential supply in 2022. Better rail connectivity – New track extension from Bukit Panjang MRT to Sungei Kadut MRT Interchange by mid 2030's. Close to Bukit Timah Nature Trail Walk and Bukit Timah Nature Reserve.
