About Perfect Ten

Perfect Ten is an ultra-luxury freehold residential development in District 10 designed by acclaimed DP Architects. The project offers spacious living with strategic positioning near major amenities, educational institutions, and nature reserves.

About the Development

Perfect Ten represents a modern interpretation of District 10 living. The developer, Japura Development Pte Ltd, is linked to Hong Kong billionaire Li Ka-Shing and has an extensive portfolio of prestigious projects. The 24-storey residential building emphasizes luxury living and ample space, with completion expected in 2025.

Location & Connectivity

The property is strategically located along Bukit Timah Road with excellent transportation access. Two nearby MRT stations—Newton and Stevens—provide convenient connectivity. The development is close to major shopping destinations including Orchard Road and Novena, while remaining near Singapore Botanic Gardens and Bukit Timah Nature Reserve.

Nearby Schools

The area offers numerous educational institutions: Singapore Chinese Girls' School, Anglo Chinese School, Raffles Girls School, Catholic Junior College, St. Joseph's Institution, and Chatsworth International School.
