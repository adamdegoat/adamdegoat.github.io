About Coco Palms

Just 5-minute walk to Pasir Ris MRT, Coco Palms is a haven where the comforts of home seamlessly combine with the serene ambience of living in a luxury resort. Conveniently located within minutes walk to Pasir Ris MRT and White Sands, it is a prestigious new condo offering 12 residential blocks with 944 units in a verdant and beautiful resort style landscaped setting.
