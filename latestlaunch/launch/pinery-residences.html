About Pinery Residences

Pinery Residences is a new launch in Singapore's East, at Tampines Street 94 in District 18, developed by Hoi Hup Realty and Sunway. With launch timed around March 2026, it's one of the more anticipated suburban projects of the year.

Location and connectivity

Set in the established Tampines-Pasir Ris corridor, the site sits within easy reach of Tampines' regional centre, one of the best-served town hubs outside the city, with three MRT stations across the East-West and Downtown lines, malls, the regional library and the Tampines Hub.

For families, the East is well covered for schools, parks and the upcoming amenities planned for the area, while the expressway network (PIE, TPE and the future cross-island links) keeps the rest of the island within reach.

The development

The unit mix is designed around space-efficient family layouts, 2-bedroom-with-study, 3-bedroom-with-study and 4-bedroom-with-study configurations, so the project leans towards owner-occupier households rather than small investor units.

Hoi Hup and Sunway are an experienced partnership with a long track record of Singapore residential projects, which tends to reassure buyers on build quality and timely completion.
