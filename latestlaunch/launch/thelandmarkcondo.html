About The Landmark

The Landmark is a 39-storey single tower residential development located in prime central District 3. The Landmark condo sits on the other side of Pearl's Hill City Park, allowing residents to enjoy peaceful moments away from the city hustle in the city. The development comprises only 396 units with luxury finishes and five decks of lifestyle amenities spanning multiple levels.

Location & Connectivity

The development occupies an elevated position on Pearl's Hill, offering 360-degree unblocked views of the city skyline, Singapore River, CBD, and Sentosa Island. It is approximately 7 minutes' walk to Outram Park MRT (triple-line interchange with EWL, NEL, and TEL) and Chinatown MRT station.

Key connectivity includes proximity to Central Expressway (CTE), Ayer Rajah Expressway (AYE), East Coast Parkway (ECP), and Marina Coastal Expressway (MCE). The CBD is accessible within 10 minutes by car.

Surrounding Amenities

The Landmark is surrounded by shopping centres including Chinatown Point, People's Park Complex, UE Square, Liang Court, and The Central. Dining options include Hong Lim Food Centre, People's Park Food Complex, and Tiong Bahru Food Complex.

Entertainment venues nearby include Robertson Quay, Clarke Quay, and Boat Quay. The area features heritage attractions, hipster cafes, and cultural treasures throughout Chinatown.

Education & Healthcare

Many good schools near The Landmark condo, e.g. River Valley Primary School, Outram Secondary School, Duke-NUS Medical School & SMU. The development is approximately 1km from River Valley Primary School.

Singapore General Hospital (SGH) Medical Hub is in close proximity. Prime Minister Lee Hsien Loong unveiled extensive redevelopment and expansion plans for Singapore General Hospital (SGH) Campus which will span the next two decades.

Facilities

A huge number of facilities for the enjoyment of residents, total 60 facilities at The Landmark. The development features five decks of holistic lifestyle facilities.

Level 2, Clubhouse: Features a main lobby with a towering water feature overlooking Pearl's Hill City Park, with access to the 50-metre Infinity Pool, Children's Pool, and Paddling Pool.

Level 14, Fitness Deck: Includes gym facilities, yoga spaces, treadmills, Jacuzzi Aqua Spa, and a Rejuvenation Deck with views of the downtown skyline.

Level 34, Social Deck: Features The Social (lounge area), The Dinette, and The Hashery, offering views of River Valley, the Park, and city skyline.

Geographical USPs

Seven key geographical unique selling points: city views above the rest; City Centre Fringe location; integrated transport network (CTE/ECP); crossroad of 4 MRT lines (DT/NE/EW/TEL); high density of amenities; elevated on Pearl's Hill; proximity to vibrant Singapore River.
