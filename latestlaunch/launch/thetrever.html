About The Tre Ver

A 99-year leasehold development located at Potong Pasir Ave 1, comprising 729 units on the former Raintree Gardens site. It will be designed by the award-winning architect WOHA.

Overview

Jointly developed by UOL and SingLand, the project comprises units ranging from 484 to 1,378 sq ft. 80 percent of the units enjoy either a river view or swimming pool view, while the remaining units overlook Woodleigh Park and the upcoming Bidadari estate.

Location & Connectivity

The development is located near Potong Pasir MRT station with access via Pan-Island, Central, and Kallang-Paya Lebar Expressways. It benefits from the Bartley-Tampines viaduct and Kallang Park Connector. The area is surrounded by malls and prestigious schools, with planned amenities at nearby Bidadari.

Unit Quality

2-room and 1-room units are incredibly lavish and spacious, exceeding typical market standards for smaller apartments.
