About One Marina Gardens

One Marina Gardens is the inaugural residential development on Marina Gardens Lane, positioned adjacent to Gardens by the Bay in Singapore's Marina South precinct. Featuring two towers with sweeping views of Marina Bay, the coastline, and Downtown Core, it represents a luxury waterfront living concept blending urban sophistication with natural surroundings.

Gardens in the Sky, Beacon on the Marina

One Marina Gardens anchors Marina South's dynamic community, offering a prime vantage point that connects residents to future essential amenities just a 10-minute walk away.

Unmatched Connectivity & Prestige

The development sits within Singapore's Central Area, providing access to the financial district, cultural attractions, shopping hubs, and world-class dining. Residents benefit from proximity to Marina South Pier and Marina Bay Cruise Centre, with sweeping vistas of Marina South's coastline and convenient walking distance to Gardens by the Bay.

Marina South Urban Design & Amenities

The Marina South master plan envisions a 45-hectare coastal enclave with expansive public spaces and recreational facilities. The 10-minute neighbourhood framework incorporates amenities at the doorstep such as fresh food stores, convenience shops, bakeries, medical clinics, and student care centres; community uses including childcare centres and multi-generational facilities; age-inclusive sports and play spaces; and community spaces such as a pedestrian mall, neighbourhood streets, plazas, an underground pedestrian link to the future Marina South MRT Station, and an elevated landscaped bridge.

A wellness hub focused on physical, emotional, and mental well-being is planned for the Marina South Coastal waterfront site, coordinated by the Singapore Tourism Board.

Waterfront Living & Natural Integration

The precinct merges leisure with indulgence across dining, shopping, entertainment, and cultural experiences. The development emphasizes living within nature rather than merely beside it, with greenery an integral part of daily life throughout the master plan.

Luxury Residences & Design

The taller tower reaches 45 stories, offering a serene retreat above the city with breathtaking views and refined comforts. Interior features include floor-to-ceiling windows in contemporary bedrooms, high-end fixtures and refined finishes, large outdoor spaces for year-round private use, open-plan layouts maximizing natural light, and extra-large windows framing Marina South or Gardens by the Bay views. Thoughtful design incorporates fresh air and lush surroundings suitable for all ages.

About Kingsford Group

The Kingsford Group operates across China, Singapore, and Australia. Singapore projects include Kingsford Hillview Peak, Kingsford Waterbay, and Normanton Park. Current developments comprise The Hill @ One-North (142 units), Chuan Park (916 units), and One Marina Gardens (937 units).
