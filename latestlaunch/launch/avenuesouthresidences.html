About Avenue South Residences

Avenue South Residence will be the 1st residential development in Singapore's Newest District and have unrivalled views of Greater Southern Waterfront, located at Silat Avenue that is just 2 kilometres away from the Central Business District in Singapore. The project comprises two 56-storey towers with 1,074 residential units positioned minutes from future Cantonment MRT Station and between Tiong Bahru MRT and Outram Park Interchange.

56-Storey Towers on the Southern Tip of Singapore

Development features 2 56-storey towers with 1,074 residential units across a 245,972 sqft site. The location offers 43 Hectare Medical Gateway and Major Transportation Hub, all Within Reach — located next to the CBD, close to Sentosa, Integrated Resort and Downtown Orchard Road.

Greater Southern Waterfront Development

Development begins in 5–10 years. The 2,000-hectare Greater Southern Waterfront stretches from Pasir Panjang to Marina East, envisioned as a gateway for live, work, play.

Approximately 1,000 hectares will be freed for development after port terminals relocate to Tuas.

Green Rail Corridor

The rejuvenated 24 km Green Rail Corridor runs from Woodlands to Tanjong Pagar along the former railway line. The central section offers shared space for Biodiversity and Greenery, Heritage and Culture and recreational activities.

Nature and Views

Units on higher floors enjoy sea views. Avenue South Residences allows its residents to stay close to nature with Mount Faber only a few minutes walk away as well as Telok Blangah Park and the Southern Ridges.
