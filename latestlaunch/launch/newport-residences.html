About Newport Residences

Newport Residences is an upcoming freehold condo in Singapore's busy Central Business District (CBD) of Tanjong Pagar developed by the award winning City Developments Ltd (CDL). The integrated luxury development combines a shopping mall (Newport Plaza) and a grade A office tower (Newport Tower), with residences occupying Levels 23 to Level 45.

Overview

Newport Residences presents a rare opportunity for homeowners and investors to acquire a brand new luxury condo project in a highly sought-after Tanjong Pagar area. City Developments Ltd is expected to tear down the former Fuji Xerox Towers and build a 51-storey mixed condo development building with expansive commercial space, breathtaking residential - Newport Residences, and grade A office - Newport Tower.

The development will have a gross floor area (GFA) of 655,000 square feet, equivalent to 60,851 square meters (sqm), 25% more GFA than the current office property on the site.

Location & Connectivity

Newport Residences located in the much coveted prime district of Tanjong Pagar in District 02 of Singapore is an area rich in culture and history. The neighborhood combines diverse lifestyles for singles, couples, old and young.

The spectacular Newport Residences residents will enjoy the well-established private and public transport system in the vicinity. The major Anson Road and Keppel Road allow motorists to access various amenities around the neighbourhood quickly. Also, several MRT stations and bus stations are conveniently set near the Newport Residences.

Within 5 mins walk from Newport Residences is the Tanjong Pagar MRT station which is connected to the grade A office by Guocoland - Guoco Tower making it a very convenient starting point for exploration of Tanjong Pagar district with a great selection of cafes and restaurants to whet your palates.

Education & Schools

Residents of Newport Residences enjoy close proximity to shopping malls such as 100 AM, Tanjong Pagar Plaza, Icon Village, and Raffle City Singapore.

Besides the smooth commute, Newport Residences residents that are family-oriented will also have the privilege to take their kids to some of the best schools in the neighbourhood. Most elite education centres are just a hop, skip and jump away from the vibrant Newport Residences.

Neighborhood Character & Dining

Tanjong Pagar has now become essentially an extension of Singapore's CBD with many offices now located in this part of town. The highlight of the area is the great selection of bars and restaurants in the old shophouses.

The area features local dining options, heritage sites, and cultural attractions including Jinrikisha Station which was built in 1903 and various restored shophouses with restaurants and hotels.

Unique Selling Points

Designed by renowned Japanese architecture firm Nikken Sekkei Ltd.

Spectacular views of the city and the sea.

Rare freehold residences from Level 23 to Level 45 (Super Penthouse level).

Provides Premier Residential Services.

Each residence is efficient with spacious layout and fitted with smart home features, designer kitchen appliances such as V-Zug and De Dietrich.

Features 4 lifestyle gardens (Level 25 – Play Garden, Level 29 – Wellness Garden, Level 37 – Fitness Garden, Level 41 – Horizon Garden) and 2 levels of lifestyle facilities.

Singapore's first private residence as well as the first mixed development comprising of serviced apartments, office and restaurants (F&B) to attain the prestigious BCA Green Mark Platinum Super Low Energy Awards.

Proximity to Tanjong Pagar MRT station (East-West line), upcoming Prince Edward Road MRT station (Circle line) and Cantonment MRT station (Circle line).
