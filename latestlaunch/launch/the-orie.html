About The Orie

Project Overview

The Orie presents an exceptional blend of contemporary elegance and rich local heritage. Designed with families in mind, the development comprises two majestic towers offering units from studios with studies through to five-bedroom residences with private lifts. The project draws inspiration from the Japanese words ORI (origami/fold) and IE (home).

Location & Connectivity

Positioned in Toa Payoh's heart, The Orie sits just 330 meters from Braddell MRT Station with proximity to Toa Payoh MRT and Bus Interchange within 816 meters. The location provides easy access to the Central Business District and Orchard Road shopping belt plus connections to major expressways (PIE, CTE, KPE) and the upcoming North-South Corridor.

Neighborhood Amenities

Toa Payoh functions as a hub of activity, offering malls, markets, and hawker centers. Residents access Junction 8 shopping mall, Toa Payoh Town Centre, SAFRA facilities, and MacRitchie Reservoir. The area features iconic hawker dishes and traditional coffee shops alongside modern conveniences.

Education & Healthcare

Schools within reach include Pei Chun Public School, CHIJ Primary/Secondary, Kheng Cheng School, and Raffles Girls' School (Secondary). Healthcare facilities encompass Toa Payoh Polyclinic, Tan Tock Seng Hospital, Mount Alvernia, Mount Elizabeth Novena, and Thomson Medical Centre.

Heritage & Community

As Singapore's first satellite town planned by the Housing & Development Board, Toa Payoh stands out as a vibrant, well-rounded neighborhood where tradition meets progress. The iconic Dragon Playground and Toa Payoh Town Park reflect its heritage, while the area maintains a welcoming and tight-knit atmosphere.

Building Features & Sustainability

The development achieves the Building and Construction Authority (BCA)'s Green Mark Platinum Super Low Energy (SLE) certification with 100% landscape replacement. The project features efficient floorplans without air-conditioning ledges, classified as a harmonised condo design.

Amenities & Services

A range of amenities at The Orie include a refreshing swimming pool, a well-equipped gym, a tennis court, and dedicated spaces like a yoga corner and meditation deck, along with BBQ pits for social gatherings. Over 40 facilities support residents' wellness and recreational needs.

Unit Specifications & Smart Home

All apartments include quality fittings by Hansgrohe, Duravit bathroom wares, and premium appliances by De Dietrich and Samsung. Smart features encompass a digital lockset, smart camera for remote surveillance, smart home system gateway, and smart air conditioning and lighting controls.

Residential Services

The project offers Residential Services for their day-to-day needs such as parcel collection, arrangements for laundry and transport bookings or even special occasions like party catering and restaurant reservations.

Unique Selling Points

Key differentiators include eight years of pent-up demand in Toa Payoh, joint development by three reputable builders, award-winning contractor Woh Hup, comprehensive facilities, efficient layouts, Green Mark Platinum certification, a five-minute walk to MRT, proximity to quality schools, and access to major expressways.
