About Grandeur Park Residences

Grandeur Park Residences is an upcoming new launch condominium located along New Upper Changi Road / Bedok South Avenue 3 (Parcel B). Developed by renowned developers CEL Development (Chip Eng Seng Corporation), Grandeur Park Residences offers 720 luxurious residential units.

Location Highlights

A 1 minute covered linkway walk connects you directly to the Tanah Merah MRT Station taking you downtown, Changi Business Park or Changi Airport.

Driving is always convenient with swift access to the East Coast Parkway (ECP) and Pan-Island Expressway (PIE).

Smart Home Features

Project includes wifi pan/tilt camera with motion detection, night vision, Yale Biometric Digital Lockset, cloud storage, and 2-way audio connectivity.
