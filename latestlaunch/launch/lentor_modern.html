About Lentor Modern

Lentor Modern is an integrated mixed-use residential development at Lentor Hill Estate featuring direct access to a shopping mall and the new Lentor MRT station. The project brings together transit-oriented design with sustainability-focused urban planning in Northern Singapore's Lentor neighbourhood.

Location & Connectivity

Lentor Modern sits near Florissa Park along Lentor Drive and Yio Chu Kang Road, positioned between Ang Mo Kio, Thomson, Yishun and Sin Ming. Fast and convenient commuting to City area and various parts of Singapore is enabled through the integrated Lentor MRT station. Thomson Plaza and Ang Mo Kio Hub serve nearby shopping and dining needs, with additional eateries along Sembawang Road shophouses.

Design & Community Philosophy

The development embraces warm tones and a rustic forested garden in a resort-like urban sanctuary. Guocoland's approach encourages communities of young families and working professionals to flourish together through resource-efficient design and community-minded urban planning. The integrated layout positions home amenities and common areas as extensions of residential life.

Sustainability & Green Features

Innovative green features optimise the management of the entire residential ecosystem, from energy to water and waste, greenery to lighting and cooling systems. This reflects Guocoland's commitment to safeguarding ecological conditions while supporting modern urban living standards.

Amenities

Facilities include infinity and lap pools, fitness/yoga studios, gym and spa, clubhouse, sundeck, pool bar, BBQ areas, dining pavilion, sky garden, leisure garden, functional rooms, reading cove, and walking paths.
