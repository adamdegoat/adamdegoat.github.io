About Parc Komo

Parc Komo is Singapore's first Integrated Resort Living nestled within the perennial charm of rustic Changi. This freehold mixed development by CEL offers Nature, Life & Community through scenic landscaping and 276 meticulously designed low-rise units across 10 blocks of 5 storeys, complemented by an integrated boutique mall.

Location & Connectivity

Located within walking distance to the upcoming Loyang station, residents benefit from the North-East Line expansion. The development sits along Jalan Mariam in District 17, offering proximity to Changi Business Park, Changi Airport, and major expressways (ECP, PIE, TPE). A short drive away places residents near Jewel Changi Airport with over 280 shops and dining outlets.

Lifestyle & Amenities

The development features over 60 facilities including a 50-meter lap pool, leisure pool, Komo Club and Gym with panoramic vistas, Sunset Deck, Night Water Lily Pond, Pool Party Pavilion with jacuzzi, Herb Pavilion, and urban farming plots. The fully-equipped barbecue pavilion and intimate Lap Pool evoke nature-inspired relaxation.

Mixed-Use Development

Parc Komo includes Komo Shoppes - An Integrated Boutique Mall with holistic offerings providing dining, shopping, and daily necessities. Parking comprises 276 residential lots and 24 commercial lots with accessible spaces.

Neighborhood Character

The area features rustic charm and slower pace of life with hundred-year-old trees and kampong-style boardwalks. Nearby attractions include Changi Point's coastal boardwalk, nature parks, country clubs, and water-themed activity centers. Changi Village Hawker Centre offers local delights alongside cafes and microbreweries.

Education & Employment

The neighborhood provides good schools including international and tertiary institutions. Employment opportunities exist through nearby Changi Business Park, Changi International Logispark, Changi Aviation Park, and the upcoming Changi East Industrial Zone.

Design Philosophy

Units are meticulously designed so as to open up to skies allowing nature to flow through. The development emphasizes komorebi, sunlight streaming through trees, with cool stone path, water features, and a vibrant tapestry of flowers and foliage.

Why Buy Parc Komo

Likely the most affordable brand new freehold development. Rare mixed development offering an estimated 3000 sqm of commercial space. Exclusive and private living with low density surrounded by landed houses. Resort styled living with extensive facilities and lush landscaping. Upcoming Loyang MRT accessibility. Close proximity to Jewel and Changi Airport.
