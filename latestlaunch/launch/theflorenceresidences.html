About The Florence Residences

The Florence Residences offers a distinctive "Club-Condo" living concept with 1,410 units across 9 towers in District 19. Located between Kovan and Hougang MRT stations on Hougang Avenue 2, the development uniquely features 12 clubs and 128 amenities, reputedly the first condo in Singapore to offer such extensive recreational facilities.

Location & Connectivity

The project sits "between 2 MRT stations, Kovan MRT & Hougang MRT Station" with easy access to the Town Centre. Residents enjoy "daily conveniences such as shopping, dining and leisure needs from nearby malls within the vicinity."

Facilities & Amenities

The development boasts "128 indoor and outdoor facilities," including a 2-storey clubhouse with dining and multi-purpose suites overlooking an "80 metres Long Island Lap Pool," an amphitheatre with outdoor movie screening, gym, outdoor fitness corner, and a sparring ring pavilion.

Unit Layouts

Floorplans range from "474 - 603" sq ft for one-bedrooms up to five-bedroom units spanning "1,668 - 1,916" sq ft, offering "an ideal Florence Residence Floor Plan to suit every need."
