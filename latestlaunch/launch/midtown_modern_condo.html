About Midtown Modern

Midtown Modern is a luxury residence by Guocoland, sitting right on top of Bugis MRT interchange. The development features two 30-storey towers with over 500 apartment units in a forest garden setting and a retail podium. Located in the Central Business District, it offers seamless connectivity to the whole of Singapore.

Location & Connectivity

Directly linked to amenities such as Bugis MRT, Bugis Junction, Duo Galleria, the upcoming Guoco Midtown, as well as Suntec City, the project benefits from large green spaces and a wide range of recreational facilities not usually found in Central Business District projects. Residents have unparalleled connectivity to two MRT lines – the East-West and Downtown Lines.

Design & Amenities

The development spans a huge site area of over 124,000sqft, which is rather rare for a residential development in prime Central Business District. Designed by ipli Architects, which also designed Martin Modern for Guocoland, it follows the same designed concept – a botanic garden right at your home. The project includes 20 thematic gardens and landscaped public spaces spread across Guoco Midtown and Midtown II.

Surrounding Development

Within the larger Guoco Midtown mixed-use development, residents enjoy 770,000 sq ft allocated for Grade A office spaces, 80,000 sq ft business and lifestyle Network Hub, and three retail villages spanning some 50,000 sq ft. The development will connect the three office micromarkets of City Hall, Marina Centre and Bugis.

Transportation & Accessibility

Served by 4 MRT lines and Nicoll Highway, the location offers great connectivity to 3 major expressways in Singapore, East Coast Parkway (ECP), Marine Coastal Expressway (MCE) and Central Expressway (CTE). The future North-South Corridor, which runs adjacent to Midtown Modern, will feature cycling tracks, pedestrian paths and dedicated bus lanes.

Educational Proximity

Anglo-Chinese School is just three stops away at Newton, while Singapore Chinese Girls' School and St Joseph's Institution are four stops away at Stevens MRT. The East-West Line provides additional educational options including Anglo-Chinese Junior College and Singapore Polytechnic.
