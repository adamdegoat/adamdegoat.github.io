About Gramercy Park

This exclusive freehold development in prime District 10 comprises 174 luxury apartments situated among embassies and good-class bungalows, just a short walk from Orchard Road. The project offers residents absolute privacy with spacious layouts and high-end finishes throughout.

Location & Accessibility

Positioned at 57 Grange Road within the Orchard/Tanglin corridor, the development is a 5-minute walk to Orchard Boulevard MRT Station on the Thomson-East Coast Line. The location provides proximity to prestigious schools including Raffles Girls' Secondary School, Anglo Chinese School, and International Schools, plus country clubs and hospitals like Mount Elizabeth and Gleneagles.

Design & Amenities

Architectural Design firm NBBJ created a luxurious quality residential development designed with sustainability and efficiency in mind. The expansive 170,000 sq ft estate features a grand lawn of more than 11,000 sqft and comprehensive condominium facilities including concierge service and private lifts.

Unit Finishes

Each suite features fine marble for the living and dining areas, and American Walnut timber for the bedrooms. Master bathrooms showcase designer collections from Axor by Hansgrohe and Geberit, while kitchens include top-of-the-line appliances from Gaggenau with wine chillers in select units.
