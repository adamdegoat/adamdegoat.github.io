About Fyve Derbyshire

Fyve Derbyshire is a beautiful freehold residential development within close proximity to Novena MRT, comprising 71 exclusive residences of uncompromising quality across 3 blocks of 5 storeys. The project is designed as a private yet centrally located freehold urban sanctuary with a city address, thoughtfully crafted to appeal to the five human senses of sight, sound, smell, taste and touch.

Location Highlights

7-minute drive to Health City Novena. Within 1 km of St Joseph's Institution Junior, Anglo-Chinese School (Junior), and Farrer Park Primary School. Short walking distance to United Square Shopping Mall and Velocity @ Novena Square. Close to Novena MRT and adjacent to Newton MRT.

Developer Background

Developed by established Roxy Pacific Holdings (SGX Mainboard listed since 2008). The site was acquired at $1,390 psf ppr in December 2017 via collective sale of the former 20-unit Derby Court for $73.88 million. Design architect: KYOOB Architects Pte Ltd; Main contractor: Seah Construction Pte Ltd.
