About Jui Residences

The former National Aerated Water Company site along Serangoon Road offers verdant surrounds and an enclave of calmness near Potong Pasir MRT. The historic mid-1950s art-deco industrial building represented Singapore's industrial past and post-war modern architecture.

The Past / The Future

The historic National Aerated Water Company building housed iconic soft drinks production including Sinalco, Kickapoo Joy Juice, Green Spot, and Royal Crown Cola. The site represents Kallang's intertwined industrial history including brickmaking and sawmills.

Location & Connectivity

Situated between Boon Keng and Potong Pasir MRT on the North East Line. The two-storey property adjoins Kallang River. Nearby amenities include NEX shopping mall, Chomp Chomp food centre, Singapore Sports Hub, Kallang Basin Swimming Complex, and schools like St Andrew's Junior College.

Lifestyle

Residents enjoy a thriving lifestyle at the city fringe area with convenient access to CBD and Marina Bay.
