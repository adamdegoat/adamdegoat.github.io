About Parktown Residence

Parktown Residence represents Singapore's first fully integrated mixed-use development in Tampines, merging residential apartments with a retail mall and transport interchange featuring direct MRT and bus connections, positioning it as a comprehensive urban living solution within a mature, well-connected estate.

Welcome to Parktown Residence

The development is described as the largest integrated condo development with transport hub in a matured estate, offering a luxury living experience with varied unit sizes across five bedroom categories.

Why Tampines is Appealing

The page highlights Tampines as vibrant and well-developed.

Connectivity: Well-served by Tampines MRT (East-West & Downtown Lines) and Tampines West & East MRT stations, with proximity to major expressways (TPE, PIE, ECP).

Shopping & Dining: Home to major malls including Tampines Mall, Century Square, and Tampines 1, plus Giant, IKEA, and Courts Mega Stores.

Green Spaces: Features include Tampines Eco Green and Bedok Reservoir Park with dedicated cycling paths.

Education: Top schools such as St. Hilda's Primary & Secondary, Temasek Polytechnic, and United World College (UWCSEA).

Healthcare & Amenities: Tampines Hub (OTH) - Singapore's largest integrated community hub - plus Changi General Hospital and polyclinic services.

Key Development Features

The integrated design combines residential, retail, and transport infrastructure. A direct link to the future Tampines North MRT Station on the Cross Island Line is highlighted as particularly advantageous. The page notes that integrated design (transport + retail + residential) tends to hold or appreciate value better.

Nearby Amenities

Residents have access to shopping, dining and leisure options throughout Tampines.
