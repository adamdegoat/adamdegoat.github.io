About Parc Esta

Parc Esta is an upcoming new condo formerly Eunosville HUDC, located 3 mins walk to Eunos MRT. Located along Sims Avenue on the former Eunosville site, this 99-year leasehold project features 1,399 residential units and five shops spread across nine 18-storey blocks.

Location & Accessibility

Parc Esta sits between CBD, East Coast Park, and Changi Airport. Eunos MRT station is less than 200m away. Pan Island Expressway and East Coast Parkway provide major highway access. Katong Road eateries are 2 minutes' drive; Parkway Parade shopping mall and medical suite are nearby. CBD is 15 minutes away.

Nearby Amenities

Geylang Serai Market and Food Centre, Paya Lebar Square, One KM Mall, and upcoming Paya Lebar Quarters are positioned nearby. Schools include Haig Girls' School, CHIJ (Katong) Primary, and Tao Nan School.

Project Features

Complete and unique amenities, including a guard house, club house, function room, indoor gym, tennis court, olympic-sized (50m) swimming pool, sun deck, BBQ pits and children's playground. Additional facilities include SMART home, kids playground, tatami room, infinity pool, and cabanas.

Development Background

MCL Land acquired the former Eunosville HUDC estate through collective sale exceeding $750 million in late 2017. Former residents received a 19% increase above their demanding price.
