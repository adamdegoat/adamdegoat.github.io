About The Chuan Park

Chuan Park is an upcoming mega condo project offering 916 units of private residential units located at Serangoon precinct, Lorong Chuan Road (District 19), developed by a joint venture between Chinese developers Kingsford Huray Development. Nestled amidst the heart of matured Serangoon estate right beside Lorong Chuan MRT (Circle Line), this new-concept private residential development is expected to offer an unparalleled living experience, combining modern elegance with sophisticated design.

Location & Connectivity

Conveniently located next to the Lorong Chuan MRT station along the Circle Line, Chuan Park comprises an infinity swimming pool, lap pool, gymnasium, yoga lawn, spa, clubhouse, sundeck, pool bar, BBQ pits, dining pavilion, sky garden, leisure garden, reading cove, walking path and many more facilities to suit different lifestyles and recreational needs.

The development sits in mature Serangoon with proximity to NEX shopping mall, Junction 8, Serangoon Bus Interchange, and nearby schools including St. Gabriel's Primary, Yangzheng Primary, Australian International School, and Nanyang Junior College.

Amenities

Includes infinity and lap pools, gymnasium, yoga lawn, spa, clubhouse, sundeck, pool bar, BBQ pits, dining pavilion, sky garden, leisure garden, and walking paths.

Nearby Shopping

NTP+ shopping mall at New Tech Park (Lorong Chuan) features 43,000 sq ft with 26 retail/F&B units including Collin's, Wine Connection, Dutch Colony Coffee, Anytime Fitness, and food court.

Architecture

AGA Architects (established 1979) designed the project.

Developer Profile

Kingsford incorporated its Singapore subsidiary in 2011 with operations across China, Australia, and Singapore. Notable projects include Kingsford Hillview Peak, Kingsford Waterbay, Normanton Park, and The Hill at One North.
