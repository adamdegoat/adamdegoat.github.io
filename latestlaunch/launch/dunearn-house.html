About Dunearn House

A pioneering residential development marking the first private housing launch within Singapore's expansive Turf City masterplan precinct. The 380-unit collection distinguishes itself through non-PPVC construction enabling post-TOP customization, award-winning design by Ong & Ong, and positioning as an early-entry asset within a government-led transformation.

The true pioneering gateway project of the massive 15,000-home Turf City Masterplan

The development represents the inaugural residential offering in what promises to be a transformational precinct spanning 15,000 homes.

Location & Educational Positioning

Dunearn House is positioned squarely on Singapore's most coveted educational belt, with proximity to Methodist Girls' School, Nanyang Primary, Raffles Girls' Primary, and Hwa Chong Institution.

Transit Connectivity

A 5-minute walk to Sixth Avenue MRT (Downtown Line) provides CBD access in under 30 minutes. The future Cross Island Line with a dedicated Turf City MRT station will place a second major train transit line practically at residents' doorstep.

Construction & Design Features

Non-PPVC construction allows full customization of internal walls and layout after TOP, with highly functional floor plans. Select high-rise stacks reach 19 storeys offering unblocked views directly over surrounding Good Class Bungalow enclaves and Turf City Nature Park.
