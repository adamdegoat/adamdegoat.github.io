About The Collective at One Sophia

The Collective at One Sophia is a mixed-use integrated development at the former Peace Centre site in Bencoolen, combining exclusive residential and commercial spaces for the modern cosmopolitan and offering a lifestyle of sophistication and convenience near Orchard Road and the city's business hub.

Location & Proximity

The development sits at the epicenter of a rapidly growing global business and financial hub.

Residents enjoy close proximity to a variety of premier shopping destinations along Orchard Road, including ION Orchard, Orchard Central, and Plaza Singapura. An integrated retail podium within the development adds further convenience, offering residents a seamless blend of lifestyle and accessibility.

Mixed-Use Development

The property offers exclusive residential and commercial spaces for the modern cosmopolitan. The integrated approach creates a truly connected living experience, where modern comforts meet unparalleled convenience.

Design & Amenities

Every unit is a sanctuary of elegance, designed with high-end finishes and innovative features to meet the demands of modern living.

The development features thoughtfully designed spaces, premium finishes, and exceptional attention to detail that redefine modern living, with spacious interiors crafted for comfort and high-quality materials that exude elegance.

Lifestyle & Community

The location promises everything from business opportunities to dynamic cultural experiences. It is more than residences — it's an investment in your future, where quality meets sophistication and every detail enhances your everyday life.
