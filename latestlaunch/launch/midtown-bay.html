About Midtown Bay

Midtown Bay is the residential component of Guoco Midtown, a landmark mixed-use development by GuocoLand that rejuvenates the Beach Road district with premium Grade A office space integrated into its retail and living space. The residential tower offers 219 luxury units directly connected underground to Bugis MRT interchange.

Guoco Midtown: Transforming Beach Road

In world cities like New York and Tokyo, Midtown is the area where once older neighbourhoods have been transformed with great success to form a vibrant part of the city centre, with a high concentration of trendy residences, prime corporate offices, shops as well as fashionable dining and entertainment venues.

Guoco Midtown represents a comprehensive urban rejuvenation strategy. Following the success of Guoco Tower in Tanjong Pagar, GuocoLand developed this 950,600 sq ft mixed-use development comprising one 30-storey office tower with 770,000 sq ft of premium Grade-A office space, a Network Hub spanning 80,000 sq ft, over 30,000 sq ft of retail and entertainment space, 170,000 sq ft of landscaped public spaces, and Midtown Bay luxury residences.

Connectivity & Location

Guoco Midtown provides convenient and direct access to 4 MRT stations - City Hall Interchange, Promenade Interchange, Esplanade and a sheltered air-conditioned underground connection to Bugis Interchange. Located within the Downtown Core Planning Area at the intersection of Beach Road and Ophir-Rochor Road, it links up 3 micromarkets: City Hall, Marina Centre & Bugis.

The district has established itself as vibrant, hosting global and national level events such as the Singapore Grand Prix and National Day Parade, making it an iconic and trendy area to live, work and entertain.

Placemaking & Public Spaces

Rather than a single mega-structure, Guoco Midtown comprises ten different buildings of varying heights: from one and two-storey retail pavilions, a five-storey network office building, to a 22-storey office tower and a striking 26-storey residential tower.

The development features ten public squares and gardens including two major public city room spaces: a 14-metre high Midtown Square, and a nine-metre high Market Place together with sky parks and sky terraces. A new internal street called Seawater Street will be flanked by two large sheltered public plazas, creating a relaxed place for social interaction and a more bustling interpretation of a street market.

Midtown Hub: Exclusive Urban Social Club

The social heart of the development, Midtown Hub is a stunning, dedicated 80,000 sq ft space that brings together the best of entertainment, business and leisure. Described as a first-of-its-kind urban and social business club, it features networking lounges, MICE facilities, collaborative work spaces, as well as venues for private gatherings and social parties.

Residents of Midtown Bay enjoy exclusive membership to Midtown Hub, where they can socialise, network, and connect with a like-minded community of innovative thought leaders.

Midtown Bay Residences

Midtown Bay offers an exclusive collection of luxury city homes designed for flexibility. The residences at Midtown Bay will offer urbanites the flexibility to live, work and entertain at home. Each unit will have spacious living and dining areas that allow for ample configurations to entertain guests on an intimate scale.

Rare city duplex units also offer greater flexibility for residents to use the lower and upper floors in many different ways. The layout allows the living and dining areas is also nimble enough to be converted to conducive work spaces.

Design & Interiors

Award-winning designer Peter Tay created interiors evoking a sense of welcome and poise, with a material palette that is refined and stylish. He explains: For me, design is really about the spatial quality... When the interiors are this simple, that is when you really enjoy the space.

The design approach emphasizes minimalist, straightforward rectilinear spaces that become extremely flexible in their multiple adaptations for one's living, working, and entertaining options.

Residential Amenities

Midtown Bay features exclusive amenities including a Pool Garden at Level 7 and Sky Garden at Level 25, alongside access to Guoco Midtown's broader facilities and community spaces.

Developer: GuocoLand Group

Listed on the Singapore Exchange since 1978, GuocoLand Limited (GuocoLand) is an award-winning real estate developer distinguished by quality, innovative design and concepts. In Singapore, the group has successfully developed 36 residential projects yielding approximately 11,000 apartments and homes, and developed the iconic Guoco Tower, the tallest building in the city-state reaching a height of 290 metres.
