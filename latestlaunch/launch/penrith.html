About Penrith

Penrith is a 460-unit luxury condominium in Queenstown by GuocoLand, marking the first major launch on Margaret Drive in seven years. The project combines contemporary design with premium amenities in a city-fringe location offering strong MRT connectivity and proximity to schools and green spaces.

Why You'll Love Living Here

The property emphasizes a 1-minute walk to Queenstown MRT (East-West Line) for seamless city access. Nearby amenities include top-tier schools like Queenstown Primary School within walking distance, lush greenery with sustainable design elements, and the vibrant Dawson precinct featuring dining, retail, and parks.

Development Overview

The project represents a revival of the Margaret Drive district after a seven-year hiatus, blending timeless architecture, sophisticated interiors, and lifestyle-driven amenities. Units are described as a curated collection featuring modern wellness facilities and tranquil sky gardens designed for both elegance and functionality.
