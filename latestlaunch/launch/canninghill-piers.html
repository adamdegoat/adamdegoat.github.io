About Canninghill Piers

Canninghill Piers is an integrated waterfront development designed by renowned Danish architect Bjarke Ingels Group, combining 696 residential units with commercial, hotel, and serviced residence components. The project transforms the former Liang Court site into a landmark featuring two distinctive towers (48 and 24 storeys) with direct MRT access and panoramic views of the Singapore River and Fort Canning Hill.

Integrated Development Concept

CanningHill Piers is part of an integrated development that also includes a commercial component with F&B and retail outlets named CanningHill Square, a hotel operated under the Moxy brand by Marriott International and a serviced residence with a hotel licence operated under the Somerset brand.

The design comprises four towers with varying heights set atop a double-storey commercial podium. A continuous draped façade unifies the components while strategic openings reveal entrances, communal areas, and green spaces.

Architectural Design

The architecture was created by Bjarke Ingels Group, one of the world's most well-known contemporary architects responsible for projects including 8 House in Copenhagen and Via 57 West in New York. This marks BIG's first residential project in Asia.

Location & Connectivity

Situated in the heart of Clarke Quay – Singapore's most popular riverfront lifestyle and entertainment precinct – on the fringe of Singapore's Central Business District, the development enjoys direct linkage to Fort Canning MRT Station on the Downtown Line and proximity to Clarke Quay MRT on the North East Line.

The property provides convenient access to the CBD via the Central Expressway and is a mere eight-minute walk from Clarke Quay MRT station.

Residential Towers

At 180 metres, the 48-storey riverside-facing tower serves as the tallest residential development along the Singapore River, while the 24-storey tower overlooking Fort Canning Hill stands at 100 metres. The two towers connect via an iconic sky bridge on Level 24.

Sky Bridge & Amenities (Level 24)

The connecting sky bridge includes Sky Gym, Infinity Lap Pool, Sky Gourmet, Flexi Lounge and Function Room offering unobstructed views of Fort Canning Hill, Singapore River, the Central Business District (CBD), Marina Bay and beyond.

Ground Level Amenities (Level 3)

Residents enjoy an outdoor jogging track, a bouldering wall and an adventure-themed children's play area amidst landscaping.

Sky Club (Level 45)

The Sky Club, a luxurious clubhouse spanning over 1,900 square feet (sq ft), with breath-taking views of Marina Bay, together with the Sky Garden, are located on Level 45.

Riverfront Promenade & Landscape

Part of an existing road that currently separates the project site and the river will be transformed into an attractive riverfront promenade that connects seamlessly with the vibrant Clarke Quay lifestyle precinct. The landscaping extends the lush greenery of Fort Canning Hill to its various sky terraces.

Urban Planning Integration

Future plans include hiking from Fort Canning Park to Pearl's Hill City Park through the scenic Singapore River, as part of the Urban Redevelopment Authority's Master Plan 2019, with Clarke Quay's revitalized retail and F&B offerings serving the enlarged residential population.

Commercial Component

The commercial offerings will be seamlessly connected to Clarke Quay, offering residents the convenience of top-notch F&B and retail offerings right on their doorstep.

Developer Credentials

CDL is a leading global real estate operating company with a network spanning 103 locations in 29 countries, with over 55 years of experience and having developed over 43,000 homes.

CapitaLand is one of Asia's largest diversified real estate groups with a global portfolio worth S$131.7 billion and presence across more than 200 cities in over 30 countries.
