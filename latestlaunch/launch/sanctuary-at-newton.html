About Sanctuary @ Newton

Sanctuary @ Newton is a distinguished freehold apartment expected to TOP in 2025. With 38 bespoke units, it is strategically located near Newton and Novena MRT stations.

Location & Area Description

Novena is primarily residential with shopping destinations including United Square, Goldhill Plaza Mall, Square 2, and Velocity mall near Novena MRT. The area comprises five sub-zones (Mount Pleasant, Moulmein, Thomson, Malcolm, and Balestier) with approximately 50,000 residents across nine square kilometers.

URA Master Plan Highlights

The URA master plan emphasizes transport and healthcare improvements. In 2013, URA's master plan to develop the 17 hectare Health City Novena targeted to be completed by 2030, is a 17-hectare modern integrated community of healthcare, medical education complex physically linking TTSH, the medical school and all public and volunteer healthcare facilities ramping up the built-up area for all healthcare facilities and services from 250,000 sqm to 600,000 sqm with the completion of the complex.

Novena is an established medical precinct with Tan Tock Seng Hospital, Ren Ci Hospital, the National Skin Centre and the National Neuroscience Institute, Novena Medical Centre and the Mount Elizabeth Novena Hospital and Medical Centre nearby.

North-South Corridor (NSC)

The North-South Corridor (NSC) is a multi-modal transportation corridor that will enhance connectivity from the northern region to the city, serving towns such as Sembawang, Yishun, Ang Mo Kio, Toa Payoh, Novena and Rochor.

Opening is targeted for 2027 for the viaduct portion and 2029 for the rest. Bus commuters can expect to save up to 15 minutes of travel time when the corridor is fully operational.

Development Location & Amenities

Nestled in the heart of District 11, Sanctuary @ Newton is a distinguished freehold development set to complete in 2025. With 38 bespoke units, it offers an intimate community vibe. Strategically located near Newton and Novena MRT stations, it ensures effortless travel throughout Singapore.

Nearby amenities include Newton Food Centre, United Square, Novena Square, Square 2, Velocity malls, and Orchard Shopping Belt just a short 5-minute drive away.

Educational institutions within proximity: St Joseph's Institution (Junior), Anglo-Chinese School (Junior), Anglo-Chinese School (Primary) & Singapore Chinese Girls' School (Primary).

Various nature spots including Novena Park, Singapore Botanic Gardens, and Gardens By the Bay offer opportunities for quality family time and recreational activities. Additionally, the development is conveniently situated near medical institutions such as Novena Medical Centre, Mount Elizabeth Novena, and Tan Tock Seng Hospital.
