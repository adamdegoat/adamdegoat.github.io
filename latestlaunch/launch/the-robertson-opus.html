About The Robertson Opus

Prime Address in District 9

Located at Robertson Quay, a prestigious, tranquil enclave near Orchard Road and CBD, The Robertson OPUS offers a 999-year leasehold tenure — an increasingly rare asset in land-scarce Singapore.

Riverside Serenity, City Connectivity

Imagine waking up to the calm of the Singapore River, yet being minutes away from vibrant dining, art galleries, and nightlife. Great World MRT (TEL) and Fort Canning MRT are nearby, making commuting seamless.

Boutique Luxury with Space and Style

Unlike dense mega-developments, The Robertson OPUS offers low-density, exclusive living with thoughtfully designed, spacious units — ideal for both discerning homeowners and savvy investors.

Investment-Worthy Location

Robertson Quay is part of a mature, high-demand neighbourhood, appealing to both locals and expatriates. Its strong rental yield potential and capital appreciation make it an intelligent portfolio addition.

Lifestyle and Leisure at Your Doorstep

From wine bars and riverfront dining to art spaces and wellness studios — this is curated urban living. Perfect for the modern, cultured buyer who values both peace and personality in their environment.
