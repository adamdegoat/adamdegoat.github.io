About Pullman Residences Newton

Pullman Residences is a new freehold condo in District 11 along Newton Road. The development features contemporary design inspired by the global Pullman hotel brand, combining business and leisure in a residential setting. Located in an established residential area surrounded by Good Class Bungalows, the project sits opposite Newton MRT station.

Location & Connectivity

The property sits 1km away from prestigious schools such as ACS Primary, ACS Junior and St Joseph's Institution. The vicinity provides access to top-notch medical facilities, world-class shopping and Singapore Botanic Gardens, a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Residents can exit into Bukit Timah Road or connect to the CTE or PIE if you drive, or take a train from Newton MRT station, just a two-minute walk away. Nearby dining includes Newton Circus across the road for hawker fare and Michelin-starred restaurants, just minutes away.

Architecture & Design

Standing 30 storeys high, the building features louvered, vertical panels with a facade that commands pure strength. The design optimizes surrounding views and encourages effective cross-ventilation. Units begin at level two (17m above ground level), giving you great views of the city or the landed enclave towards Bukit Timah Hill.

The architecture creates an impression of an oasis within the forest where one resides with nature through integration of tropical greens and arches that manifest themselves in different forms.

Amenities

Recreational facilities include a 50m Lap Pool, Wellness Pool, Kid's Pool, Jacuzzi, Dining Pavilion with BBQ, Wellness Gym, Aqua Gym, Recreational Tennis Court with Half Basketball Court, Club Lounge, Party Lawn and Garden Courtyard.

Unit Features

Units feature spacious balcony in every unit, wet and dry kitchen, and smart technology for 24/7 monitoring, a biometric digital door lock with break-in alert, and aircon control. Exclusive three and four-bedroom units are served by a private lift for added privacy. Penthouse units enjoy high ceiling height for more storage space.

Accor Ownership Benefits

Residents receive a membership in the Accor Ownership Benefits Program, extending you VVIP status globally including Platinum status in Accor Live Limitless loyalty program, 20% off best available rate at more than 4,000 Accor hotels and resorts worldwide, and local benefits at select Accor hotels in Singapore.

Developer Background

EL Development has developed more than 3,000 homes in Singapore. The company won awards including FIABCI Singapore Property Awards (2018 & 2019) and BCA Green Mark Award (2016 & 2018). The developer is creating the first Pullman Hotel in Singapore on Hill Street with 350 rooms.
