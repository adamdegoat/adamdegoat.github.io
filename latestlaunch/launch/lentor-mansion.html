About Lentor Mansion

Lentor Mansion is a 533-unit private residential condo in District 26 developed by GuocoLand. Inspired by black-and-white bungalows, Lentor Mansion promises tranquil mansion-style living in lush greenery, situated on the largest low-density site in the Lentor district, just a five-minute walk from Lentor MRT Station.

From The Developer Leading The Lentor Transformation

GuocoLand demonstrates commitment to developing Lentor through multiple projects including Lentor Modern and Lentor Hills Residences, with acquisition of a fourth site showing the next phase in GuocoLand's long-term vision and unwavering commitment to transforming the Lentor precinct.

Lentor Mansion's Location

Lentor MRT station on the Thomson-East-Coast-Line is within walking distance. The project is near Lentor Modern mall featuring a 12,000 sq ft supermarket and various lifestyle amenities.

Facilities & Lifestyle Features

The development includes a double-storey clubhouse that evokes a luxurious aesthetic, a 50-metre lap pool, 223-metre forest trail, grand lawn, tots club, forest pavilion, and multiple landscaped gardens spread across zones for diverse activities and family bonding.

Design & Architecture

Interiors feature clean lines, neutral color schemes and quality materials. The iconic black-and-white clubhouse provides a grand drop-off point, creating an impressive arrival experience, with an airy second-floor verandah and flexible, efficient unit layouts.

Guocoland Limited Company Profile

GuocoLand Limited is a real estate development company based in Singapore established in 1976, engaged in property development, investment, hotel operations, and management across Asia with emphasis on sustainability and innovation.
