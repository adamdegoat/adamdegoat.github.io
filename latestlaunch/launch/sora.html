About Sora

Sora (formerly Park View Mansions) is a new residential development overlooking Jurong Lake Gardens with 300 meters of waterfront access, capitalising on the transformation of the Jurong Lake District into Singapore's second-largest business hub and connecting to upcoming metro lines and 90 hectares of green and recreational spaces.

Main Concept

Infinite Possibilities in a Boundless Oasis.

The development emphasizes flexible spaces blending urban convenience with natural surroundings, reimagining possibilities under expansive skies, with the tagline "Live creatively, work comfortably, play passionately."

Location & Environment

The property features 300m frontage overlooking the tranquil Jurong Lake Gardens and access to 90 ha of green spaces and recreational zones. Jurong Lake Gardens — one of Singapore's three national gardens — includes an ActiveSG Gym, 10-kilometer river promenade, and upcoming facilities like a Science Centre (2027).

District Development

As the second-largest business district outside the city center, Jurong Lake District encompasses a 6.5-hectare white site expected to yield 146,000 square meters of office space, 1,700 residential units, and 73,000 square meters of retail and hospitality space. URA's Master Plan projects over 160 hectares of parks and waterbodies, 10,000 new jobs, and 20,000 new homes by 2050.

Transportation & Connectivity

The Jurong Region Line and Cross Island Line (opening 2029 and 2032 respectively) will connect Jurong Lake District to major hubs including Woodlands Regional Centre, Paya Lebar Central, and Punggol Digital District, complementing existing East-West and North-South Lines.
