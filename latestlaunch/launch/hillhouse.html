About Hillhouse

Welcome Home to Hill House

Perched exclusively on 11 Institution Hill, just above River Valley Road, Hill House offers a harmonious blend of serene living and urban convenience, making it an ideal choice for discerning homeowners and investors. Step out of your home and you are connected to downtown Singapore – from the CBD and Marina Bay to Clarke Quay and Orchard Road. Work, play, and other exciting possibilities are just a stroll or short drive away.

Hill House promises a respite away from the hustle and bustle of the city, yet is extremely well-connected to the best of District 9. From shopping and food to arts and nature, world-class offerings are within minutes' reach. And if you wish to go a little further, Fort Canning MRT is just 9 minutes by foot. There, the Downtown Line whisks you to Marina Bay in a heartbeat.

Prime Location with Exceptional Connectivity

Central Position: Nestled in the tranquil enclave of Institution Hill, Hill House provides residents with a peaceful retreat while being just minutes away from the vibrant Orchard Road shopping district.

Transport Accessibility: The development is approximately a 6 to 7-minute walk (about 580 meters) from Somerset MRT Station, part of the North-South Line, ensuring seamless connectivity to various parts of Singapore. Additionally, Fort Canning MRT Station on the Downtown Line and Great World MRT Station on the Thomson-East Coast Line are within walking distance, enhancing accessibility for residents.

Proximity to Elite Educational Institutions

Renowned Schools: Families will appreciate the close proximity to esteemed schools such as River Valley Primary School, which is within a 1 km radius.

Comprehensive Amenities and Lifestyle Offerings

Shopping and Dining: Residents can indulge in a plethora of retail and culinary experiences with nearby shopping centers including Orchard Gateway, Orchard Central, 313@Somerset, and The Centrepoint. The proximity to the Orchard Road shopping belt ensures that a wide array of dining, entertainment, and shopping options are just a stone's throw away.

Recreational Facilities: Hill House is thoughtfully designed with nature-inspired amenities spread across its sky and rooftop terraces. Residents can enjoy features such as a lap pool, hammock garden, communal study terrace, and lush themed gardens, providing a serene environment for relaxation and social gatherings.

Thoughtful Design and Quality Living Spaces

Exclusive Residences: Comprising a single 10-storey block with 72 residential units, Hill House offers a range of unit types from cozy one-bedroom to spacious three-bedroom apartments. Each residence is meticulously designed with premium finishes, state-of-the-art appliances, and layouts that maximize natural light and ventilation, ensuring a luxurious living experience.

Investment Potential

999-Year Leasehold: The rare 999-year leasehold tenure, commencing from 1 July 1841, offers a near freehold status, enhancing the property's value retention and appeal to investors seeking long-term capital appreciation.

Nature-Inspired Design Elements

Studies have shown that nature helps to improve concentration. Enjoy a change of scenery even as you work from home. Head to the Co-working Terrace, get some fresh air, put on your thinking cap, and you might just land on your next big idea at Hill House.

The abundance and variety of nature at Hill House creates a joyful, verdant oasis. Inspired by the many beautiful gardens around the world, the landscape is made up of 7 unique gardens, starting from the arrival lobby and ending at the rooftop. From the refreshing modern rainforest to the sweetly perfumed floral garden, each of the 7 gardens presents a different and immersive enjoyment of nature.

Lifestyle Amenities

Life is perfect when you are lounging by a 20-metre sky pool, with your favourite book, overlooking the city skyline. Surrounded by lush tropical foliage, this is your personal paradise, where time slows down and cares melt away.

The studio gym makes working out at home a breeze. With floor-to-ceiling windows that look out to the surroundings, this is where you recharge and get fit. Opt for a HIIT workout or yoga practice to bring wholeness back into your life.

Host a party in the function room or hang out with friends at the atmospheric rooftop oasis recreates a resort lifestyle right in the heart of the city. Perfectly Instagrammable, the teepees also offer you a tranquil place to contemplate, or enjoy the company of your loved ones in a cosy and intimate setting.
