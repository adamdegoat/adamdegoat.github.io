Project Description

33 Residences is an exciting freehold launch which is rare in today's market. 33 Residences is a boutique apartment development which gets to enjoy two unit types; 2 bedrooms and 3 bedrooms.

The facilities will be on the ground floor and the top level which comes with a water fountain, swimming pool, a gymnasium and a jacuzzi.

Location & Connectivity

The development is within 7 mins walk to Paya Lebar Business Hub and positioned in one of Singapore's most vibrant districts resting on the city fringe.

Geylang benefits from proximity to Geylang River connecting to East Coast Park and Gardens by the Bay, plus shopping at Parkway Parade, I12 Katong, Suntec, and SingPost.
