About Promenade Peak

Promenade Peak is a 63-storey ultra-prime residential tower in District 3 near Great World MRT and River Valley. The development emphasizes panoramic views of Singapore River and the Orchard Road skyline, with premium amenities and strong connectivity to Singapore's central business and leisure districts.

Location & Connectivity

Located in District 03, the development is minutes' walk to Great World MRT and Great World City Mall, with seamless connectivity to Orchard Road, the CBD, and Robertson Quay.

Design & Views

The tower offers sweeping, panoramic views that stretch from the iconic Singapore River to the vibrant Orchard Road skyline. The development blends heritage and modernity with ever-changing cityscapes visible from units.

Amenities & Features

Features include a Grand Promenade at Level 1, a Social Peak at Level 22, a Wellness Peak at Level 43 featuring professional Symbio brand gym equipment, and a Sky Peak infinity pool (described as the tallest in Singapore) with an adjacent function room. Suites collection units enjoy private lift service.

Lifestyle & Dining

Top-tier dining, cafes, and retail options are available at Great World City and the River Valley enclave.

Schools & Education

The development is surrounded by elite institutions including River Valley Primary and international schools.

Investment Potential

Limited land supply in the River Valley/Zion corridor positions the development as rare. It is suited for long-term capital appreciation and rental demand, with a strong expat tenant pool due to CBD, Orchard, and international school proximity, and consistent rental yield potential in the sought-after enclave.
