About Orchard Sophia

Located at the tip of Sophia Road, Orchard Sophia is a rare freehold exclusive condo and award-winning modern urban sanctuary designed by renowned firm Ong & Ong Architects. A winner of three awards at PropertyGuru Asia Property Awards Singapore, it reflects the Bauhaus architectural style uniting function and beauty.

Overview

Located at the tip of Sophia Road, this is the pinnacle of luxury living. A modern urban freehold sanctuary and winner of three awards at PropertyGuru Asia Property Awards Singapore, Orchard Sophia is an oasis of serenity within the city.

Orchard Sophia is an award-winning statement of redefined luxury. Sporting pockets of space that evoke an urban gallery aesthetic, Orchard Sophia is a stunning reflection of the Bauhaus architectural style uniting function and beauty.

Designed by renowned firm Ong & Ong Architects, a unique metal oak coloured mesh is layered over the grey facade of the building, creating an iconic image - a striking structure on Sophia Road.

Connectivity

Unrivalled connectivity lives at Orchard Sophia - with three MRT lines mere moments away, you are never too far from the things you love. Nestled within Singapore's premier shopping district, your desires are always met here.

Living in Orchard area in Singapore offers a vibrant and upscale lifestyle that blends luxury, convenience, and urban charm. Orchard Road is one of Singapore's most iconic and famous shopping districts, known for its upscale malls, high-end boutiques, and a wide array of entertainment and dining options.

Lifestyle & Living Experience

As you open the grand entrance of your home, you are immediately greeted by a breathtaking oasis of exquisite luxury living. Every detail has been carefully curated to create a serene and indulgent atmosphere, offering you the perfect retreat from the outside world.

Stepping outside, you find yourself in a mesmerizing outdoor sanctuary. An expansive pool glistens under the moonlight, surrounded by lush green at Orchard Sophia. The social lounge, complete with cozy daybeds offers the perfect spot to unwind and enjoy a refreshing beverage.

Education & Family Living

For families with children, Orchard is close to excellent educational institutions, including international schools and renowned local schools, ensuring quality education options for all ages.

Orchard Sophia sits within proximity to Singapore's financial hub. Oscillate with ease between the bustle of city life and the quietude of your prime residential enclave. Choices abound - with Marina Bay Sands and Gardens by the Bay within easy reach, you'll have plenty of options for leisure, shopping, dining and entertainment.

Culture & Entertainment

Orchard Sophia lies at the nexus of Singapore's colourful cultural district - with the historical zones of Bugis and Kampong Glam, and Little India accessible in minutes. For the erudite learner, visit Singapore's finest museums with stories to tell - each with a collection of tales, history and wonder within its walls. Partake in an exchange of cultures in illustrious heritage areas and experience authentic Singaporean traditions.

Orchard Sophia is near to several entertainment venues, theaters, and cinemas. You can catch the latest movies, attend concerts, and enjoy cultural performances in the area. The nearby Esplanade - Theatres on the Bay hosts world-class performances and events.

Dining & Food

Orchard Sophia is situated in a food lover's haven. From upscale restaurants offering international cuisines to chic cafes and trendy eateries, there's a vast range of dining options to suit every palate. You can savor fine dining experiences or explore local hawker centers to enjoy authentic Singaporean dishes.

Green Spaces

Despite being in the heart of the city, Orchard Sophia offers green retreats where you can relax and unwind. Fort Canning Park, located nearby, provides a tranquil escape from the urban bustle, with lush greenery, historical sites, and picturesque walking trails.

Arrival Hall & Entry Experience

Designed after a splendid hotel lobby, the arrival hall at the basement instantly centres you within a world of class and luxury - welcoming you to a private sanctuary within the city. Visitors are bestowed with a sense of serenity and refinement. The air carries the scent of fragrant blooms that adorn the manicured gardens, further enhancing the atmosphere of sophistication.

Facilities & Amenities

Work at your own pace at the Reading Lounge, away from distractions and the buzz of the world around you. Wine and dine at the Rooftop Dining or the Rooftop Bar as you partake in the wondrous lights of the Singapore skyline.

Elegance and function come together at the Social Lounge - host gatherings or celebrate with friends and family within cosy settings. Stay fit and focused at the Outdoor Fitness in comfortable surroundings - productivity and pleasures have never felt more at home.

At the heart of Orchard Sophia, a central courtyard beckons, sporting a sleek, luxurious pool calling you in for a rejuvenating soak. Recline and relax on the pool deck by the poolside or enjoy calming views of the waters from balconies in choice units.

Rejuvenate the senses and enter a world of relaxation in the private Spa Pool amidst the mesmerising view of the city landscape. Play host at the Alfresco BBQ deck or just take a step back and relax at the Social Lawn. Recline in spaces designed for pure comfort and pleasure.

Living Spaces & Units

Enter a modern refuge created to fit your lifestyle needs. A rare spaciousness exists here, with choice units planned to allow spaces for multi-generational and co-living. Selected units feature a stylish Juliet balcony, extending your living spaces and visual access to your neighborhood and beyond.

Quality Finishes & Fittings

Quality fittings for quality homes at Orchard Sophia. Bedroom spaces are crafted to rejuvenate your senses, providing you with a sense of calm. Bathrooms are adorned with statement sanitary wares and bathroom accessories from renowned brands like Duravit, Hansgrohe and Gessi.

Live in style at Orchard Sophia. Revel in luxury with a spacious kitchen well-equipped with fittings and finishes from renowned brands - with hobs, hoods and refrigerators from SMEG, added kitchen utilities from Franke, and washers from Bosch.

Keeping in pace with the latest innovations, each unit at Orchard Sophia comes equipped with a digital lock, with two electric vehicle charging units within the development.
