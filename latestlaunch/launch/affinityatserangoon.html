About Affinity @ Serangoon

This mixed-use development replaces the former HUDC Serangoon Ville estate, offering a diverse residential community with both apartment towers and exclusive strata landed homes. The project emphasizes extensive amenities centered around multiple water features and themed gardens spanning the grounds.

Location

The development sits near shopping centers (NEX, myVillage), food establishments (Chomp Chomp, Serangoon Gardens Market), and serves multiple primary schools. "Within 1 km to Rosyth Primary School and Zhonghua Primary School" plus proximity to international institutions. Transportation access includes Kovan, Serangoon, and Lorong Chuan MRT stations, with "a short 20 minutes' drive to the Central Business District."

Units

Units feature "Quality fittings & appliances Bosch appliances, Grohe fittings, Mitsubishi Aircon & Yale digital lockset." Premium bedroom options accommodate queen-size beds, with fully fitted kitchens including quartz countertops. Higher-tier units include utility yards. Strata landed homes feature "high ceiling & natural skylight above dining" with direct pool access.

Facilities

Amenities span three themed islands including "Clubhouse with Floating Gym and Sky BBQ" and extensive water features: "50m lap pool with Aqua Gym facilities, 67m Sanctuary Pool, Family and Wading pools." Recreation options include "Tennis Court, Outdoor Gym, Jogging Trail, Fitness Corner and Reflexology Path" plus "more than ten thematic gardens."
