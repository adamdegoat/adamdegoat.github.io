About Amber Park

Amber Park represents a reimagined seafront living experience on the East Coast, reconnecting residents with coastal pleasures while maintaining convenient city access. This freehold development by CDL features the signature Stratosphere—a rooftop recreation deck connecting three towers 235 feet above sea level, creating a distinctive architectural identity for the neighborhood.

The Stratosphere Seafront Living

"The Stratosphere at Level 22 – a rooftop recreational deck, 235ft above sea level" connects all three residential towers. Designed by SCDA Architects' Chan Soo Khian, this signature feature includes a 600-meter jogging track, yoga deck, lounge, sea-facing gymnasium, and gourmet kitchen facilities, positioning exercise and recreation as sensory experiences overlooking the coastline.

Comprehensive Facilities

Facilities span two recreation zones: the Lagoon Bay at Level 1 and The Stratosphere. "Make a splash at the Lagoon Pool, or take a soak at the Aqua Beds or the Hydrotherapy Pool." Additional amenities include leisure pools, kids' lagoon pool, fitness court, coastal walk, BBQ pavilions, spa pool, and lush gardens with quiet pavilions.

Connectivity & Regional Development

The East benefits from Changi Airport proximity and the Cross Island Line opening five new MRT stations by 2029, including an interchange at Pasir Ris. The Thomson-East Coast Line completion in 2024 enhances accessibility. "Just 3-minutes' walk to the upcoming Tanjong Katong MRT Station" provides direct public transit connectivity.

Living Advantages

East Coast living offers "good balance between urban conveniences and a laid-back lifestyle." The area provides expressway access via ECP, PIE, and MCE; proximity to Changi Airport and CBD; nearby shopping at Parkway Parade and Katong malls; established schools; and the foodie culture for which East Coast is renowned.

Unit Features & Finishes

Units feature "fully-equipped kitchen fitted with compressed quartz countertop and V-Zug appliances," "high quality sanitary wares and fittings from Villeroy & Boch and Grohe," marble flooring in living/dining areas, engineered timber in bedrooms, and built-in storage with LED lighting. Premium units include private lift access and walk-in wardrobes.

Smart Home Integration

Premier residential services include remote air conditioning control, smart lighting scheduling, door monitoring with notifications, smoke detection alerts, voice control via Google Assistant, facility booking and keyless access, parcel station notifications, video intercom systems, and QR-based visitor invitations for security.

Investment Rationale

"Freehold, rare land size of over 230,000sqft in prestigious District 15" offers rental potential within a private residential enclave. Nearby Meyer Park's enbloc transaction provides valuation reference. Short walk to East Coast Park's 15km coastline and island-wide expressway connectivity support long-term appreciation.
