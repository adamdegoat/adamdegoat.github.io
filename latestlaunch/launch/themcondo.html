About The M

The M is a brand new mixed development condo on Middle Road that is diagonally opposite South Beach and just at the doorstep of downtown Singapore. The development offers 522 residences across three 20-storey towers and a 6-storey tower, with retail stores and F&B selections at ground-level.

Location & Connectivity

The project features 3 mins walk to Bugis MRT, mins walk to Esplanade MRT & City Hall MRT, with access to 4 lines: North-south, East-West, Circle and Downtown line.

Minutes' drive to the ECP, KPE, PIE and MCE, with the district planned for underground walkways and pathways for ultra-connectivity.

Facilities & Amenities

A 50m Starlight Pool and The Pendulum relaxation area complement The Verandah where you can soak up the bustle of the city.

Residents can enjoy a Jacuzzi Pool, a barbeque with friends at The Grill, or simply lay back and admire the night sky on the Star Deck.

Five configurable function rooms, with The Boardroom to host work meetings, plus Bakerzone, Fitness X, and Golf Snooker facilities.

Smart Home/Work Concept

The development introduces flexible, adaptable Studios and 1-bedroom residences that transform seamlessly from living areas to productive workspaces.

Residents experience pioneering technology including a touchless wave scanner to access your lift lobby – enjoy seamless integration and connectivity.

Innovative features that maximise space such as a dual-use kitchen counter-top, sliding wardrobes and well thought out ceiling storage spaces.

Building & Design

Aluminium frames against crystal and euro grey tinted glass on the façade project modern living for the mobile and successful executive.

Homes that are built to embody your style and standards while remaining practical and useful, with fittings and appliances from some of the most recognised brands.

Surrounding Amenities

Shopping & Dining: Bugis Junction, Bugis+, Suntec City Mall, Raffles Shopping Centre, with Albert Centre Market and Food Centre, Tekka Market and Food Centre, North Bridge Road Market and Food Centre.

Education: Stamford Primary School, Saint Magaret's Primary School, Singapore Management University (SMU), Nanyang Academy of Fine Arts.

Recreation: Singapore Recreation Club, Singapore Cricket Club, Fort Canning Park, Esplanade Park.

Developer Background

Wing Tai Holdings was founded in 1955 in Hong Kong and incorporated in Singapore on 9 August 1963, and is described as Singapore's leading property developer and lifestyle company with assets exceeding S$4.5 billion.
