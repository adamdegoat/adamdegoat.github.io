About The Essence

The Essence sits along Chong Kuo Road in the Springleaf neighbourhood, approximately seven to eight minutes slow walk to the future Springleaf MRT Station on the Thomson-East Coast Line. The development occupies a low-rise private enclave of 2 blocks of 5-storey buildings, surrounded by nature reserves and water bodies.

Location & Connectivity

The development is surrounded by Springleaf Nature Park, Upper and Lower Seletar Reservoir Parks, and Sembawang Country Club. Shopping options include Thomson Plaza, Northpoint City, and Sembawang Shopping Centre. Major expressways (SLE, CTE, TPE) are minutes away.

Recreational Facilities

The development features a 40m lap pool, kids pool, pool pavilion, contemplation court, active court, gym, and lawn.

Finishes

Units include top-notch fittings with renowned brands such as Hansgrohe, Duravit and Bosch.
