About Meyer Mansion

Meyer Mansion is a freehold residential tower on Meyer Road designed as a modern reinterpretation of the area's historic grand mansions. The single 25-storey development combines contemporary architecture with tropical design elements and emphasizes expansive sea and city views through floor-to-ceiling windows.

Location & Connectivity

Meyer Mansion sits on the East Coast near a stretch of historic bungalows, positioned between the seafront and the city. The upcoming Katong Park MRT Station (Thomson-East Coast Line) is approximately 5 minutes' walk away, providing direct connectivity to Raffles Place within 6 stops. The East Coast Parkway offers quick access to the Central Business District and Marina Bay in under 10 minutes. The Singapore Sports Hub is 4 minutes away by car.

The property combines coastal leisure with urban convenience, situated where the East Coast's laid-back character meets proximity to downtown amenities and expressways.

Design & Amenities

Units feature dumbbell layouts minimizing corridor space, separated WC and shower areas, en-suite bathrooms for all bedrooms, and master baths with freestanding tubs. The development allocates up to 80% of the site to landscaping and amenities, including a Grand Lawn, swimming pools, a minimalist Clubhouse, and a Beach House pavilion. Deep balconies inspired by tropical verandahs frame panoramic views of the East Coast and surrounding areas. Premium units include private lifts and dual balconies.

Building Specifications

Storeys: 25. Lifts: 2 common lifts, 4 private lift stacks. Carpark: 200 spaces plus 3 accessible lots. Bicycle Parking: 34 spaces. Floor-to-Ceiling Heights: 4.2m (ground floor), 3.3m (mid-floors), 6.6m living/dining (penthouse).
