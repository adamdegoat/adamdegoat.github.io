About Upperhouse

Upperhouse at Orchard Boulevard is a luxury condominium development in Singapore's prestigious Orchard district, featuring approximately 301 premium residences with smart home technology and world-class amenities. Located steps from Orchard MRT station, the development emphasizes elegant urban living with resort-style facilities and seamless connectivity to shopping, dining, and top schools.

The Peak of Orchard Living

A rare collection of luxury homes with smart living, seamless connectivity, and world-class amenities in Singapore's most coveted address.

Orchard Living Considerations

While Orchard offers unparalleled access to luxury boutiques, fine dining, and vibrant energy, prospective buyers should consider whether they prefer serenity, green spaces, and a more relaxed atmosphere versus a dynamic, walkable neighborhood.

Premium Features

The development offers spacious, designer interiors with premium finishes and smart home technology. Amenities include infinity pools, sky lounges, private dining pavilions, fully equipped gyms, private lounges, wellness centers, and business lounges with concierge services.

Connectivity & Proximity

Located minutes to Orchard MRT and major expressways, residents enjoy walkable access to Tanglin Mall, Camden Medical, Botanic Gardens, and the Orchard shopping belt. Nearby schools include ACS Junior, SCGS, and RGS.

Smart & Sustainable Living

Features include app-controlled smart home systems for lighting, climate, and security, along with energy-efficient designs and luxury concierge services.

Investment Appeal

Orchard Boulevard is a prime real estate destination with strong capital appreciation and high rental demand. The development benefits from ongoing infrastructure upgrades and retail developments supporting long-term property value growth.

Design & Architecture

Created by UOL's expertise, the development is described as embodying a timeless legacy, meticulously crafted, and representing collectible and sophisticated luxury living.
