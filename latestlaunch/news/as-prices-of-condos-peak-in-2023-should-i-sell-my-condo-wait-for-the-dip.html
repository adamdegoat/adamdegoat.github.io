As Prices Of Condos Peak In 2023, Should I Sell My Condo & Wait For The Dip?

8/12/2023

Despite rising mortgage rates and potential economic slowdown, Singapore's condo market continues setting new price records in 2023.

When evaluating a sale, assess your comfort level affording the waiting period and factor in transaction costs including agent fees, closing expenses, and applicable taxes.

Real estate markets are unpredictable. While prices may decline, they could unexpectedly rebound, requiring preparation for multiple scenarios.

Evaluate Singapore's current market conditions and historical pricing patterns. Consistent price increases with no slowdown signals might make selling and waiting unwise; however, downturn indicators could justify this strategy.

Accurately predicting price movements is risky, as property values depend on economics, interest rates, government policy, and numerous other variables.

Assess whether selling aligns with immediate financial requirements or essential relocations, as these needs may outweigh potential price savings from waiting.

Consider how this decision supports your broader financial strategy and long-term objectives.

Evaluate holding costs including mortgage payments, property taxes, and maintenance expenses while waiting for price declines.

Selling now allows investing proceeds in alternative assets that may generate returns while the real estate market stabilizes.

Personal factors like relocation or upgrading may justify selling regardless of potential price fluctuations.

Evaluate your comfort managing market timing risks, as perfectly predicting dips presents emotional and financial challenges.

Kiwi Lim advises: "Attempting to time the market perfectly can be challenging and risky. It's often better to make decisions based on your own financial goals, needs, and a long-term perspective rather than solely trying to predict market movements."

The optimal decision depends on financial goals, risk tolerance, market insights, and individual circumstances, with no universal answer.
