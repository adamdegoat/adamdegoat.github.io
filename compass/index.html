Singapore Home-Buying Roadmap

Your whole property purchase, on one page

Answer a few quick questions and Compass maps your entire journey — what you're eligible for, what you can afford, every dollar of cash you'll need, the timeline and a step-by-step checklist. Built on the real IRAS, HDB & MAS rules.

Compass · by Adam Phang.

A personalised guide, not financial or legal advice. Figures use Singapore rules current as at June 2026: BSD & ABSD, TDSR 55% · MSR 30% (HDB/EC) · stress rate 4% · first-loan LTV 75% (5% min cash), HDB 5-year MOP, EC household income ceiling $16,000. Stamp duty is charged on the higher of price or valuation. Confirm your eligibility, loan and exact figures with HDB, the bank, a conveyancing lawyer and IRAS for your own situation.
