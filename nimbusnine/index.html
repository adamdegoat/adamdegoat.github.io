Why we built Nimbus Nine.

Most small businesses in Singapore lose customers for one small reason. Someone messages after closing, or in the middle of a rush, and no one answers in time. By the next morning, that person has already booked somewhere else.

We started Nimbus Nine to close that gap. Not with a call centre or another app for you to babysit, but with a front desk that answers in your name, around the clock, and holds onto every enquiry until you can take it from there.

Every one is built by hand, here in Singapore, around the way your business actually runs. No lock-in, no per-message fees, and a real person you can reach when you need one.

Questions, answered.

Is there a contract?

No lock-in, cancel anytime. One flat price of S$399 a month, with no per-message fees and no setup surprises.

Is it worth S$399 a month?

One recovered booking usually covers the month. It works around the clock, so the enquiries you would otherwise miss after hours become customers.

What if it says something wrong?

It only answers from what you give it, your real prices, services and hours. If it does not know something, it never guesses, it takes the customer's details and passes them to you.

Will it sound like my business?

Yes. Your business name, your tone, your prices and hours, and a voice you choose. It feels like your place, not a generic bot.

Do I need a website?

No. It works on its own link and QR from day one. Add a website whenever you are ready, and we build the front desk right into it.

Where do my enquiries go?

Straight to your WhatsApp the moment someone leaves their details, and they are saved for you too. Nothing is sold or shared.
