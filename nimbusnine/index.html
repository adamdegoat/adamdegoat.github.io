Why we built Nimbus Nine.

Most small businesses in Singapore lose customers for one small reason. Someone messages after closing, or in the middle of a rush, and no one answers in time. By the next morning, that person has already booked somewhere else.

We started Nimbus Nine to close that gap. Not with a call centre or another app for you to babysit, but with a front desk that answers in your name, around the clock, and holds onto every lead until you can take it from there.

Every one is built by hand, here in Singapore, around the way your business actually runs. No lock-in, no per-message fees, and a real person you can reach when you need one.
