Nimbus Nine is a small studio in Singapore, building the always-on front desk that small businesses could never afford before.

Why we started Nimbus Nine.

Every day, small businesses lose customers to a missed message. Someone asks a question after closing, or in the middle of a rush, and by the time anyone replies, they have already booked somewhere else. Big companies solved this years ago with call centres and expensive software. Everyone else just learned to live with it.

We didn't think that was fair. So we set out to build a front desk that any small business can afford. One that answers in seconds, in your name, around the clock, and never lets a lead slip away.

No call centre. No app for you to babysit. Just a front desk that works while you get on with the actual job.
