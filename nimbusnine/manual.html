Nimbus NineOperating guide

How to run this, simply.

You do four things: get clients, set them up, keep them happy, collect money. The software answers their customers and sends you leads, 24/7. This is everything you need.

02Add a client (about 10 minutes)

  1. Open the owner console and log in.
  2. Type their business name, pick their trade, and hit Create front desk.
  3. You get two links: a customer link + QR (for the client to share) and an admin link (to fill in their details).
  4. Open the admin link, fill in their real services, prices, hours, and the About your business box. Hit Save. (Or just send that admin link to the client and let them fill it in themselves.)
  5. Give them their customer link + QR. They put it in their Instagram bio, WhatsApp, shopfront, name cards. Live.
Trade not in the list? Pick "General / other" and fill it in, or pick the closest trade and edit. Any business works.

03How the front desk knows their business

It answers only from what's filled in the admin: their services, prices, hours, FAQ, and the About your business box. The more they write in, the smarter it sounds. It never makes things up, if it doesn't know something, it takes the customer's details as a lead instead.

04How leads reach you

When a customer leaves their name or number (typed in the chat, or via the "Leave your details" button), it becomes a lead. Every lead shows up in three places:

  • Your owner console (Recent leads)
  • The client's admin (Your leads)
  • Telegram, instantly
For instant Telegram alerts, the client messages @Nimbusninebot once, then pastes the ID it shows into their admin under "Where your leads go". Until then, leads still appear in the dashboards.

05Changing things later

The client (or you) log into the admin anytime, change a price, add a service, update hours, hit Save, and it's live on the next customer straight away. Nothing to rebuild.

06Money

  • Front desk: S$399 a month per client. Websites from S$1,299. Custom add-ons: quoted.
  • You collect payment yourself for now (PayNow, bank transfer or an invoice).
  • Your running costs are tiny, a few dollars a month in total, so the margin is yours.

07If something looks wrong

  • "This front desk isn't available" → the client is paused, or the link is wrong.
  • The bot gives a thin answer → add more detail in the admin (the About box and FAQ do the most).
  • No Telegram alerts → check the client messaged @Nimbusninebot and their ID is saved in the admin. Leads still show in the dashboards regardless.
  • Locked out of the console → it's your password; it can be reset.

08The one rule

Everything the front desk says comes from what's in the admin. Keep that filled in well, and each front desk runs itself.
