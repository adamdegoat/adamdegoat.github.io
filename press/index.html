How it works

1

New every Monday

Three fresh posts land here automatically at 4am — built from the week's real URA & HDB data.

2

Download the image

Tap Download on any post to save the image straight to your phone or computer.

3

Copy the caption

Tap Copy caption — the hook, the value, hashtags and your link line are ready to paste.

4

Post to Instagram

Paste, post, done. Space the three across the week (Mon / Wed / Fri works well).

Add the Studio to your phone

Install it like an app for one-tap access. iPhone: open in Safari → Share → Add to Home Screen. Android: Chrome menu → Install app.
